Category: Deposits

Q: What are the minimum and maximum deposit limits?

A: The minimum deposit is $10 for most payment methods. Maximum deposit limits vary by method: credit/debit cards allow up to $5,000 per transaction, e-wallets up to $10,000, and bank transfers have no upper limit. VIP players may have higher limits. All deposits are processed instantly except bank transfers, which may take 1-3 business days.
