Q: How long does it take to process a withdrawal?

A: Withdrawal processing times depend on the method: e-wallets (Skrill, Neteller, PayPal) take 0-24 hours, credit/debit cards take 1-3 business days, and bank transfers take 3-5 business days. All withdrawals go through a security review, which typically takes up to 24 hours. The minimum withdrawal amount is $20. Please ensure your account is fully verified before requesting a withdrawal.
