Q: How do welcome bonuses work?

A: New players receive a 100% match bonus on their first deposit, up to $500. The bonus is credited automatically after your deposit. To withdraw bonus funds, you must meet the wagering requirement of 30x the bonus amount within 30 days. For example, if you receive a $100 bonus, you need to place $3,000 in qualifying bets. Slots contribute 100% toward wagering, table games contribute 10%, and live dealer games contribute 5%.
