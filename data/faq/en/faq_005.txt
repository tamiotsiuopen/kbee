Category: Wagering Requirements

Q: What are wagering requirements and how do I check my progress?

A: Wagering requirements indicate how many times you must bet the bonus amount before you can withdraw winnings. You can check your progress in My Account > Bonus Status, which shows the remaining wagering amount. Bets placed with real money are used first before bonus funds. Maximum bet while a bonus is active is $5 per spin or hand. Exceeding this limit may void your bonus and any associated winnings.
