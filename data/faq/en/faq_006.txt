Category: Responsible Gaming

Q: What responsible gaming tools do you offer?

A: We provide several tools to help you stay in control: deposit limits (daily, weekly, monthly), loss limits, session time reminders, cooling-off periods (24 hours, 7 days, 30 days), and self-exclusion (6 months or permanent). You can set these up in Settings > Responsible Gaming. We also partner with GamCare and Gambling Therapy for professional support. If you feel you need help, please contact our support team or call the national gambling helpline.
