Category: Sports Betting

Q: How do I place a sports bet?

A: Navigate to the Sports section, select your sport and event, then click on the odds you want to bet on. This adds the selection to your bet slip. Enter your stake amount, review the potential payout, and click "Place Bet" to confirm. You can place single bets, accumulators (parlays), or system bets. Live in-play betting is also available for most major events. Cash-out options may be available depending on the event and market.
