Q: How does the VIP loyalty program work?

A: Our loyalty program has 5 tiers: Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, and Diamond. You earn loyalty points for every real-money bet placed (1 point per $10 wagered on slots, 1 point per $20 on table games). Points determine your tier and can be exchanged for bonus cash at a rate of 100 points = $1. Higher tiers unlock benefits like faster withdrawals, personal account managers, exclusive promotions, birthday bonuses, and invitations to VIP events.
