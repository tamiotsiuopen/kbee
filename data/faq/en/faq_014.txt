Q: How do I use my free spins?

A: Free spins are credited to specific slot games as indicated in the promotion. Go to the designated game and the free spins will activate automatically. Winnings from free spins are usually credited as bonus funds subject to a 20x wagering requirement. Free spins expire 7 days after being credited if not used. You can check your available free spins in My Account > Bonus Status. Maximum win from free spins is typically capped at $500 unless stated otherwise.
