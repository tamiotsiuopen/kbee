Category: Self-Exclusion

Q: How do I self-exclude from the platform?

A: You can self-exclude by going to Settings > Responsible Gaming > Self-Exclusion. Choose a period of 6 months, 1 year, or permanent exclusion. During self-exclusion, you cannot log in, deposit, or place bets. Any active bonuses will be forfeited. Self-exclusion cannot be reversed during the chosen period. For permanent exclusion, your account will be closed and any remaining balance returned to you. You can also contact support to initiate self-exclusion.
