Q: How do progressive jackpots work?

A: Progressive jackpots grow with every bet placed by players across the network until someone hits the winning combination. The jackpot amount is displayed in real-time on the game. To be eligible for the full jackpot, you typically need to play at the maximum bet level, though some games offer scaled jackpots at lower stakes. Once won, the jackpot resets to a seed amount and starts growing again. Popular progressive jackpot games on our platform include Mega Moolah, Divine Fortune, and Mega Fortune.
