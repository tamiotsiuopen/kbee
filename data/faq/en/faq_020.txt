Category: Complaints & Disputes

Q: How do I file a complaint or dispute a transaction?

A: To file a complaint, contact our support team via live chat, email (support@kbee.com), or the in-app complaint form under Help > File a Complaint. Please provide your account ID, a description of the issue, and any relevant screenshots or transaction IDs. We aim to resolve complaints within 48 hours. If you are unsatisfied with the resolution, you may escalate to our independent dispute resolution partner, eCOGRA, or the relevant gambling authority (MGA or UKGC) depending on your jurisdiction.
