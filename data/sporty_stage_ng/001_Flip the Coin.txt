Flip the Coin

Overview:  is a fast-paced game of chance that keeps the excitement rolling! The game is played with a single coin, and your objective is simple: predict whether the coin will land on heads or tails. With a variety of betting options, you can place a minimum bet of 10 NGN or go big with a maximum bet of 1,500,000 NGN. How to Play: Choose Your Bet: Use the stake selection bar to pick how much you want to bet. 2- : Once you place your bet, the coin spins and rotates a few times before it stops. 3- If your guess matches the coin face, you win double (2x) your bet! 4- If your guess is wrong, you lose the bet. 5- Caution: If the coin stops in the middle, the round is lost. 6- You can always check your past bets by tapping the Bet History button in the menu.
