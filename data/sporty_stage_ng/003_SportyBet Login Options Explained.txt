SportyBet Login Options Explained

Understanding “Keep Me Signed In” and “Remember Me” on SportyBet When you log in to your SportyBet account using your phone number and password, you’ll see two useful options: “Keep Me Signed In” and “Remember Me.” Both are designed to make your login process faster and easier. ? Keep Me Signed In Selecting “Keep Me Signed In” keeps your session active even after you close the app or browser. This means you won’t need to log in again every time you open SportyBet — ideal if you use your account often. If you log out manually, your session will end, and you’ll need to re-enter your login details the next time. ? Remember Me Choosing “Remember Me” saves your login information (like your phone number) on your device. The next time you open the login page, your details will already be filled in, saving you time. ⚠️ Security Tip Use these options only on your personal or trusted devices to keep your account safe. Avoid enabling them on shared or public devices.
