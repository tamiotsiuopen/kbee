How do I delete a Bet from my Bet History

To delete a Bet from your bet history for all settled bets, please follow the steps below: Step 1: Log in to your SportyBet account. Step 2: Navigate to the Bet History section. Step 3: Tap on the "Settled" tab to view your settled bets. Step 4: Click the three-dot icon to access the date filter. This allows you to narrow down specific timeframes and select the tickets you'd like to delete. Step 5: Alternatively, you can either "swipe left on any ticket to delete" or "hold down on a ticket to activate the delete menu, and then select the delete button".
