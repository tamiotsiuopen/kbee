How to Check the SportyBet App Version Installed on Your Mobile Device

To view the version of the SportyBet app currently installed on your mobile device: Open the SportyBet App and log in using your credentials. Scroll down to the bottom of the main screen or homepage. Locate the ‘About Us’ section. Just below the SportyBet icon, you will see the app version number displayed. This method works for most versions of the SportyBet app and is typically consistent across Android and iOS devices.
