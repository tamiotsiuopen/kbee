How to reset the Password?

If you forgot your password or see the message "The credentials are invalid. Please try again", follow these steps to reset it: 1. Click Login at the top-right corner and select Forgot Password? 2. Enter your registered phone number and request an OTP via Telegram, Voice or SMS method. To receive the Telegram option, you need to have an active Telegram account linked to your phone number. 3. Enter the 6-digit code received based on your selected method to receive the OTP. 4. Now, set a new password and you're done! Note: You can request a limited number of verification codes in a day; the same is displayed on the enter OTP page.
