How to Change Your Email Address on SportyBet

How to Change the Email Address on the SportyBet Account Overview You can update your verified email address on your SportyBet account using one of two methods. Please note the limitation on how often you can change your email address. Method 1: Change Email via Profile Section 1. Go to Profile. 2. Click Edit (Email ID). 3. Enter your Password. 4. Confirm your New Email Address. 5. A verification email will be sent to the new address within 72 hours. Method 2: Change Email via Settings 1. Go to Settings. 2. Navigate to Safety and Security. 3. Select Multi-Factor Authentication. 4. Click Edit (Email ID). 5. Enter your Password. 6. Confirm your New Email Address. 7. A verification email will be sent to the new address within 72 hours. Email Change Limit - You can change your verified email address only once per year. - Ensure the new email is active and accessible.
