How to Enable or Disable Biometrics Authentication (Face ID, Touch ID, Fingerprint)

How to Set Up Biometrics Authentication (Face ID, Touch ID, or Fingerprint) Overview This guide explains how to enable biometric authentication (Face ID, Touch ID, or Fingerprint) on your SportyBet account. Enabling Biometrics Authentication 1. Go to Settings in your SportyBet account. 2. Select Multifactor Authentication. 3. Click on Biometrics Authentication. 4. Click Enable Biometrics. 5. Enter your password when prompted. 6. Verify your account using either Voice OTP or SMS OTP. 7. Once the OTP is verified, follow the prompt to scan your face or use your fingerprint. Completion After completing these steps, biometrics authentication will be enabled on your account. Disabling Biometrics Authentication You can disable biometric authentication at any time from the same settings menu. Note If you have tried the above steps and are still unable to set up biometrics authentication, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
