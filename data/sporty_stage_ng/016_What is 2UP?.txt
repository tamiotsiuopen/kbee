What is 2UP?

2UP offers an early payout if your chosen team leads by 2 goals, regardless of the final result - win, lose, draw, or even if the match is abandoned. With 2UP, you can receive your payout early and continue betting on other matches. 2UP is currently available on select football leagues for pre-match bets with a dedicated 1X2 - 2UP market. You can also add 2UP to your selections in the Insure section of your Betslip. 2UP aims to enhance your betting experience, making it more dynamic and engaging. Please note that in multiple bets, if your selected team gains a two-goal lead, that specific selection is considered a win, while the remaining selections continue as normal. ﻿﻿﻿﻿SportyBet reserves the right to void selections if any integrity issues or abusive behaviors are detected. ﻿Steps to Create a 2UP Bet Choose the match or matches you want to bet on, focusing on the 2UP market. Below the stake amount field, go to "Insure" and check the box for 2UP to ensure your bet qualifies for early payout if your team leads by two goals at any point. Review your selections, confirm that 2UP is selected, and place your bet. ﻿﻿
