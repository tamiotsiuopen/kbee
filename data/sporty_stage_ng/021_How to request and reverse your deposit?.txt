How to request and reverse your deposit?

SportyBet accepts no responsibility should you make a deposit into any account other than that of your SportyBet account. Your deposit is credited to the SportyBet account using the Bank Card or Bank Account information provided by you. We suggest you reach out to the respective financial institution and raise a dispute against this erroneous transaction. It is your responsibility to ensure that you add the correct details as indicated on our site instructions and your SportyBet account and/or payment details. In the event that an error occurs, SportyBet accepts no responsibility for recovering these funds.
