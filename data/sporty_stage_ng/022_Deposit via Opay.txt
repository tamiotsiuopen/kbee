Deposit via Opay

Deposits via Opay can be made via the below methods: 1.Opay Checkout a) Log in to your SportyBet account: From the homepage, click on your Account Balance in the top-right corner or select the “Me” tab at the bottom right. Then, click on “Deposit”. b) Enter Deposit Amount: Enter the amount you wish to deposit into your account. Click the “Top Up Now” button. c) Complete the Transaction: Enter your PIN (depending on the verification method used) to complete the transaction. d) Confirmation: You will receive a confirmation that your SportyBet account has been successfully funded! 2. Pay from Opay App Log into your OPay App. From the home page, click on “Betting” and select SportyBet from the list. Enter your SportyBet Phone Number for eg. 080xxxxxxxx and the amount you wish to deposit. Confirm all your details are correct and select your preferred payment method. Upon successful payment, you will receive a notification and your SportyBet wallet will be credited instantly.
