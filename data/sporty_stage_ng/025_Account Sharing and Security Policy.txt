Account Sharing and Security Policy

Can Someone Else Use My Account? Account Sharing Policy - It is not recommended to share your account information with anyone else for security reasons. - SportyBet holds no responsibility if you share your account details with others. Security Warning - If your account details are compromised, fraudsters may attempt to defraud you. Note: If you have already shared your account details and are experiencing issues, and the above information does not resolve your concern, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
