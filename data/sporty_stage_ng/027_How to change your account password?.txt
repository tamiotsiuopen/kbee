How to change your account password?

To change the password, please follow the below steps Step 1: Log in to your SportyBet account, and go to the "My Account" drop-down menu. Step 2: Click on Settings next to the username at the top of the screen Step 3: You will be redirected to "My Account Info". Then click on "Change Password". NOTE : Once an account is created with a verified phone number, it cannot be changed to a different number. If you have lost your SIM, we request you to please reach out to your mobile network provider and request a new SIM with the same number.
