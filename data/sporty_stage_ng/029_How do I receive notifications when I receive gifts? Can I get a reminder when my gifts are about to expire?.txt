How do I receive notifications when I receive gifts?

Can I get a reminder when my gifts are about to expire?

To ensure our users do not miss out on availing gifts, SportyBet will send notifications on receipt of a new gift to the account, and a reminder alert is sent 24 hours before the gift expires. Make sure that the alerts are activated by enabling Promotions from Notifications Settings. This can be done by clicking on ME and then click on the settings icon on the top right corner and click on Notification settings. The alerts can be viewed in the Notification Centre by simply clicking on ME>>>Notification Centre
