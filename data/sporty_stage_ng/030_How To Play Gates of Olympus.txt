How To Play Gates of Olympus

Gates of Olympus - How to Play 1. Game Overview The Gates into Zeus’ realm open wide to all players in Gates of Olympus™, the 6×5 video slot where symbols tumble down and pay in clusters of at least 8 matching symbols. Zeus’ gifts come in the form of multiplier symbols up to 500x. Enter Free Spins with 15 free games, where all multipliers are added together, unlocking massive win potential. 2. Game Objective The objective is to spin the reels, form winning symbol clusters, and maximize winnings using multiplier symbols and Free Spins. 3. Reel, Pay & Cluster Structure Reels: 6 Rows: 5 Layout: 6×5 Pay Mechanic: Cluster Pays Minimum Winning Cluster: 8 matching symbols Paylines: Not traditional; wins are based on clusters Wins are awarded when 8 or more identical symbols appear anywhere on the grid, connected horizontally or vertically. 4. Gameplay Flow Set Your Bet Choose your bet amount before spinning. Spin the Reels Press the Spin button to start the round. Symbols fall into place from the top. Cluster Win Evaluation The game checks for clusters of 8 or more matching symbols. Winning symbols are removed. Tumble Feature New symbols fall down to replace removed ones. Additional wins can occur in the same round. 5. Multiplier Symbols Multiplier symbols appear as Zeus multipliers. Possible multiplier values range from 2x up to 500x. Multipliers apply to the total win of the current tumble sequence. Multiple multipliers in the same round are added together. 6. Free Spins Feature How to Trigger Free Spins are triggered by landing the required Scatter symbols (as defined in-game). Free Spins Rules 15 Free Spins are awarded. During Free Spins: All multiplier symbols are collected and added together. The total multiplier is applied to the win at the end of the spin. This feature offers very high win potential. 7. Winning Rules Wins are calculated based on: Cluster size Symbol value Bet amount Applied multipliers Tumbles continue until no new winning clusters are formed. 8.
