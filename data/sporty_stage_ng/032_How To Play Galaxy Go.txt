How To Play Galaxy Go

Galaxy Go – How to Play 1. Game Overview Galaxy Go is a crash game where a spaceship launches and flies forward while a win coefficient increases continuously. The player must cash out before the spaceship vanishes. If the spaceship vanishes before cash out, the bet is lost. 2. Game Objective The objective is to cash out at the right time to multiply your bet by the current coefficient. Cash out before the spaceship vanishes → Win Fail to cash out before it vanishes → Lose bet 3. Game Flow (Step-by-Step) Place Bet Select a bet amount. Press the BET button before the round starts. Watch the Flight The spaceship takes off. The coefficient starts at 1.00x and increases continuously as the spaceship flies. Cash Out Press CASH OUT at any time before the spaceship vanishes. Your winnings are locked at the coefficient shown at the moment of cash out. 4. Coefficient Rules The coefficient always starts at 1x. It increases continuously during the round. The round ends when the spaceship vanishes. The exact vanishing point is random and unpredictable. Winning Formula: Winnings = Bet Amount × Cash-out Coefficient 5. Bet & Cash Out Rules You must place your bet before the round starts. You can cash out anytime while the spaceship is flying. If you do not cash out before the spaceship vanishes: The entire bet amount is lost. Once cashed out, winnings are instantly settled. 6. One-Tap Bet Feature When One-Tap Bet is enabled: Bet confirmation pop-ups are disabled. Bets are placed instantly with a single tap. This feature does not change game outcomes, only bet confirmation behavior. 7. Free Bet Gifts Availability Free Bet Gifts can be checked via the Gift icon on the screen. If no Free Bet Gift is available, the Gift icon will not appear. Usage Rules You may use: The full gift amount, or A partial gift amount. You cannot combine: Real money + Free Bet Gift in the same bet. Only one funding type is allowed per bet, Either Free Bet Gift OR real money. 8.
