How To Play Sporty Kick

About Sporty Kick Sporty Kick is a crash game where a ball is kicked at the beginning of each round and travels through the stadium and into the sky. A coefficient (multiplier) increases continuously as the ball flies higher. To win, players must cash out before the crash event, which happens when the ball deflates and falls. If you fail to cash out before the crash, your bet is lost. ﻿﻿ How to Play Sporty Kick Step 1: Place a Bet Select your bet amount before the round starts. Press the BET button to participate in the round. Step 2: Watch the Coefficient Increase Once the round begins, the ball is kicked and starts flying upward. The coefficient starts at 1.00x and increases continuously (example: 22.65x). The longer the ball stays in the air, the higher the potential reward. Step 3: Cash Out to Win Press CASH OUT at any time before the ball crashes. Your winnings are secured at the coefficient displayed when you cash out. If the ball crashes before you cash out, your bet is lost. Coefficients Explained Coefficients represent the multiplier applied to your bet. Every round starts at 1x and increases until the crash occurs. Higher coefficients offer higher rewards but involve greater risk. Winnings Formula Your Winnings = Your Bet × Cashout Coefficient Example: Bet Amount: 200 Cashout Coefficient: 22.65x Winnings: 200 × 22.65 = 4530 Betting & Cashout Rules Placing a Bet Choose your bet amount. Press BET before the round starts. Cashing Out Press CASH OUT while the ball is still flying. Winnings are instantly credited after a successful cashout. Losing a Bet If you do not cash out before the ball crashes, the bet is fully lost. Rain Feature (Free Bets) The Rain feature randomly drops Free Bets in the chat. Click the “Claim” button to receive the Free Bet. Free Bets must be used in Sporty Kick before they expire. Expired Free Bets cannot be used.
