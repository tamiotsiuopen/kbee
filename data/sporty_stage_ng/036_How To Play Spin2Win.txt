How To Play Spin2Win

About Spin2Win Spin2Win is a fast-paced modern take on European Roulette. The wheel has numbers 1 to 36 and one GREEN section. Numbers between 1 and 36 can be RED or BLACK. Players can place a maximum of 10 bets in one spin. Types of Bets Exact Number Bet on which number will be drawn next. Dozens Bet on which dozen the next drawn number belongs to: 1–12 13–24 25–36 Even/Odd Bet on whether the next drawn number will be EVEN or ODD. Color Bet on the next drawn number’s color: RED, BLACK, or GREEN. Sector Bet on the next drawn number’s sector. There are 6 sectors: A: 1–6 B: 7–12 C: 13–18 D: 19–24 E: 25–30 F: 31–36 High/Low Bet on whether the next drawn number will be HIGH or LOW: LOW: 1–18 HIGH: 19–36 High/Low/Red/Black Bet on combinations like: LOW & RED LOW & BLACK HIGH & RED HIGH & BLACK Color & Combination Details LOW & RED: 1, 3, 5, 7, 9, 12, 14, 16, 18 LOW & BLACK: 2, 4, 6, 8, 10, 11, 13, 15, 17 HIGH & RED: 19, 21, 23, 25, 27, 30, 32, 34, 36 HIGH & BLACK: 20, 22, 24, 26, 28, 29, 31, 33, 35 Payouts Exact Number: 36x Dozens: 3x Even/Odd: 2x Color: 2x (RED or BLACK), 36x (GREEN) Sector: 6x Gameplay Steps Tap any bet type button on the game board. Set your bet amount by tapping on chips. Tap Spin when ready. Tap Confirm to place bets → wheel spins → chance to win. Use Rebet to repeat your last bet or place a new one. Features UNDO: Removes last chip placed. CLEAR ALL: Removes all chips. STATS: View last 10 spins. PAYOUTS: View payout table. REBET: Repeat previous bet. BET HISTORY: See past bets. ONE TAP BET: Skip confirmations. Free Bet Gifts Check available gifts via Gift icon. Use full or partial gift amount. Cannot combine real money with Free Bet Gift in one bet.
