How to Play Spin Match

 🎯 Game Objective The goal of Spin Match is to predict the multiplier that will appear on both spinning wheels. Multipliers available for betting are: x2, x4, x6, x15, x24, x54 You can place bets on multiple multipliers at the same time. 1️⃣ Place Bets Choose one or more multipliers to bet on. You can adjust your stake for each selected multiplier. Controls: Undo: Removes the last chip placed. Clear All: Removes all placed bets. One-Tap Bet: Skip confirmation pop-ups for faster gameplay. 2️⃣ Spin the Wheels Two wheels spin simultaneously. Wait for both wheels to stop to see the result. 3️⃣ Winning Conditions Exact Match: If both wheels stop at the multiplier you bet on → You Win. Partial Match: If wheels stop at different multipliers → Winning multiplier = x2 (applies only if you bet on x2). No Match: If neither wheel stops on your chosen multiplier → You Lose. 4️⃣ Wild Rules Wild Symbol: Acts as the multiplier of the other wheel. Wild on Both Wheels: Triggers a Free Spin. 5️⃣ Calculating Winnings Winning Amount=Stake×Winning Multiplier×Winning Multiplier\text{Winning Amount} = \text{Stake} \times \text{Winning Multiplier} \times \text{Winning Multiplier}Winning Amount=Stake×Winning Multiplier×Winning MultiplierExample: Stake = 10 Winning multiplier = x6 Winning Amount = 10 × 6 × 6 = 360 6️⃣ Free Bet Gifts Check available gifts via the Gift icon. Note: Free Bet Gifts cannot be combined with real money bets.
