Can I open another account?

Can I close my current account and open another one?

SportyBet only allows one account per customer. Any new account registered after self-excluding or deactivating the primary account without acceptable reasons will be considered as duplicate/ multiple accounts. Owning duplicate/ multiple SportyBet accounts may lead to closure of the accounts while any funds in the account may be withheld as per SportyBet policies. Kindly check SportyBet’s Terms and Conditions > Your SportyBet Account> Clause 1.6 for more details. We urge our users to use only their primary account. If you're experiencing issues accessing your primary account, kindly reach out to SportyBet's customer support for help.
