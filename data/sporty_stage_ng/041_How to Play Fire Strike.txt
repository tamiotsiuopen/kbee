How to Play Fire Strike

1. Game Overview Fire Strike is a slot machine game. It has 5 reels and 3 rows, with 5 fixed paylines. The game is easy to play and made for fast spins and big wins. 2. Game Goal The goal is to spin the reels and match the same symbols on a payline to win prizes. 3. Reels & Paylines Reels: 5 Rows: 3 Layout: 3×5 Paylines: 5 (cannot be changed) Only wins that happen on these paylines will pay. 4. How to Play Set Your Bet Choose how much you want to bet before you spin. Spin the Reels Press the Spin button to start. All 5 reels spin at the same time. Check Wins When the reels stop, the game checks the paylines. If you win, the payout is added automatically. 5. Winning Rules You win when the same symbols land on a payline. Your win amount depends on: The type of symbol How many symbols match The size of your bet All payouts are shown in the paytable. 6. Game Style Classic slot machine game Easy rules No special or complicated features Made for: Fast play Clear results Simple gaming experience 7. Game Information & Help You can find full game details, symbols, and payouts by: Clicking the “i” (information) button in the game The “i” button is at the bottom-left of the game screen. 8. Important Notes Fire Strike is a slot game, not a skill or crash game Each spin is random Paylines are fixed and cannot be changed All winning rules are listed in the game paytable
