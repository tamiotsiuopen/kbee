How to play Skyward

1. Game Overview Skyward is a crash game. A plane takes off and flies forward. As it flies, a multiplier keeps going up. You must cash out before the plane crashes. If the plane crashes before you cash out, you lose your bet. 2. Game Goal The goal is to cash out at the right time. Cash out before the plane crashes → Win Do not cash out before it crashes → Lose Your bet is multiplied by the number shown when you cash out. 3. How to Play (Step by Step) Place Your Bet Choose how much you want to bet Press the BET button before the round starts Watch the Flight The plane takes off The multiplier starts at 1.00x The multiplier keeps increasing as the plane flies Cash Out Press CASH OUT anytime before the plane crashes Your win is saved at the multiplier shown at that moment 4. Multiplier Rules The multiplier always starts at 1x It keeps increasing during the round The round ends when the plane crashes The crash time is random and cannot be known in advance Win Formula: Winnings = Bet Amount × Cash-Out Multiplier 5. Bet & Cash Out Rules You must place your bet before the round starts You can cash out anytime while the plane is flying If you do not cash out before the crash: You lose the full bet amount Once you cash out, winnings are paid instantly 6. One-Tap Bet Feature When One-Tap Bet is turned on: No confirmation messages appear Bets are placed instantly with one tap This feature does not affect game results, only how bets are placed 7. When You Lose You lose your bet if: The plane crashes before you cash out You do not press cash out during the round 8. When You Win You win if: You cash out before the plane crashes. Winnings are calculated using the coefficient at cash-out time. 9. Important Notes The crash point is random and cannot be predicted. Higher multipliers mean higher risk and higher rewards. The player controls when to cash out. Skyward does not guarantee winnings.
