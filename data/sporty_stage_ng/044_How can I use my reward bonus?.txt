How can I use my reward bonus?

How can I use my rewards bonus? A reward bonus can mean two different things: A bonus that you get for qualifying for multiple bets. Gifts that are applied to qualifying bets. The rewards bonus is automatically applied to qualifying multiple betslips and is included in the Potential Winning amount, which already reflects both your regular payout and any bonus you qualify for. Only the Free Bet Gift can be used directly. When a Free Bet Gift is used, its value will be deducted from any potential winnings if the total return is higher than the stake.
