How to Play Mines

1. Game Overview Use your luck to find gems without clicking on a mine. 2. Game Goal The goal is to find gems and cash out before hitting a mine. Cash out before hitting a mine → Win Hit a mine before cashing out → Lose Your bet is multiplied by the number shown each time you open a safe block. 3. How to Play (Step by Step) Choose how many mines you want Press Start Game Click on blocks to find gems Avoid the mines Press Cash Out anytime to collect your win 4. Multiplier Rules The lowest win multiplier is 1.03x The highest possible multiplier is 4,940,285x This can happen when using 13 mines and opening 12 safe blocks in a row The maximum win is limited by the operator You can check win limits in the Limits section in the settings menu 5. Bet & Cash Out Rules After finding one or more gems, you can press Cash Out Win amount = Bet amount × Current multiplier Winnings are rounded down to two decimal places If you find all gems, the game will cash out automatically 6. When You Lose You lose your bet if: You hit a mine before cashing out You do not cash out during the round 7. When You Win You win if: You cash out before hitting a mine Your winnings are based on the multiplier at the time you cash out 8. Important Notes Mine positions are random and cannot be predicted Higher multipliers come with higher risk and higher rewards The player decides when to cash out Mines does not guarantee winnings.
