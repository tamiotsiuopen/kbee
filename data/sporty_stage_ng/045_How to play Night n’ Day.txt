How to play Night n’ Day

Gameplay Overview The game is played using a pointer placed on a circular dial. The dial is divided into two equal parts: DAY and NIGHT. Players place their bets before the pointer is spun. Once the bet is placed, the pointer spins for a few moments. After spinning, the pointer comes to a stop. The direction of the pointer determines the result of the round. Winning & Losing Rules If the pointer stops on the selected side (DAY or NIGHT), the player wins. Each winning turn pays DOUBLE (2X) the stake amount. If the pointer stops at the MIDDLE (the dividing line between DAY and NIGHT), the player loses the bet. Betting Controls Players can select their desired stake amount by: Adjusting the stake slider between the minimum and maximum values, or Selecting predefined betting chips. Bets must be placed before the spin begins. Navigation & Bet History Players can view their betting history by clicking on the Bet History option from the hamburger (☰) menu. Pressing the “<” button at the top-left of the screen takes the player back to the Game Lobby. One-Tap Bet Turning on One-Tap Bet stops bet confirmation pop-ups from appearing. This allows faster and smoother gameplay. Free Bet Gifts Available Free Bet Gifts can be accessed by clicking on the Gift icon on the screen. If no Free Bet Gifts are available, the Gift icon will not be shown. Players can choose to use: The full Free Bet amount, or A partial Free Bet amount. Free Bet Gifts cannot be combined with real money in a single bet. Final Notes Night & Day is easy to understand and quick to play. The game offers a balanced mix of simplicity and excitement. A stable internet connection is recommended for the best gameplay experience.
