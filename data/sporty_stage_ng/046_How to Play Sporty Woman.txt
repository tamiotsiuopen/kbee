How to Play Sporty Woman

Game Overview Sporty Woman is an adrenaline-pumping, single-player crash game where you step into the shoes of a female superhero flying through a space road. As you fly, gems appear in two lanes. The goal is to collect gems to increase the multiplier while avoiding crash gems. If you collect a crash gem, the game ends, and you lose your bet. Game Goal Cashing Out Before You Collect a Crash Gem → Win Failing to Cash Out or Collecting a Crash Gem → Lose Your bet is multiplied by the multiplier at the time you cash out. How to Play (Step by Step) Place Your Bet Choose your bet amount. Press the BET button before the round begins. Fly Through the Space Road You control Sporty Woman, who flies through a space road. Gems appear in the lane as you fly. Switch Lanes to Collect Gems Use the Change Lane button to switch between the two lanes and collect gems. Collecting gems increases the multiplier. The more gems you collect, the higher the multiplier. Avoid Crash Gems Some gems are Crash Gems. These will end your run if you collect them. Be careful and avoid these dangerous gems while flying. Cash Out Press CASH OUT anytime before you collect a crash gem. Your winnings are calculated based on the multiplier at the time you cash out. Multiplier Rules The multiplier starts at 1x and increases as you collect gems. The round ends if you collect a Crash Gem. The Crash Gem locations are random and cannot be predicted. You must cash out before collecting a crash gem to secure your winnings. Win Formula Winnings = Bet Amount × Cash-Out Multiplier Bet & Cash Out Rules Place your bet before the round begins. You can switch lanes and collect gems to increase your multiplier. If you collect a Crash Gem or fail to cash out in time, you lose your bet. Winnings are paid immediately when you cash out. One-Tap Bet Feature One-Tap Bet allows you to place your bet instantly without any confirmation messages. This feature only affects how bets are placed and does not change the game outcomes.
