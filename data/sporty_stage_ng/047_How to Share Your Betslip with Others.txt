How to Share Your Betslip with Others

1. Share Right After Placing a Bet Immediately after placing a bet, look for the Share button on the confirmation screen, located next to the booking code. Clicking this button lets you copy the link or share it directly on platforms like X, Telegram, WhatsApp, and Facebook. 2. Share from Your Sports Bet History If you missed sharing at the time of placing the bet, you can still share it later from your Sports Bet History. Navigate to your Sports Bet History and open the Unsettled tab. Find the bet you want to share and click on it. Below the Booking Code, you will see a Share icon. Clicking it allows you to copy the link or share your betslip on social media. Important Note: Users cannot share the actual betslip once it is placed; they can share their bet selection. However, if you have won a bet, you can click on Show Off to share the Winning Pop-up and Ticket Snapshot on social media. This is a fun way to celebrate your wins and show your achievements to friends.
