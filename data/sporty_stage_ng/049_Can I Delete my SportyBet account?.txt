Can I Delete my SportyBet account?

Please note that a number once registered to open a SportyBet account cannot be deleted. However, you may deactivate your SportyBet account: 1. Deactivation While Logged In To deactivate your SportyBet account while logged in: - Go to My Account - Navigate to Safety & Security - Click on Deactivate - Enter your registered mobile number - Select 'Deactivate' - Choose 'Other' as the reason - Complete the account ownership verification ✅ Result: Your account will be temporarily deactivated 2. Deactivation While Logged Out To deactivate your SportyBet account while logged out: - Navigate to the login page and click on Deactivate or Reactivate Your Account - Enter your registered mobile number - Select 'Deactivate' - Choose 'Other' as the reason - Complete the account ownership verification ✅ Result: Your account will be temporarily deactivated
