How to Play Magic Money Tree

Game Overview Magic Money Tree is a crash game where you bet on how many coins notes a magical tree can grow. The more money it produces, the higher the multiplier. The goal is to cash out before the Lightning strike. If the tree stops before you cash out, you lose your bet. Game Goal Cashing Out Before the Lightning strike → Win Failing to Cash Out Before the Tree Stops → Lose Your bet is multiplied by the multiplier at the time you cash out. How to Play (Step by Step) Place Your Bet Choose the amount you wish to bet. Press the BET button before the round begins. Watch the Tree Grow Coins The magic tree begins to grow coins. The multiplier starts at 1.00x and increases as more coins are produced. Cash Out Press CASH OUT before the Lightning strike on the tree. Your win is calculated using the multiplier at the time of cash-out. Multiplier Rules The multiplier starts at 1x and increases as more coins are produced. The round ends when the tree stops producing coins. The stopping point is random and cannot be predicted. Win Formula Winnings = Bet Amount × Cash-Out Multiplier Bet & Cash Out Rules Place your bet before the round starts. You can cash out anytime during the round. If the tree stops producing coins before you cash out, you lose your bet. Winnings are paid immediately after you cash out. One-Tap Bet Feature One-Tap Bet allows for immediate betting without confirmation messages. This feature does not affect the results. When You Lose You lose your bet if the tree stops producing coins before you cash out. When You Win You win if you cash out before the tree stops producing coins. Winnings are calculated using the multiplier at the moment of cash-out. Important Notes The tree's stopping point is random and cannot be predicted
