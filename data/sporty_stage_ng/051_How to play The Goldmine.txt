How to play The Goldmine

Game Overview The Goldmine is an exciting mining-themed game where you bet on how deep a miner can dig for gold. The deeper the miner goes, the higher the multiplier. The goal is to cash out before the miner hits an obstacle or gets stuck. If the miner hits an obstacle before you cash out, you lose your bet. Game Goal Cashing Out Before the Miner Hits an Obstacle → Win Failing to Cash Out Before the Miner Hits an Obstacle → Lose Your bet is multiplied by the multiplier at the moment you cash out. How to Play (Step by Step) Place Your Bet Choose your bet amount. Press the BET button before the round starts. Watch the Miner Dig Dig for Treasure • Tap the bet button to unearth a winning symbol. • Each symbol corresponds to a multiplier. • Your reward is: Multiplier x Bet • Explore Both Maps Freely • Switch maps at any time. • Collect all symbols on both maps to complete the full journal!. Cash Out Auto bet and turbo bet • Players can select "Auto bet" mode to automatically dig multiple times and set the number of digs in advance. • Players can enable "Turbo" mode to speed up the process and skip the animation. Turbo mode can be toggled on or off from either the main screen or the side panel. • Players can click on the bet history button inside the side panel and view the history of the bet placed by them. • Turning on One-Tap Bet will stop bet confirmations from appearing. One-Tap Bet Feature One-Tap Bet allows you to place bets instantly, with no confirmation messages. It does not affect the game results. When You Lose You lose your bet if the miner hits an obstacle before you cash out or if you forget to cash out in time. When You Win You win if you cash out before the miner hits an obstacle. Winnings are calculated using the multiplier at the time of your cash-out. Important Notes The obstacle point is random and cannot be predicted. Higher multipliers mean higher rewards but also higher risks. You control when to cash out, but there is no guarantee of winning.
