How to Play Sporty Sub

Game Overview Sporty Sub is a thrilling underwater adventure game where players bet on how deep a submarine can dive. As the submarine descends, a multiplier increases. The goal is to cash out before the submarine sinks. If the submarine reaches its deepest point or sinks before you cash out, you lose your bet. Game Goal Cashing Out Before the Submarine Sinks → Win Failing to Cash Out Before the Submarine Sinks → Lose Your bet is multiplied by the multiplier at the time you cash out. How to Play (Step by Step) Place Your Bet Choose the amount you want to bet. Press the BET button before the round starts. Watch the Submarine Dive The submarine begins to dive. The multiplier starts at 1.00x and increases as the submarine goes deeper. Cash Out Press CASH OUT at any point before the submarine reaches its deepest point or sinks. Your win is calculated using the multiplier at the time of cash-out. Multiplier Rules The multiplier starts at 1x. The multiplier increases as the submarine dives deeper. The round ends when the submarine sinks or reaches its maximum depth. The crash point is random and cannot be predicted in advance. Win Formula Winnings = Bet Amount × Cash-Out Multiplier Bet & Cash Out Rules Place your bet before the round starts. You can cash out at any point during the round. If you do not cash out before the submarine sinks, you lose your full bet amount. Winnings are paid instantly when you cash out. One-Tap Bet Feature One-Tap Bet allows you to place bets instantly without confirmation messages. This feature only affects how bets are placed and does not impact the game outcome. When You Lose You lose your bet if the submarine sinks before you cash out or if you forget to cash out during the round. When You Win You win if you cash out before the submarine sinks. Your winnings are calculated based on the multiplier at the moment you cash out. Important Notes The submarine's sinking point is random and cannot be predicted.
