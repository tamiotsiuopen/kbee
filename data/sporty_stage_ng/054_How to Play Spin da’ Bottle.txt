How to Play Spin da’ Bottle

Introduction • Spin da’ Bottle is a simple and engaging prediction game where players bet on the final position of a spinning bottle. • With just two main outcomes, UP or DOWN, the game offers quick rounds and instant results. Game Objective • The objective of the game is to correctly predict whether the bottle’s neck will stop facing UP or DOWN after the spin. Gameplay Overview • A bottle is placed on a circular table divided into two halves: UP and DOWN. • Players place their bets before the bottle is spun. • The bottle spins for a few moments and then comes to rest. • The final position of the bottle’s neck determines the outcome. Winning & Losing Rules • If the bottle’s neck stops in the chosen direction, the player wins. • Each winning round pays DOUBLE (2X) the stake amount. • If the bottle’s neck stops at the MIDDLE, the player loses the bet. Betting Controls • Players can select their stake amount by: • Using the stake slider, or • Selecting predefined betting chips. • Stakes must be placed before the spin begins. Navigation & History • Players can view their betting history through the Bet History option in the hamburger menu. • Pressing the “<” button at the top-left returns the player to the Game Lobby. One-Tap Bet • Turning on One-Tap Bet disables bet confirmation pop-ups, allowing faster gameplay.
