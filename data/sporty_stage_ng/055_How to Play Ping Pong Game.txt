How to Play Ping Pong Game

Introduction • Ping Pong is a real-time crash game where two players engage in a continuous rally while a multiplier increases over time. • The challenge lies in deciding when to cash out before the rally ends. • The longer the rally continues, the higher the potential reward—but also the greater the risk. Gameplay Overview • Each round begins with a rally between two players. • Players can place up to two bets per round before the rally starts. • Once the round begins, the multiplier increases continuously as the rally progresses. • The rally ends at a randomly determined point. Betting & Cash Out • Players must place their bets before the match begins. • During the rally, players can cash out at any time. • Winnings are calculated based on the multiplier at the moment of cash out. • If a player does not cash out before the rally ends, the bet is lost. Multiplier System • The multiplier starts at 1x and increases as the rally continues. • Winnings are calculated as: • Bet Amount × Cash-out Multiplier • Before each round starts, a provably fair random number generator determines the multiplier at which the rally will end. Multiple Bets • Players can place two simultaneous bets by using the second betting panel. • Each bet can be cashed out independently at different multipliers. Auto Bet & Auto Cash Out • Auto Bet automatically places the same bet amount in each new round until turned off. • Auto Cash Out automatically cashes out winnings when the selected multiplier is reached. • Auto features are disabled if the game is sent to background mode. One-Tap Bet • Enabling One-Tap Bet removes bet confirmation prompts for faster gameplay. Free Bet Gifts • Available Free Bet Gifts can be checked using the Gift icon. • If no gifts are available, the icon will not appear. • Players can use either: • Full Free Bet amount, or • Partial Free Bet amount. • Free Bet Gifts cannot be combined with real money.
