How to play Rush

Game Overview Rush is an adrenaline-filled game where you bet on how fast a vehicle can accelerate. The faster the vehicle goes, the higher the multiplier. The goal is to cash out before the vehicle crashes. If the vehicle crashes before you cash out, you lose your bet. Game Goal Cashing Out Before the Vehicle Crashes → Win Failing to Cash Out Before the Vehicle Crashes → Lose Your bet is multiplied by the multiplier at the time you cash out. How to Play (Step by Step) Place Your Bet Choose your bet amount. Press the BET button before the round begins. Watch the Vehicle Speed Up The vehicle begins to accelerate, and the multiplier starts at 1.00x. Before the round start you need to set the target Multiplier. Cash Out If the selected multiplier set by you reaches you will automatically won and lose bet according to your selection. Multiplier Rules The multiplier starts at 1x and increases as the vehicle accelerates. The round ends when the Multiplier crashes. The crash point is random and cannot be predicted. Win Formula Winnings = Bet Amount × Cash-Out Multiplier set Bet & Cash Out Rules Place your bet before the round begins. You can cash out anytime while the vehicle is accelerating. If you fail to cash out before the crash, you lose the bet. Winnings are paid immediately when you cash out. One-Tap Bet Feature One-Tap Bet allows for instant betting with no confirmation messages. It only affects how bets are placed, not the game results. When You Lose You lose your bet if the vehicle crashes before your set Multiplier When You Win You win if you cash out before the vehicle crashes. Winnings are calculated based on the multiplier at the moment you cash out. Important Notes The vehicle crash Multiplier is random and cannot be predicted. Higher multipliers offer greater rewards but come with higher risk. You control the timing of when to cash out.
