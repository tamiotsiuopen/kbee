How to Play Mayan Ancient Riches

Welcome to Mayan Ancient Riches, a classic slot game inspired by ancient treasures and mysterious civilizations. This guide explains the game rules, betting options, and controls to help you get started. Game Overview Paylines: 5 fixed paylines Winning Direction: Left to right on adjacent reels only Win Calculation: All wins are multiplied by the bet per line Multiple Payline Wins: All winning paylines are added together to form the total win How to Win Match identical symbols on a payline starting from the leftmost reel. Winning symbols must appear on adjacent reels. Only combinations that pay from left to right are valid. If more than one payline wins in a single spin, all wins are combined. Betting Bet Selection Click the Bet Panel to open the bet options. Choose your preferred bet amount. Bet Adjustment Use the – / + Bet Adjustment Buttons to decrease or increase your bet value. All wins are calculated based on the selected bet per line. Game Controls Spin Button Click to spin the reels and start the game. Turbo Button Click to enable faster reel spins for quicker gameplay. Auto Spin Button Click to open the Auto Spin panel. Select the number of automatic spins. Auto Spin can be stopped at any time. Menu & Information Menu Button Click to open the game menu. Rule Button Click to view the detailed game rules and paytable. History Button Click to view your game and betting history. Audio Controls Audio Button (Outside Menu) Click to mute or unmute all game audio. Music Button Click to mute or unmute background music. Sound Button Click to mute or unmute sound effects.
