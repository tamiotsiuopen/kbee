Withdrawal failed due to the name not matching

If you are unable to withdraw due to a name mismatch, you will get the error message below. It is because the name on the withdrawal bank account doesn't match the name on the SportyBet account. You can change the name on the account by following the below steps: Try making the withdrawal again and when the error message comes up, Click on "Customer service" showing below that error message. You will be redirected to another page to verify, Click on "Verify". Please enter your NIN details in the "Enter NIN" field as shown in the image below to get your name updated correctly. If you don't have an NIN or get an error entering your NIN, please click on the link "I don't have an NIN" and fill out the requested details. Fill in the online form and upload the details requested: Full Name (First <space> Middle, Last) ID Info First Deposit Info Click "Submit" (Submit button will only be active after you have updated the 3 details, and documents should be below 5MB). To further verify your account, please select your preferred method from the options mentioned on the screen to receive your OTP. To receive the Telegram option, you need to have an active Telegram account linked to your phone number. Input the 6-digit code (OTP) that was received by you as per your preferred method of delivery. Wait for 48 hours for document verification. If rejected, check the reason by clicking on the "Rejected" button, then click on "Verify" again and upload the correct details. Once the details are verified, the name will be updated. If it's been more than 48 hours since you submitted the documents and still have not received any feedback from us. Please click on "Contact Support" to chat with customer support. Note: If a withdrawal has been processed successfully from your SportyBet account in the past, the name on the account cannot be changed.
