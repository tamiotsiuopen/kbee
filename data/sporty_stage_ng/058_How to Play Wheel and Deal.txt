How to Play Wheel and Deal

Welcome to Wheel and Deal, an exciting wheel-based game where every spin can land you a rewarding multiplier. This guide explains the gameplay, rules, and features to help you play with confidence. Game Overview Players spin a wheel divided into colored segments. Each segment contains a multiplier. Your win is calculated by multiplying your bet amount by the multiplier where the wheel stops. How to Play Enter the game and set the following options: Bet Amount Risk Level Segment Setting Alternatively, enable Auto Spin and choose the number of spins. Click Start to spin the wheel. You may also choose Auto Mode for continuous spins. After each spin, the wheel stops on a multiplier segment. Your prize is awarded based on the multiplier landed. Game Rules Multipliers Each wheel segment has a different multiplier, represented by different colors. When the wheel stops, the multiplier on that segment determines the payout. Example: If the wheel stops on a 4.00x segment and the bet amount is 100, the total payout is 400 (including the original bet). Betting Options Bet Amount Players can set their bet amount in the Bet Amount section at the bottom of the screen. Risk & Segment Settings Risk Levels Players can choose one of three risk levels: Low Risk – Lower multipliers, higher chance of landing wins Medium Risk – Balanced chance and reward High Risk – Higher multipliers, lower probability Higher risk levels offer the potential for larger multipliers. Segment Settings Players can choose how many segments the wheel contains: 10 Segments 30 Segments 50 Segments The segment setting determines how the wheel is divided and affects gameplay outcomes. Risk & Segment Combinations Risk and segment settings can be combined freely. There are 9 possible combinations in total. Players can select the combination that best suits their play style. Auto Spin Feature Enable Auto Spin to spin the wheel automatically. Select the number of spins you want to play. Auto Spin can be stopped at any time. Turbo Spin Enable Turbo Mode to skip wheel
