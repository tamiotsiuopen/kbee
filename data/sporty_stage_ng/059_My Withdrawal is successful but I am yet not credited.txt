My Withdrawal is successful but I am yet not credited

If your withdrawal has been successful but has not been credited to your bank account, kindly share the Session ID for the corresponding withdrawal transaction with your bank. The Session ID is generated for transfers once the transfer is successfully initiated. It serves as proof of transfer. The bank should be able to trace the transfers using the session ID and apply the amount to your bank balance. Kindly treat this Session ID confidentially and never reveal it to anyone other than your bank. To find the Session ID for your withdrawal transaction, please follow the below steps Step 1: Log in to your account. Step 2: Click on your Account Balance on the top-right corner. Step 3: Click on transactions. Step 4: Select Withdrawals from the drop-down column Step 5: Select the Withdrawal transaction of which Session ID is to be noted. Step 6: Please find the Session ID under the withdrawal transaction details. If you have contacted your bank with the session ID and still have not received the funds in your bank account. Please click on 'Contact Support' to contact our customer support team with the withdrawal bank account statement in pdf format which includes transaction details from 10 days earlier till the current date. Note – For GTB Bank: If the withdrawal has been successful but has not been credited to your bank account, please reach out to your bank with the session ID and allow up to 72 hours for it to reflect in your account. If the withdrawal is still not reflected after 72 hours, please click on 'Contact Support' to reach out to our customer support team. Kindly provide your withdrawal bank account statement in PDF format, including transaction details from 10 days prior to the withdrawal date up to the current date.
