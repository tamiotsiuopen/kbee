How to Play Ref's Call

Game Overview Ref's Call is an exciting, fast-paced betting game where you predict whether the referee will show a RED CARD or a YELLOW CARD in a football (soccer) match. The game revolves around a referee decision scene where you place your bet and wait for the referee's final decision. Each round is filled with suspense as you try to predict the card outcome! Game Objective Predict the Referee's Card The goal of the game is to predict whether the referee will show a RED CARD or a YELLOW CARD after a football collision scene is triggered. If you predict correctly, you win DOUBLE (2X) your bet! How to Play (Step by Step) Place Your Bet Select Stake Amount Players can select their desired bet amount by adjusting the slider between the minimum and maximum bet amounts. Alternatively, players can click on the chips to quickly place their bet. Football Collision Scene Once you place your bet, the football collision scene is triggered, adding suspense and excitement to the game. Referee's Decision After a few moments, the referee makes a decision by showing a card (or no card). This decision determines the outcome of your bet. If the referee shows the correct card (based on your prediction), you win and your payout will be DOUBLE (2X) the amount you bet. If the referee shows the opposite card or no card (Play On), you lose your bet. Winning and Losing Winning: If the referee shows the card you predicted (either RED or YELLOW), you win 2X your bet amount. Losing: If the referee shows the opposite card or no card (Play On), you lose the amount you wagered. Additional Features Bet History Players can click on the Bet History button inside the Ham Menu to view the history of bets they have placed. This allows players to track their past decisions and see how well they've been predicting the outcomes. Exit to Game Lobby Pressing the 'X' button at the top-left of the screen will exit to the Game Lobby screen, where you can explore other games or adjust your settings.
