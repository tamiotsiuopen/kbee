How to Play Fruit Hunt

Welcome to Fruit Hunt, an action-packed skill-based game where timing and precision determine your rewards. Follow this guide to understand the gameplay and how to win. Game Overview In Fruit Hunt, players launch a knife toward fruits flying across the screen. Each fruit hides a multiplier that determines the outcome of the round. Your winnings are based on the stake placed and the multiplier revealed. How to Play Select your stake by choosing a chip from the chip panel at the bottom-right corner of the screen. You may also proceed with the default chip if you prefer. Once your chip selection is finalized, align the knife by tapping one of the three available position markings. After selecting a position: Tap on the knife area or Tap on the dotted line This will launch the knife in the selected direction. The knife will slash the fruits flying above it. The first fruit hit determines the result of the round. Winning & Multipliers Each fruit contains a hidden multiplier. The multiplier is revealed once the fruit is slashed. Your win is calculated as: Win = Stake × Multiplier Example: If your stake is 10 and the revealed multiplier is 2x, you win 20.
