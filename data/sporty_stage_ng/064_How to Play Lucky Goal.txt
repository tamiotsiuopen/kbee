How to Play Lucky Goal

Objective of the Game The main objective of Lucky Goal is to predict the tile that contains a GOAL while avoiding unfavorable outcomes such as Yellow Cards and Red Cards. Possible Tile Outcomes Each tile on the board can result in one of the following outcomes: Goal Yellow Card Red Card Board Types Lucky Goal is played using three different board sizes: Small Board 5 Columns × 3 Rows Each column contains: 1 Goal 1 Yellow Card 1 Red Card Medium Board 5 Columns × 4 Rows Each column contains: 2 Goals 1 Yellow Card 1 Red Card Big Board 5 Columns × 5 Rows Each column contains: 3 Goals 1 Yellow Card 1 Red Card Stake Selection Players can select their desired stake amount by: Adjusting the slider between the minimum and maximum values, or Selecting the betting chips directly. Gameplay Rules & Mechanics With each Goal revealed in a column, the player’s cashout amount increases based on the multiplier linked to that column. On revealing the first Yellow Card, the player’s cashout amount is reduced by half. On revealing the second Yellow Card, the player’s cashout amount becomes zero, resulting in a loss. Revealing a Red Card immediately ends the round, and the player loses all cashout value. Cashout Rules Players can cash out at any time during the game, even before selecting a tile. Cashout is not allowed if the player has already received 2 Yellow Cards or 1 Red Card. Additional Features Bet History: Players can view their Lucky Goal betting history by tapping the Bet History button at the top-right of the screen. Navigation: Clicking the back arrow (<) at the top-left returns the player to the games lobby. Inactive Bets: Open bets that are not acted upon for more than 60 days will be automatically invalidated and cashed out. One-Tap Bet: Enabling One-Tap Bet removes bet confirmation pop-ups for faster gameplay.
