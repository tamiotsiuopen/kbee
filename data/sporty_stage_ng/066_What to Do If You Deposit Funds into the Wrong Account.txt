What to Do If You Deposit Funds into the Wrong Account

How to Request and Reverse Your Deposit Overview - Deposits are credited to your SportyBet account using the Bank Card or Bank Account information you provide. - It is your responsibility to ensure that all payment details entered are correct as indicated on the SportyBet site and your account. Important Note - SportyBet accepts no responsibility if you make a deposit into any account other than your SportyBet account. - SportyBet is not responsible for recovering funds deposited in error. What to Do if You Made a Deposit Error 1. If you have deposited funds into the wrong account, contact your respective financial institution. 2. Raise a dispute regarding the erroneous transaction with your bank or card provider. 3. We need a confirmation from the respective deposit bank / financial institution at nigeria.support@sportybet.com stating that the deposit transaction was made in error to the another SportyBet account. Please note that we can only reverse the amount if there is any, upon receipt of a dispute from the financial institution. Warning - Ensure you always double-check your account and payment details before making a deposit. Note - If you have tried the above solutions and they did not work, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
