Types of MMA Betting Markets

MMA Markets Overview This section describes the different types of betting markets available for Mixed Martial Arts (MMA) events. Market Types Winner - Predict which competitor will win the fight. - If the fight results in a draw, all bets will be void. 1X2 - Predict which competitor will win the fight. Over/Under - Predict whether the total number of rounds played will be over or under a specified line. Winning Method - Predict the exact method of victory in the fight. Winning Method – Overtime - Predict the exact method of victory in the fight when the match goes to overtime. Will the Fight Go the Distance - Predict if the full amount of scheduled rounds will be completed. Winner & Exact Rounds - Predict which competitor will win and the round in which the fight result will be determined.
