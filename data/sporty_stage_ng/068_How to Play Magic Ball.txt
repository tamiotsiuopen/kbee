How to Play Magic Ball

Welcome to Magic Ball, an exciting shuffling game that tests your observation and intuition. Follow the guide below to understand how the game works and how to place your bets. Game Overview The game features three cups placed in Spot 1, Spot 2, and Spot 3. A ball is placed under one of the three cups at the start of each round. The initial position of the ball is shown to the player before the game begins. How to Play At the beginning of the round, the ball is placed under one of the three cups. The game briefly shows the initial spot where the ball is located. The cups are then shuffled to mix their positions. Once the shuffling ends, players can: Select a chip value from the chip panel. Place their bet(s) on the cup they believe contains the ball. Placing Bets Choose your desired chip from the chip panel. Place one or more bets on Spot 1, Spot 2, or Spot 3. You may place multiple bets as allowed by the game rules. Winning If the ball is revealed under the cup you selected, you win according to the game’s payout rules. If the ball is not under your chosen cup, the bet is lost.
