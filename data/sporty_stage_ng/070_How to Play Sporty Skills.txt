How to Play Sporty Skills

Introduction Sporty Skills is an action-packed crash game where a football player juggles a ball in a stadium as the crowd cheers. As long as the ball stays in the air, the win coefficient increases. The moment the ball falls, the round crashes. To win, players must cash out at the right time before the ball falls. Game Objective The objective of Sporty Skills is to cash out before the ball crashes and secure winnings at the chosen coefficient. Gameplay Overview Each round begins with a football player juggling the ball. Players must place a bet before the round starts. As the player continues juggling, the coefficient increases. The round ends instantly when the ball falls. If the ball falls before the player cashes out, the bet is lost. Betting & Cash Out Place a BET before the round begins. Watch the coefficient increase as the ball is juggled. Press CASH OUT at any moment to lock in your winnings. If you do not cash out before the ball crashes, your BET is lost. Coefficient System The win coefficient starts at 1x. The coefficient increases continuously as long as the ball is being juggled. Winnings are calculated as: Your Bet × The coefficient you cashed out at (X = the coefficient where you cashed out) Minimum coefficient: 1.00x Maximum coefficient: 100,000,000.00x Multiple Bets Players can place up to two bets at a time. Each bet can be cashed out independently. Auto Bet & Auto Cash Out Auto Bet Automatically places the entered bet amount in every following round. Continues until manually turned OFF. Auto Cash Out Automatically cashes out winnings when the selected multiplier is reached. One-Tap Bet Turning on One-Tap Bet disables bet confirmation prompts. This allows faster and smoother gameplay. Free Bet Gifts Available Free Bet Gifts can be accessed via the Gift icon on the screen. If no Free Bet Gifts are available, the Gift icon will not be shown.
