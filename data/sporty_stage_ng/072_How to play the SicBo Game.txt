How to play the SicBo Game

Introduction Sic Bo is a classic dice-based casino game where players place bets on different outcomes related to the roll of three dice. The game offers a wide range of betting options—from simple Big and Small bets to more advanced combinations—making it suitable for both beginners and experienced players. Game Overview In Sic Bo, players place bets on the game board before the dice are rolled. Once all bets are confirmed, three dice are rolled simultaneously. Winnings are determined based on the total sum, specific combinations, or individual die faces shown on the dice. Types of Bets Big Wins if the total sum of the three dice is between 11 and 17. Triple dice results are excluded. Small Wins if the total sum of the three dice is between 4 and 10. Triple dice results are excluded. Three Dice Total Wins if the total sum of all three dice matches the exact number selected by the player. Two Dice Combination Wins if any two dice show the selected combination (for example, 2 & 5). Die Face Bet A single-number bet on any number from 1 to 6. The payout depends on whether the selected number appears on: One die Two dice All three dice Payout Structure Big / Small bets offer lower risk with more stable returns. Total sum bets provide higher payouts depending on how difficult the chosen outcome is. Two-dice combinations and single-die bets offer varying multipliers based on how many dice match the selected numbers. How to Play Sic Bo Tap on any betting grid on the board to begin placing your bet. Select your desired chip value to set the bet amount. Place chips on one or multiple betting options. Tap Roll Dice after completing your selections. Tap Confirm to Pay to finalize your bets. The dice are rolled, and the results are displayed instantly. Choose Rebet to repeat the same bets or place a New Bet for the next round. Game Features UNDO Removes the last chip placed. Can be used multiple times until all chips are removed.
