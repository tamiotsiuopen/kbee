How to Play Sporty6

Introduction Sporty6 is a lottery-style number game where players select numbers from a fixed pool and win based on how many of their chosen numbers match the numbers drawn by the house. Alongside standard number betting, the game also offers Special Betting options, adding more variety and strategic depth. Game Overview Sporty6 is played using numbers from 1 to 48. From these 48 numbers, the house randomly draws 35 winning numbers. Players win based on how many of their selected numbers appear among the house-drawn numbers. Additional betting options are available under Special Betting, allowing players to wager on attributes of the first drawn balls. Types of Bets Number Betting The player selects 6 different numbers out of 48. The house randomly draws 35 numbers. If all 6 selected numbers appear among the 35 drawn numbers, the player wins. The payout depends on how early the selected numbers appear in the draw sequence. Special Betting Special Betting options allow players to place bets on specific outcomes related to the first balls drawn: Sum of First Five Balls Bet on whether the sum of the first five drawn numbers will be lower or higher than 122.5. First Ball Colour Bet on the colour of the first drawn ball. There are 8 colours available to choose from. First Ball Over / Under Bet on whether the first drawn number will be lower or higher than 24.5. First Ball Even / Odd Bet on whether the first drawn number will be Even or Odd. Sporty Icons Feature Special Sporty Icons may appear within the house-drawn numbers. If the player matches one Sporty Icon, their odds are doubled (2x). If the player matches two Sporty Icons, their odds are tripled (3x). All enhanced odds remain subject to payout rules.
