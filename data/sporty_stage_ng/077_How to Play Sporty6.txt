Additional Controls Quick Pick: Automatically selects numbers. Erase: Clears the current number selection. Rebet / Rebet All: Replays bets from the previous round. One-Tap Bet: Disables bet confirmation pop-ups for faster play. Special Betting Flow Players can place multiple Special Bets individually. Each Special Bet is evaluated based on the corresponding outcome of the house draw. Wins are determined strictly by the drawn numbers. Key Notes To win in Number Betting, all six selected numbers must appear in the house draw. Earlier appearance of selected numbers leads to higher payout potential. Sporty6 combines chance-based gameplay with strategic selection through its multiple betting formats
