How to Play Climb Extreme

Gameplay Overview Each round starts with the mountaineer beginning his climb. Players must place a bet before the round starts. As the mountaineer climbs upward, the coefficient increases. The mountaineer may skid downward, causing the coefficient to decrease. If the mountaineer resumes climbing, the coefficient can increase again. The round ends when the mountaineer completely slips off the mountain. Betting & Cash Out Place a BET before the round begins. Watch the coefficient change as the mountaineer climbs or skids. Press CASH OUT at any moment to lock in your winnings. If you do not cash out before the mountaineer completely slips off the mountain, your BET is lost. Coefficient System The win coefficient starts at 1x. The coefficient: Increases as the mountaineer climbs up. Decreases when the mountaineer skids downward. Winnings are calculated as: Your Bet × The coefficient you cashed out at (X = the coefficient where you cashed out) Multiple Bets Players can place up to two bets at the same time. Each bet can be managed and cashed out independently. Auto Bet & Auto Cash Out Auto Bet Automatically places the entered bet amount in every following round. Remains active until manually turned OFF. Auto Cash Out Automatically cashes out winnings when the selected multiplier is reached. One-Tap Bet Turning on One-Tap Bet disables bet confirmation prompts. This allows for faster and smoother gameplay. Free Bet Gifts Available Free Bet Gifts can be accessed via the Gift icon on the screen. If no Free Bet Gifts are available, the Gift icon will not be shown. Players may choose to use: The full Free Bet amount, or A partial Free Bet amount. Free Bet Gifts cannot be combined with real money in a single bet. Final Notes Climb Extreme is easy to play but requires quick decision-making. The longer you wait, the higher the potential reward—and the higher the risk. A stable internet connection is recommended for the best gameplay experience.
