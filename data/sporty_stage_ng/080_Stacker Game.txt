How to Play:  Objective: Stack the blocks as high as possible to win bigger rewards! Step 1: Stop the Moving Blocks Tap the STACK button to stop the moving blocks. Timing is key—press when the blocks align perfectly. Step 2: Align Blocks to Build the Stack Keep stacking blocks on top of each other. The higher you stack, the bigger the payout! Step 3: Avoid Misalignment If you fail to stack correctly, the game ends. Precision matters—misaligned blocks will result in NO STACK. Step 4: Play Again To start another round, place bets in eligible games. Example: Place 6 more bets at NGN 100 or more in qualifying games to unlock another play.
