How to Play Scorched Fortune

 Welcome to Scorched Fortune, a cascading slot game packed with multipliers, free spins, and big win potential. This guide explains the symbols, features, and controls to help you play with confidence. Symbols Scatter Symbol The SCATTER symbol pays in any position on the reels. Scatter payouts are based on the number of symbols landed. When 4 or more SCATTER symbols land, the Free Spins Feature is triggered. Multiplier Symbols MULTIPLIER symbols can land randomly during both the main game and Free Spins. Multiplier values range randomly from 2x to 500x. When a cascading sequence ends, the values of all multiplier symbols on the reels are added together. The total win of the cascade sequence is multiplied by the combined multiplier value. Cascade Feature After each spin, all winning combinations are paid. Winning symbols disappear from the reels. Remaining symbols fall down to fill empty spaces. New symbols drop in from above. Cascades continue until no new winning combinations are formed. There is no limit to the number of possible cascades in a single spin. All wins from the cascades are added together and paid at the end of the sequence. Free Spins Feature Triggered when 4 or more SCATTER symbols land on the reels. The feature starts with 15 Free Spins. Free Spins Multipliers During Free Spins, whenever a MULTIPLIER symbol lands and the spin results in a win, its value is added to the total multiplier. For the duration of the Free Spins feature, whenever there is a win, the total accumulated multiplier is applied to the win amount. Additional Free Spins During Free Spins, landing 3 or more SCATTER symbols awards 5 additional Free Spins. How to Play Bet Selection Click the Bet Panel to open the bet options. Choose your preferred bet amount. Use the Bet Adjustment Buttons to increase or decrease your bet value. Spin Button Click Spin to start the game.
