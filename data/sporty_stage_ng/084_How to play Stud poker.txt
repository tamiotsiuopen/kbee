How the Jackpot Works The Jackpot bet is a fixed stake, separate from the Ante. The Jackpot amount is displayed above the Jackpot switch. Jackpot payouts depend on qualifying hand combinations shown in the paytable. Payouts may be: A fixed amount, or A percentage of the displayed Jackpot. Jackpot payouts are independent of the main game outcome. A player can win the Jackpot even if they lose to the dealer. Special Conditions If a Call bet is not placed for more than 60 days, the player’s Ante bets are invalidated and refunded. One-Tap Bet Feature Turning on One-Tap Bet disables bet confirmation pop-ups, allowing faster gameplay. Final Notes Stud Poker is a strategic yet simple game that rewards strong poker hands and smart decision-making. With dealer qualification rules and a Jackpot side bet, the game offers both steady wins and high-payout potential.
