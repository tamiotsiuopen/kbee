What is Potential Win?

Red (Key Warning / Critical Info): Potential Winnings are not guaranteed. The actual payout may differ if any selection is voided, canceled, or settled differently than expected. Amber (Important Considerations): The calculation includes your stake. It is based on the odds at the time the bet is placed, not updated odds after placement. Green (Definition / Positive Info): Potential Winnings refer to the maximum amount you can receive from a betslip if all selections win. This gives a clear estimate of your possible return before the bet is settled.
