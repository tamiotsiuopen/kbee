Early Goals Market Overview

🟢 GREEN – Early Win (EarlyGoals Condition Met) Your Over bet is settled as won early if the required number of goals are scored within the specified time: Over 1.5 → 1 goal scored by 10th minute (00:00–09:59) Over 2.5 → 2 goals scored by 30th minute (00:00–29:59) Over 3.5 → 3 goals scored by 50th minute (00:00–49:59) ✅ Bet is settled immediately as won ✅ Final match result does not matter ✅ Payout is locked in early 🟠 AMBER – Bet Continues (EarlyGoals Not Met Yet) If the required goals are not scored within the specified time: The bet continues normally It remains active until the match ends Settlement is based on the full-time score ⚠️ EarlyGoals advantage is no longer applicable ⚠️ Bet behaves like a standard Over/Under selection 🔴 RED – Odds Impact & Market Limitations Odds are reduced when EarlyGoals is selected (due to higher chances of early settlement) EarlyGoals is only available for: ✔ Over/Under – Full Time ❌ Not available for: First Half Over/Under Second Half Over/Under Corners Over/Under Any other Over/Under-based markets How to Enable EarlyGoals Open the Over/Under – Full Time market Select an Over option Go to the Sporty Insure section Enable EarlyGoals Place your bet
