How to View Your Current and Previous Bets

Viewing Bet History Steps to View Current and Previous Bets: 1. Log in to your account. 2. Click your account balance at the top-right corner. 3. Select Sports Bet History or Jackpot Bet History to view placed bets. 4. Select Open Bets or Bet History for previous bets. - Please note that bet history can only be accessed for the last 12 months.
