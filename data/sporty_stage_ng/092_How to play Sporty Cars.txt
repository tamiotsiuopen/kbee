How to play Sporty Cars

Game Overview Sporty Cars is a high-energy crash game powered by a multi-level crash engine. Players watch unique sports cars race ahead through multiple levels, each featuring immersive audio-visual effects and fast-paced action. As the car accelerates, the multiplier increases, and the objective is to cash out before the car speeds away (crashes). Players can place up to two bets per round, allowing for strategic play and flexible risk management. Game Goal Cash out before the car speeds away → Win Fail to cash out before the crash → Lose Your bet is multiplied by the multiplier at the moment you cash out. How to Play (Step by Step) 1. Place Your Bet(s) Choose your bet amount. You may place up to two bets per round. Press the BET button before the round starts. 2. Watch the Car Race Once the round begins, the car starts racing automatically. Each level features a unique car and enhanced audio-visual effects. The multiplier starts at 1.00x and increases as the car races forward. 3. Cash Out Press CASH OUT at any time before the car speeds away. Each active bet can be cashed out independently. Your winnings are calculated using the multiplier at the time of cash-out. Multiplier Rules The multiplier always starts at 1x. The multiplier increases continuously as the car races. The round ends when the car speeds away (crashes). The crash point is random and cannot be predicted. Win Formula Winnings = Bet Amount × Cash-Out Multiplier Bet & Cash Out Rules Bets must be placed before the round begins. You may cash out one or both bets at any time before the crash. If a bet is not cashed out before the car speeds away, that bet is lost. Winnings are paid instantly upon successful cash-out. One-Tap Bet Feature One-Tap Bet allows instant bet placement without confirmation prompts. This feature only affects bet placement and does not influence game outcomes.
