How to Enable or Disable Email 2-Factor Authentication (2FA) on SportyBet

2 Factor Authentication Overview 2-Factor Authentication (2FA) adds an extra layer of security to your SportyBet account. Email 2FA requires a verification code when logging in from an unrecognized device. How to Enable Email 2-Factor Authentication 1. Log in to your SportyBet account. 2. Tap "Me" on the bottom right of your device. 3. Select "Settings" from the top right corner. 4. Under "Safety & Security," choose "Multifactor Authentication." 5. Update your email address and validate it by clicking the verification link sent to your registered email. 6. Once your email is verified, a green tick will appear next to your email on the SportyBet site. 7. Select Email 2FA. 8. Follow the instructions to customize your settings. You can choose either or both of the following options: - Login from a new device: Requires validation every time you log in from an unrecognized device. - Login after X days: Requires validation if you have not logged in for the number of days you selected. 9. Based on your chosen settings, you will need to enter the OTP sent to your email to log in successfully. Important Notes - The 2FA code can only be sent to the email address linked to your account. - If you do not see the verification email in your inbox, check your spam folder. How to Disable Email 2FA 1. Log in to your SportyBet account and tap "Me" at the bottom right of your screen. 2. Go to Settings in the top right-hand corner. 3. Under "Safety & Security," select "Multifactor Authentication." 4. Click on Email 2-Step Verification and turn off Email 2FA. 5. A 6-digit OTP will be sent to your registered email address. 6. Enter the OTP to confirm and disable Email 2FA. Recommendation It is strongly recommended to keep Email 2FA enabled to minimize the risk of your account being compromised. Note If you have tried all the above solutions and are still experiencing issues, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
