How to Self-Deactivate Your SportyBet Account

Self-Deactivate Account If you have accidentally revealed your credentials or suspect your account is compromised, you can self-deactivate your account using 2FA. Steps to Self-Deactivate: 1. Go to the SportyBet login page. 2. Follow the instructions at the bottom of the page to begin self-deactivation. 3. An OTP will be sent to your registered mobile number. 4. Confirm the OTP to complete deactivation. Once an account is deactivated, you will not be able to create or register a new account using the same or a different mobile number. Note: If you have tried the above and are unable to self-deactivate, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
