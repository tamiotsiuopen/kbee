How to Deactivate or Reactivate Your SportyBet Account

Deactivating Your SportyBet Account Overview If you wish to deactivate your SportyBet account, you can do so using the self-deactivation process. Your choice is respected, and the steps below will guide you through the process. How to Deactivate Your Account 1. Navigate to 'My Account'. 2. Go to 'Safety & Security' and click on 'Deactivate'. 3. Enter your registered mobile number. 4. Choose the option to 'Deactivate'. 5. Specify the reason for deactivation as 'Other'. 6. Click on the 'Next' button. 7. To claim account ownership, select your preferred method to receive your OTP (One Time Password). If you want to use the Telegram option, you must have an active Telegram account linked to your phone number. Input the 6-digit OTP code received via your chosen delivery method. Result Your account will be deactivated. Please make a note that you cannot register a new account with an already registered number. How to Reactivate Your Account If you wish to reactivate your SportyBet account in the future, follow the same steps as above, but select the 'Reactivate' option. Important Notes - Once an account is created with a verified phone number, the number cannot be changed to a different one. - If you have lost your SIM, please contact your mobile network provider to request a new SIM with the same number. If you have tried the above solutions and they did not work, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
