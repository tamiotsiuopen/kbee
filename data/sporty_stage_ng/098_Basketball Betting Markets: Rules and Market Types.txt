Basketball Betting Markets: Rules and Market Types

Basketball Markets General Rules - Overtime is not considered in markets unless specifically stated. - If a match is interrupted or postponed and not continued within 48 hours after the initial kick-off date, all bets will be void. - If odds were offered with an incorrect match time (difference of more than 2 minutes), bets may be voided. - If markets remain open with an incorrect score that significantly impacts prices, bets may be voided. - If a match does not finish in a tie but overtime is played for qualification purposes, markets are settled based on the result at the end of regular time. - In the NBA and general basketball, if the score is tied at the end of regulation, a five-minute overtime period is played. Market Types and Definitions Match Winner Markets - Winner (including overtime): Predict which team will win the match, including overtime. - 1X2: Predict which team will win the match; overtime is not included. Total Points Markets - Over/Under: Predict whether the total number of points scored in regular time is over or under a specified line. - Over/Under (including overtime): Predict whether the total number of points scored in the match, including overtime, is over or under a specified line. - Home Over/Under: Predict whether the total points scored by the home team in regular time is over or under a specified line. - Home Over/Under (including overtime): Predict whether the total points scored by the home team, including overtime, is over or under a specified line. - Away Over/Under: Predict whether the total points scored by the away team in regular time is over or under a specified line. - Away Over/Under (including overtime): Predict whether the total points scored by the away team, including overtime, is over or under a specified line. - Total (over-exact-under): Predict whether the total number of points in the match will be over, under, or equal to a specified line.
