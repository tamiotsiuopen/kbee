Basketball Betting Markets: Rules and Market Types

 Handicap: Predict the winner in regular time, considering the handicap in brackets. - Handicap (including overtime): Predict the winner, considering the handicap in brackets and including overtime. Draw No Bet (DNB) Markets - DNB: Predict the winner of the match in regular time. If the teams draw, the selection is voided. Odd/Even Markets - Odd/Even: Predict whether the total number of points in regular time will be odd or even. Overtime not included. - Odd/Even (including overtime): Predict whether the total number of points in the match, including overtime, will be odd or even. Race to X Points Markets - Race to X points: Predict which team will reach X points in the match first. Overtime not included. - Race to X points (including overtime): Predict which team will reach X points in the match first, including overtime. If the match ends before X points are reached, the market is void. Winning Margin Markets - Winning margin: Predict by how many points the winning team will win the match. Overtime not included. - Winning margin (including overtime): Predict by how many points the winning team will win the match, including overtime. - Any team winning margin (including overtime): Predict by how many points the winning team will win the match, including overtime. Xth Point Markets - Xth point (including overtime): Predict which team will score the Xth point, including overtime. If the match ends before X points are reached, the market is void. Halftime/Fulltime Markets - Halftime/Fulltime: Predict the result at the end of the 1st half and the final result at regular time.
