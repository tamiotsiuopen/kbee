Why Cashout May Be Unavailable and How to Resolve It

Cashout Overview Cashout may be unavailable for your bet due to several reasons. Review the sections below to determine why cashout is not available and what actions you can take. Possible Reasons Cashout is Unavailable - The odds of your selections have been suspended or the markets are not available. - Cashout may be restricted for specific events, markets, or selections. - Your bet is a One Cut bet, which cannot be cashed out. - When using gifts, cashout will only be available with Free Bet Gift. App Version Requirements - Ensure you are using the latest version of the app, as outdated versions may affect your ability to cash out a running bet. - Android: Version 1.21.173 and above - iOS: Version 1.146.0 and above Note If you have tried the above solutions and cashout is still unavailable, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
