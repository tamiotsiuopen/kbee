How to Check Bet Limits on Casino Games

Overview Minimum and maximum bet limits vary across different casino games. These limits are set individually for each game and can depend on factors such as game type, provider, and platform settings. To ensure you're placing bets within the allowed range, it's important to check the specific limits directly within the game interface. How to Check Bet Limits 1. Open the Game Launch the casino game you wish to play via the SportyBet app or website. 2. Locate the Bet Panel Once inside the game, look for the betting interface. This is usually at the bottom or side of the screen and displays your current stake. 3. Adjust the Stake Use the plus (+) and minus (−) buttons to adjust your bet amount. As you do this, the game will automatically restrict the values to the allowed minimum and maximum. 4. Check the Limits Displayed Most games will show the minimum and maximum bet amounts either: Directly on the stake adjustment panel In the game’s information or help section (often marked with an "i" icon or a gear/settings icon) 5. Game Info Section Tap the info icon to view detailed rules and limits. This section typically includes: Minimum and maximum bet amounts Payout structure Game rules and special features Need Help? If you’re unable to find the bet limits or believe they are not displaying correctly, feel free to contact customer support for assistance.
