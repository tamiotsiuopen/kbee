What to Do If Your Casino or Virtual Bet Is Unsettled

My Casino/Virtual Bet is Still Unsettled Overview Casino and Virtual bets are usually settled instantly. For the following games, if your bet remains unsettled for more than 2 hours, further action is required: Even‑Odd, Red‑Black, Spin‑2‑Win, Spin da' Bottle, Sporty Hero, Sporty Kick, Sporty Skills, Blackjack, Sporty 6, Sporty Roulette, Sporty Soccer, Lucky Goal, SicBo, Stud Poker, SpinMatch, Rush, Ping Pong, Flip d’ Coin, Magic Ball, Pocket Rockets, Sporty Jet, Galaxy Go, The Special One. If your bet on any of these games is still unsettled after 2 hours, please follow the steps below. How to Find the Casino/Virtual Game Ticket ID 1. If you are using SportyBet on your phone browser or the app, tap on your account Balance in the top-right area. 2. Select Transactions. 3. Select the type of Transactions you wish to see more details for, in this case, Bets. 4. Click on the Bet to see the details. In the details, you will be able to find the 6-digit Ticket ID. Note If you have tried the above solutions and your issue is still not resolved, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
