Minimum Withdrawal Amount and Fees per Transaction

Minimum Withdrawal Amount Per Transaction Minimum Amount - The minimum amount you can withdraw per transaction is NGN 1.00. Fees Please note that SportyBet does not charge any fees on withdrawals. However, effective 1 January 2026, a stamp duty of 50 NGN is applicable to withdrawals of 10,000 NGN and above, which is fully covered by SportyBet. Any charges that appear on a withdrawal may originate from your bank and not from SportyBet.
