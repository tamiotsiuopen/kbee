Sporty Speed

 is a fast-paced crash game where your motorbike races down the highway while the win multiplier keeps climbing. The longer you stay in the race, the higher your potential win - but cash out before a speed camera catches you! 🚦 Game Objective Cash out before the speed camera flashes to win your bet multiplied by the coefficient at the moment you cash out. 🕹️ How to Play (3 Easy Steps) Place Your Bet Choose your bet amount and place it before the round begins. Watch the Speed Increase Your motorbike accelerates down the road as the multiplier (coefficient) increases. Every checkpoint upgrades your ride into faster motorbikes. Cash Out in Time Press CASH OUT anytime before a speed camera flashes to win: Winnings = Bet × Cashout Coefficient ⚠️ If you don’t cash out before the camera flashes, your bet is lost. 📈 Coefficients Explained The win coefficient starts at 1.00x. It increases continuously as the round progresses. Your bike evolves through three faster rides as the multiplier climbs. Your final payout depends on when you cash out. 💰 Betting & Cashing Out Select your bet amount and press BET to enter the round. Press CASH OUT at any time during the race to secure your winnings. If the speed camera flashes before you cash out, the bet is lost. 🤖 Auto Bet Auto Bet automatically places your selected bet amount in every new round. It remains active until you manually turn it OFF. ⏱️ Auto Cashout Auto Cashout automatically cashes out your bet once your chosen multiplier is reached. Helpful if you want consistent exits without manual timing. 👆 One-Tap Bet Turning on One-Tap Bet removes bet confirmation pop-ups. Place bets instantly with a single tap. 🎁 Free Bet Gifts Check available Free Bet Gifts by clicking the Gift icon on the screen. If no gifts are available, the icon will not appear. You can use: The full gift amount, or A partial gift amount for your bet. Free Bet Gifts cannot be combined with real money.
