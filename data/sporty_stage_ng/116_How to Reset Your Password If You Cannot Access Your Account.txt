How to Reset Your Password If You Cannot Access Your Account

 If you forgot your password or see the message 'The credentials are invalid. Please try again', use the 'Forgot Password?' option to reset your password. How to Reset Your Password 1. Enter your registered phone number and request a One-Time Password (OTP). 2. Select your preferred method to receive the OTP from the options shown on the screen. To use the Telegram option, you must have an active Telegram account linked to your phone number. Enter the 6-digit OTP received via your chosen method and set a new password which must be at least 8 characters long and must contain at least one upper case letter, one lower case letter and one number. After Resetting Your Password - You will be logged out of all devices previously logged into your account. - Use your new password to log in to your account.
