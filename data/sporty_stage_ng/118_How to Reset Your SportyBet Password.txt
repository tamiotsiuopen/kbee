How to Reset Your SportyBet Password

How to Reset Your Password Overview This guide explains how to reset your password for your SportyBet account. Steps to Reset Your Password 1. Log in to your SportyBet account and go to the My Account drop-down menu. 2. Click on Settings next to the username at the top of the screen. 3. You will be redirected to My Account Info. Click on Change Password. 4. Your password must be at least 8 characters long and must contain at least one upper case letter, one lower case letter and one number.
