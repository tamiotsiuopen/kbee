Sporty Hero VIP Mode

1. Overview Sporty Hero VIP introduces exclusive gameplay features and contests designed to reward high-stake and consistent players. VIP benefits include special protection features, turbo gameplay advantages, and leaderboard-based contests. These features are governed by eligibility rules and terms defined by Sporty. 2. VIP Mode What is VIP Mode? VIP Mode is a premium gameplay mode in Sporty Hero that unlocks exclusive features and benefits for eligible users. VIP Eligibility Criteria Eligibility is calculated based on the user’s stake record from the previous month. A minimum monthly stake threshold is required to qualify for VIP Mode. VIP Downgrade (Relegation Rules) If a user’s monthly stake falls below the required VIP limit, the user will be: Relegated to Normal Game Mode All VIP benefits will stop immediately VIP benefits resume only after the user becomes eligible again. Sporty’s Rights SportyBet reserves the right to: Change, Limit, Pause & End VIP benefits at any time without prior notice. Important Notes VIP status is not permanent Eligibility is monthly and stake-based Benefits are conditional and revocable 3. StakeSafe Feature What is StakeSafe? StakeSafe is a bet protection feature that allows users to recover part of their stake if the game crashes before a predefined multiplier. How to Use StakeSafe Tap “StakeSafe” before placing a bet Place your bet with StakeSafe enabled The bet is now protected StakeSafe Crash Protection If Sporty Hero crashes before a specific multiplier The user receives up to N% of their stake back as FBGs (Free Bet Gifts) To locate these values, click the three-line (hamburger) menu and select "VIP details”. StakeSafe Restrictions Auto cashout is disabled when StakeSafe is applied Stake limit: Stake must be ≤ 2× the user’s historical average stake per bet Applies only to StakeSafe-enabled bets Important Notes StakeSafe ≠ guaranteed win Refund is in FBGs, not cash Auto cashout is intentionally disabled 4.
