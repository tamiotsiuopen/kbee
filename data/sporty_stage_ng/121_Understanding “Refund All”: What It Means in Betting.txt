Understanding “Refund All”: What It Means in Betting

What is Refund All? 🟢 Green (What it means) Refund All means the entire stake placed on a bet is returned to the user. The bet is treated as void, with odds settled at 1.00, resulting in no win or loss. 🟠 Amber (When it applies) Refund All may be applied in situations such as match cancellations, errors, or other scenarios defined in SportyBet’s settlement rules. 🔴 Red (Important notice) SportyBet reserves the right to void any bet and apply a Refund All settlement in accordance with its Terms & Conditions.
