How to View Date of Birth (DOB)

 Overview This guide explains how users can view their Date of Birth (DOB) on SportyBet. How to View DOB Users can view their Date of Birth (DOB) via: Mobile App WAP Viewing DOB on the Mobile App and WAP Tap ME on the bottom right of the homepage. On the Profile page, click your Profile Name at the top right. Your DOB will be displayed in the DOB section on the Profile Page. Viewing DOB on Website Select My Account located at the top right corner of the page. Click My Account Info to view your Date of Birth (DOB). Important Notes DOB details are displayed on the Profile Page.
