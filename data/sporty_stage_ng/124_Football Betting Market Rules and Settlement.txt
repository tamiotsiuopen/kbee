Next Goalscorer - Own goals are not considered and are ignored for settlement. - All players who took part in the match since kickoff or previous goal are considered as runners. - If an unlisted player scores, all bets on listed players stand. - Settlement is based on TV insert and Press Association statistics unless clearly incorrect. Interval Markets - Settled based on the goal time announced by TV, or match clock if unavailable. - Goal markets: time ball crosses line. - Corner interval: time corner is taken. - Booking interval: time card is shown. - Offsides and penalties: time referee gives the decision (applies to VAR situations). - Penalties awarded but not taken are not considered. - For 5/10/15 minute interval markets, events in added injury or stoppage time are included. Next Scoring Type - Freekick: Must be scored directly from freekick or corner; deflections count if the taker is awarded the goal. - Penalty: Must be scored directly from the penalty; rebounds do not count. - Own Goal: If declared as an own goal. - Header: Last touch with the head. - Shot: Any other part of the body, excluding above types. - No Goal. Main Market Types - 1X2: Predict which team will win (overtime not included). - Double Chance: Predict match result combining two of three outcomes (1X, X2, or 12). - Draw No Bet: Predict the winner; if draw, selection is voided. - Home/Away No Bet: Predict result; if Home/Away wins, selection is voided. - Handicap/Asian Handicap: Predict winner considering the handicap. Quarter, half, and whole ball lines have specific settlement rules for win/loss/void/half-win/half-loss. - Over/Under: Predict if total goals are over/under a line.
