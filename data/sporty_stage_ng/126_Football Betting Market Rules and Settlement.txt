Corner Markets - Corners 1X2: Predict which team will get more corners. - Nth/Last Corner: Predict which team will get the Nth or last corner. - Corner Handicap: Predict corners considering handicap. - Corners Over/Under: Predict if total corners are over/under a line. - Home/Away Team Corners Over/Under/Range: Predict corners for each team. - Odd/Even Corners: Predict if total corners are odd or even (0 = even). - 1st Half and 5/10/15 minute interval versions of all above markets are available. - Race to X Corners: Predict which team will reach X corners first. Other Specialized Markets - Rest of Match Total Goals/Asian Handicap: Settled based on goals or results after bet placement, disregarding prior goals. The current score at bet placement is displayed in the market title and treated as 0:0 for settlement. - Player to be Booked: Bet on whether a specific player will receive a yellow or red card. Special Information and Notes - Some Russian Winter Championship matches last 60 minutes; halftime is at 30 minutes, full time at 60 minutes. - Bookings only include in-play players (not substitutes or coaches). - All bets are void if the match is interrupted and not continued within 48 hours. If resumed within 48 hours, bets are settled per final result.
