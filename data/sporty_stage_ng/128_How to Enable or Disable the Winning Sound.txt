How to Enable or Disable the Winning Sound

 - When your bet is successful, a Winning Pop-Up will appear on the screen along with celebratory music to enhance the experience. How to Control the Music If you’d like to mute the music, click the volume icon located in the top-left corner of the winning pop-up. To turn the music back on, simply click the volume icon again. This gives you full control over the sound so you can enjoy the win your way 🎉
