I want to delete my SportyBet account

Please note that a number once registered to open a SportyBet account cannot be deleted. However, you may deactivate your SportyBet account. To permanently restrict your account, you are requested to confirm the below details for verification purposes - 1) Last Deposit Amount and Date 2) Last Withdrawal Amount and Date 3) Last Stake Amount and Date 4) The first and last 4 digits of the card used for deposit 5) Current SportyBet wallet balance
