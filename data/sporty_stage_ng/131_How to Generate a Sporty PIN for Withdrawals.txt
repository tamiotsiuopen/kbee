How to Generate a Sporty PIN for Withdrawals

How to Generate a Sporty PIN Overview A Sporty PIN is required for withdrawal requests. Follow the steps below to generate your Sporty PIN. Steps to Generate a Sporty PIN 1. Log in to your account. 2. Click on your Account's Balance in the top-right corner or select the "Me" tab at the bottom right. 3. Click the settings icon on the top right. 4. Under the section "Safety & Security," select "Multifactor Authentication." 5. Select the Sporty PIN option to create your PIN. 6. Enter the PIN of your choice and confirm to successfully create your Sporty PIN. Important Notes - Before every withdrawal request, you will be prompted for your Sporty PIN. - Failure to input the correct PIN three consecutive times will lock your PIN, and you will not be able to place any withdrawal requests for one hour. If you have forgotten your PIN and the above solutions have been tried and did not work, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
