How to Keep Your SportyBet Account Secure

Keeping your SportyBet Account Safe Overview SportyBet is committed to the security of your account and continuously develops new features to enhance account safety. Sharing Your Account Information - Do not allow anyone else to access your account information. - SportyBet and its employees will never ask for your password, OTP, or Sporty PIN. - Only enter your password, OTP, or Sporty PIN on the official SportyBet app or website. Phishing Sites If you receive any email, SMS, or other communication requesting your password or Sporty PIN, forward it to the official support email (nigeria.support@sportybet.com) and delete the message. Technology SportyBet encrypts your information using the latest industry-accepted protocols. Tips for Account Safety - For additional tips on safeguarding your account, refer to the official guidance.
