How to Log Out of Other Devices on SportyBet

 Overview SportyBet provides a Device Management feature that allows users to monitor and manage all devices currently logged into their account. This feature enhances account security and gives users control over their active sessions. Steps to Log Out of Other Devices 1. Open the SportyBet app. 2. Go to the ME section. 3. Tap the settings icon located at the top right corner of the screen. 4. Select Device Management from the settings menu. 5. A list of all devices currently logged into your account will appear. 6. Tap on the device(s) you want to log out. 7. Click Confirm to log out the selected device(s). Result Once confirmed, the chosen devices will be immediately logged out, ensuring your account remains secure and accessible only from trusted devices. Note To access the Device Management feature, ensure your SportyBet app is updated to the latest version. If the above solutions have been tried and did not work, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
