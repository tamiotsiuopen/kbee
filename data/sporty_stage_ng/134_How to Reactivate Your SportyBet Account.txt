How to Reactivate Your SportyBet Account

Reactivating Your SportyBet Account Overview The process to reactivate your SportyBet account is quick and straightforward. You can use the SportyBet app or website to complete the reactivation. Steps to Reactivate Your Account 1. Open the SportyBet app or website. 2. Enter your registered mobile number. 3. Choose the option to 'Reactivate'. 4. Click on the 'Next' button. 5. To claim account ownership, select your preferred method from the options shown on the screen to receive your OTP (One Time Password). Note: To use the Telegram option, you must have an active Telegram account linked to your phone number. 6. Enter the 6-digit OTP code you received via your selected method. 7. After completing the verification process, you will be prompted to reset your password and SportyPIN for added security. 8. Your account will be successfully reactivated. Important Notes - Once an account is created with a verified phone number, it cannot be changed to a different number. - If you have lost your SIM, please contact your mobile network provider to request a new SIM with the same number. - If you have self-excluded your account, you must wait until the self-exclusion period has ended. Your account cannot be reactivated until this period is over. If you have tried the above solutions and still cannot reactivate your account, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
