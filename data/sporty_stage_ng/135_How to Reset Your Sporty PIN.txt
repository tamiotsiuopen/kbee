How to Reset Your Sporty PIN

How to Reset a Sporty PIN Overview This guide explains the steps required to reset your Sporty PIN for account security and withdrawals. Steps to Reset Your Sporty PIN 1. Log in to your account. 2. Click on your Account's Balance in the top-right corner, or tap the 'Me' tab at the bottom right. 3. Click the settings icon on the top right. 4. Under the 'Safety & Security' section, select 'Multifactor Authentication.' 5. Tap on 'Sporty PIN.' 6. Click 'Forgot Pin?' to start the reset process. 7. To verify your mobile number, select your preferred method from the options shown on the screen to receive your OTP (One-Time Password). Note: To use the Telegram option, you must have an active Telegram account linked to your phone number. 8. Enter the 6-digit OTP received via your chosen method. 9. Enter your new PIN and confirm to complete the reset process. Important Notes - You can only request a limited number of verification codes per day. The limit will be displayed on the OTP entry screen. - It is recommended to always protect your withdrawals with a Sporty PIN. If you have tried the above solutions and they did not work, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
