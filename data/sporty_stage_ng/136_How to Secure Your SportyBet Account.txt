How to Secure Your SportyBet Account

How Can I Secure My SportyBet Account? Recommended Security Measures - Do not share your username or password with anyone, including friends or family. - Never share personal details such as card number, CVV, or OTP with anyone, even if they claim to be a bank employee. - Create a strong password by following the password rules during registration. - Avoid using public WiFi for any transactions related to your account. - Only log in to your SportyBet account from your own devices. - Be cautious about sharing personal information on social media, especially details like your date of birth or information banks use to verify accounts or reset passwords. - Always lock your phone using fingerprint recognition, a secure PIN (not your birthday), or a unique gesture. - Be aware of how to wipe your devices if they are lost or stolen. - Never log in to your SportyBet account through suspicious links. The correct URL is sportybet.com/ng. Handling Suspicious Communications - If you receive any email or SMS requesting your confidential login details, forward the message to the official support email and delete it immediately. Note: If you have tried the above solutions and still have security concerns, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
