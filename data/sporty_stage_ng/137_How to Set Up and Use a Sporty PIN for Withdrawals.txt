How to Set Up and Use a Sporty PIN for Withdrawals

Setting up a Sporty PIN Overview A Sporty PIN is a 4-digit code that provides an advanced level of security for withdrawal transactions. This PIN is required before making any withdrawal request. How to Set Up a Sporty PIN 1. Log in to your account. 2. Click on the 'Me' icon located at the bottom-right corner of the screen. 3. Click on the settings icon at the top-right corner of the screen. 4. You will be redirected to the Settings page. Select the multifactor authentication option. 5. Choose your preferred Sporty PIN and confirm to create it successfully. Sporty PIN Usage - You will be prompted to enter your Sporty PIN before every withdrawal request. PIN Lockout Policy - If you enter the incorrect PIN three consecutive times, your PIN will be locked and you will not be able to place any withdrawal requests for one hour.
