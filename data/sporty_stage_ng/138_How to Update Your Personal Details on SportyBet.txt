How to Update Your Personal Details on SportyBet

How to Update Your Personal Details Overview This section provides instructions for updating your personal details on your SportyBet account. Procedure 1. Log in to your SportyBet account. 2. Click on your Account Balance in the top-right corner of the page. 3. Click on your username at the top of the screen. 4. You will be redirected to your Profile page, where you can change your profile details as desired.
