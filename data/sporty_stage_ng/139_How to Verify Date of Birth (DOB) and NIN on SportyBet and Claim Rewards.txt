How to Verify Date of Birth (DOB) and NIN on SportyBet and Claim Rewards

SportyBet Date of Birth (DOB) Verification via NIN – Error Handling & Next Steps Overview This guide explains how to verify your Date of Birth (DOB) and National Identification Number (NIN) on SportyBet, the rewards available after successful verification, and how to resolve common verification errors. Verification Process - Users can verify their DOB and NIN through: - Profile Page - Sporty Loyalty Page Rewards Upon Successful Verification - Immediate gift is credited to the Gift section if DOB is verified via NIN. - Birthday gift is automatically credited on the user's birth date (only if DOB is verified via NIN). - Users with a Sporty Loyalty tier of Copper or higher receive a special loyalty gift. Important Notes - The Birthday Gift Program is active only for users who verify DOB via NIN. - Gifts appear in the Gift section of the user’s account. - You cannot verify your NIN on multiple SportyBet accounts. Once NIN is verified on one account, it cannot be delinked.
