How to Verify Date of Birth (DOB) via NIN and Receive Rewards

Verification and Rewards Verification Process - Users can verify their Date of Birth (DOB) and National Identification Number (NIN) via: - Profile Page - Sporty Loyalty Page Rewards Upon Successful Verification - Immediate gift is credited to the Gift section if DOB is verified via NIN. - Birthday gift is automatically credited on the user's birth date (only if DOB is verified via NIN). - Users with a Sporty Loyalty tier of Copper or higher receive a special loyalty gift. Important Notes - The Birthday Gift Program is active only for users who verify DOB via NIN. - Gifts appear in the Gift section of the user’s account.
