How to Verify Your Date of Birth (DOB) and NIN on SportyBet

How to Verify DOB Overview This guide explains how to verify your Date of Birth (DOB) and National Identification Number (NIN) on SportyBet. Step-by-Step Instructions 1. Go to your Profile Page or the Sporty Loyalty Page. 2. Locate the DOB/NIN verification section. 3. Follow the prompts to submit your NIN for automatic verification of your Date of Birth. What Happens After Successful Verification - You will receive a verification gift if your loyalty tier is Copper or higher. - You will receive a birthday gift on your actual birth date. - All gifts will appear in the Gift section of your account. If You Encounter an Error During Verification If you receive an error such as: - The Verification Service is currently unavailable - Too many attempts Required Information for Manual Verification If you have tried the above solutions and did not succeed, the case should be handed off to a human agent. The following information will be needed: - A clear copy of your NIN card showing your Date of Birth (DOB), NIN number, and Full Name. - A screenshot of the error message received during the verification attempt. - Any other government-issued ID that includes your Date of Birth (DOB) and Full Name. - Your exact Date of Birth as registered. Summary To verify your Date of Birth and NIN, go to your Profile Page or Sporty Loyalty Page and follow the steps to submit your NIN. If you experience issues, provide the required documents for manual verification by a human agent.
