KYC (Know Your Customer): Definition and Purpose

What is KYC? Definition of KYC KYC stands for Know Your Customer. It is a process used to positively identify customers in order to comply with regulatory requirements and to help ensure the security of accounts. Purpose of KYC - To comply with legal and regulatory requirements. - To enhance the security of customer accounts. KYC Process - Customers may be asked to provide identification documents as part of the KYC process.
