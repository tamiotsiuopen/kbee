OTP Request Limits and Troubleshooting

OTP (One-Time Password) Limits and Troubleshooting Daily OTP Request Limit - The daily request limit for OTP via different methods applies. - Wait 24 hours for the OTP limit to reset. Troubleshooting OTP Issues For Telegram OTPs: - Ensure you are logged into an active Telegram account before selecting Telegram OTP. - Your SportyBet and Telegram numbers must match. - If the Telegram option does not appear with an active account, hand off the case to a human agent if the above solutions have been tried and did not work. For SMS and Voice OTPs: 1. Restart your device. 2. Ensure you are in a good network coverage area. 3. Make sure your SMS inbox has sufficient space. 4. Ensure you do not have an active DND (Do Not Disturb) service on your number. 5. If you still cannot receive OTPs, try inserting your SIM into another device or contact your network operator.
