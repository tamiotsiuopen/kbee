Preventing Underage Gambling on SportyBet

Restriction of Minors Legal Requirements - It is against the law for individuals under the age of 18 to gamble. - SportyBet enforces strict measures to prevent underage gambling. Account Registration and Verification - Individuals under 18 years of age are prohibited from creating an account or gambling on SportyBet. - During registration, users must tick a box confirming they are over 18 years old. Registration is rejected if this confirmation is not provided. - Users must provide detailed personal information, including name, date of birth, and address, to verify age. - Payment services on SportyBet require users to be at least 18 years old and to have a valid national ID card. Measures to Prevent Underage Gambling - Do not share account details such as username or password with others. - Lock your computer with a password, especially if using a public or shared device. - Educate children about the risks associated with gambling. Internet Filtering and Monitoring Tools - Net Nanny (www.netnanny.com): General web content blocking software, available for iOS and Android devices. - CYBERsitter (www.cybersitter.com): Filtering software that allows parents to choose which websites to block. - GamBlock (www.gamblock.com): Software specifically for blocking gambling websites, also available for Android devices. Note If all of the above solutions have been tried and did not work, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
