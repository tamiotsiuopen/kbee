Required Documents for KYC Verification

What Documents Are Required for KYC? Identity Verification Requirements - You may be asked to provide a copy of a photographic ID. Acceptable documents include: - Passport - Government-issued ID card - Driving license - The identification document must clearly display your full name, date of birth, and a valid expiry date. - If the expiry date is located on the back of the document, include a copy of the back as well. Residential Address Verification - You may be asked to provide a document confirming your residential address. Acceptable documents include: - Utility bill Source of Funds Verification - To verify the source of funds used for betting, you may be required to provide additional information and documentation. This can include: - Details of your occupation - Salary information - Relevant supporting documents as determined by the SportyBet team - Employment details may be required, but your employer will not be contacted as part of these checks. Enhanced Verification Process - You will be informed about the specific information or documentation required as part of the Enhanced Verification process. Note: If you have tried all the above solutions and your issue is not resolved, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
