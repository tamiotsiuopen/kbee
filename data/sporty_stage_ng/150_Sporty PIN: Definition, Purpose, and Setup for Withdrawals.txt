Sporty PIN: Definition, Purpose, and Setup for Withdrawals

Sporty PIN Overview Definition - A Sporty PIN is a 4-digit personal identification number used to enhance the security of withdrawal transactions on SportyBet. Purpose - The Sporty PIN provides an advanced level of security for your account, specifically for withdrawals. How to Use - The Sporty PIN must be entered every time you make a withdrawal request. How to Generate a Sporty PIN 1. Go to your account settings. 2. Follow the prompts to create your 4-digit Sporty PIN. Note - The Sporty PIN is required for all withdrawal requests to ensure the safety of your funds.
