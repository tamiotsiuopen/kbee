SportyBet Policy on Opening, Closing, and Duplicate Accounts

Account Policy on Opening and Closing Accounts Overview - SportyBet allows only one account per customer. - Registering a new account after self-exclusion or deactivation of the primary account, without acceptable reasons, is considered creating a duplicate or multiple accounts. Consequences of Duplicate or Multiple Accounts - Owning duplicate or multiple SportyBet accounts may result in the closure of all such accounts. - Any funds in duplicate or multiple accounts may be withheld in accordance with SportyBet policies. Important Reference - For more details, refer to SportyBet’s Terms and Conditions, under 'Your SportyBet Account', Clause 1.6. Recommendation - Users are urged to use only their primary account. Note - If you have tried the above solutions and are still experiencing issues accessing your primary account, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
