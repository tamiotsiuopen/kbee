SportyBet Single Account Policy

SportyBet Account Policy Single Account Requirement - Each customer is allowed to have only one account with SportyBet at any given time. Consequences of Multiple Accounts - Having multiple accounts is considered a breach of SportyBet's Terms and Conditions. - Multiple accounts will be treated as fraud. Regulatory and Security Reasons - This policy is in place to meet regulatory requirements and to ensure the safety and security of all customer accounts and the SportyBet betting platform.
