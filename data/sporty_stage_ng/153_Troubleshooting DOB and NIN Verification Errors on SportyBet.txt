Troubleshooting DOB and NIN Verification Errors on SportyBet

Error Handling and Next Steps Temporary Issues (Amber Status) Error Message: “The Verification Service is currently unavailable. Please try again later or contact support for assistance.” - Action Required: - Wait 24 hours from your last attempt before retrying. Too Many Attempts - If you see a message about too many attempts, you have exceeded the allowed verification tries. - To resolve, prepare and submit the following: - A clear copy of your NIN card showing: - Date of Birth (DOB) - NIN number - Full Name - Screenshot of the error message received. - Another government-issued ID showing: - DOB - Full Name - Your exact registered Date of Birth. - After submitting the required documents, expect a resolution within 24 hours.
