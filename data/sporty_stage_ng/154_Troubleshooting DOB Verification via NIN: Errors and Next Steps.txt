Troubleshooting DOB Verification via NIN: Errors and Next Steps

Temporary Issues and Next Steps Error Message - The Verification Service is currently unavailable. Please try again later. Action Required - Wait 24 hours from your last attempt before retrying. Too Many Attempts - If you see a message about too many attempts, you have exceeded the allowed verification tries. If the above solutions have been tried and did not work, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
