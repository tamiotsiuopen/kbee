Troubleshooting Registration Issues on SportyBet

Why am I unable to complete the registration process with SportyBet? Overview This guide provides a checklist to help resolve common issues encountered during the SportyBet registration process. Phone Number Format - Ensure your phone number is exactly 10 digits long. - Do not include '0' at the start of your number. Error Message: The mobile number entered is invalid or has already been registered - This message indicates your phone number is already linked to an existing SportyBet account. - Try to log in, or use the Forgot Password option to reset your password. Not Receiving the OTP (One-Time Password)? - Check that your mobile network signal is stable. - Switch between Wi-Fi and mobile data to check for better connection quality. - Ensure Airplane Mode is turned off. - Check if SportyBet messages have been marked as spam or blocked on your device. - Ensure the Telegram App is installed and logged in with the same number as SportyBet before requesting OTP. Still Unable to Register? - Clear your browser cache. - Switch to a different browser or device. - Restart your phone and try the registration process again. Note If the above solutions have been tried and did not work, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
