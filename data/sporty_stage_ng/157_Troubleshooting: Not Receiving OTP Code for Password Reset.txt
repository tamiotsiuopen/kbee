Troubleshooting: Not Receiving OTP Code for Password Reset

Unable to get a code to reset my password Overview OTP codes for password resets are usually received instantly. However, delays can occur. If you do not receive your OTP code, follow the troubleshooting steps below. General Troubleshooting Steps - Wait for the countdown timer on the screen to finish before requesting another OTP. - Try requesting the OTP using an alternative method if available. Troubleshooting for SMS and Voice OTP 1. Restart your device and try again. 2. Ensure your phone number is active and has good network coverage. 3. Check that your SMS inbox is not full. 4. Insert your SIM card into another phone and try again. Troubleshooting for Telegram OTP 1. Make sure you have an active Telegram account. 2. Ensure you are logged into the Telegram account linked to your SportyBet account before requesting an OTP. 3. Confirm that you are connected to a stable internet connection. Verification Code Request Limits - There are daily limits on the number of verification code requests per channel. The limit will be displayed on the OTP entry screen. Important Notes - You will not receive SMS OTPs on your email for withdrawals or password resets. Only 2FA codes are sent to your email. If you have tried all the above solutions and are still unable to receive the OTP code, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
