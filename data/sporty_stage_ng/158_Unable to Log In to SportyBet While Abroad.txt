Unable to Log In to SportyBet While Abroad

Unable to Login from Abroad Overview If you are unable to log in to SportyBet while abroad, it may be due to restrictions based on your current location. Permitted Countries - SportyBet only accepts customers from the following countries: - Nigeria - Ghana - Zambia - Tanzania - Brazil - South Africa Guidance - If you are visiting from a country not listed above, you may not be able to access your account due to local laws and platform restrictions. - Check the local laws regarding online betting in your current location. Note If you are in one of the permitted countries and still cannot log in, and the above solutions have been tried and did not work, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
