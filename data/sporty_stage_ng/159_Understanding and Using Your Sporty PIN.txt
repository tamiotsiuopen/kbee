Understanding and Using Your Sporty PIN

Sporty PIN Definition: - The Sporty PIN is a 4-digit code set up to assist with withdrawals and deposits. - For instructions on setting up your Sporty PIN, refer to the official guidance. Usage: - Withdrawals: Every withdrawal requires confirmation using your Sporty PIN. - Deposits: All card deposits require your Sporty PIN to complete the transaction. Warning: Do not share your Sporty PIN with anyone. SportyBet and its employees will never ask for your Sporty PIN.
