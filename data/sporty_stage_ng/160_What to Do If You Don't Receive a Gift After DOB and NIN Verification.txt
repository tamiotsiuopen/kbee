What to Do If You Don't Receive a Gift After DOB and NIN Verification

Gift Not Received (Red Status) Common Issues and Solutions - “Today is my birthday and I didn’t receive a gift.” - If DOB is not verified via NIN, no birthday gift can be issued. Verify DOB via NIN to activate birthday rewards. - “I have verified my DOB but didn’t receive a gift.” - If verified via NIN, check the Gift section. If missing, the case should be handed off to a human agent. - If verified via another method (e.g., BVN), NIN verification is required for gifts. - “I have not received any gift.” - Check that DOB is verified via NIN. - Ensure your Sporty Loyalty tier is Copper or higher. - Check the Gift section of your account. - “How can I get my birthday gift?” - Verify DOB via NIN. The gift will be auto-credited on your birthday. - Loyalty gift also applies if your tier is Copper or higher. - “I want to use it, but it’s requesting NIN.” - Birthday Gift under Sporty Loyalty requires DOB verification via NIN, even if BVN is already verified. Warning: You cannot verify your NIN on multiple SportyBet accounts, and once it is verified on one account, it cannot be delinked. Note: If the above solutions have been tried and did not work, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
