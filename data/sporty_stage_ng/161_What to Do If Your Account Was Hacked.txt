What to Do If Your Account Was Hacked

My Account Was Hacked Overview If you believe someone has accessed your account without your consent, it is important to act quickly to secure your account. Recommended Actions 1. Attempt to change your password immediately if you still have access to your account. 2. Check your account for any unauthorized changes or activity. 3. Review your account recovery options and update them if needed. Identity Verification Process If you are unable to secure your account or regain access, an identity verification process will be required to proceed with account recovery. Note If you have tried the above solutions and your issue is not resolved, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
