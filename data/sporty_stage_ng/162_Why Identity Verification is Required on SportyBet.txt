Why Identity Verification is Required on SportyBet

Why You Need to Verify Your Identity Purpose of Identity Verification - Identity verification is required to ensure that gambling is fair and open. - It helps prevent gambling from being linked to crime or activities that harm, damage, or disrupt society, orderly conduct, or individuals. - These steps are necessary to meet regulatory requirements at all times. When Verification is Needed - You may be asked to confirm your identity and demonstrate that you have sufficient funds to support your gambling activity. Account Restrictions - If requested information or documentation is not provided, your SportyBet account may be subject to certain restrictions until you satisfy these checks.
