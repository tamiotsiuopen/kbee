Why You Did Not Receive a Gift After DOB Verification via NIN

Gift Not Received Common Issues and Solutions - If today is your birthday and you did not receive a gift: - If DOB is not verified via NIN, no birthday gift can be issued. - Verify DOB via NIN to activate birthday rewards. - If you have verified your DOB but did not receive a gift: - If verified via NIN, check the Gift section of your account. If missing, escalate to a human agent. - If verified via another method (such as BVN), NIN verification is required for gifts. - If you have not received any gift: - Check that DOB is verified via NIN. - Check that your Sporty Loyalty tier is Copper or higher. - Check the Gift section of your account. - To receive your birthday gift: - Verify DOB via NIN. - The gift will be auto-credited on your birthday. - Loyalty gift also applies if your tier is Copper or higher. - If you want to use a gift but it is requesting NIN: - Birthday Gift under Sporty Loyalty requires DOB verification via NIN, even if BVN is already verified.
