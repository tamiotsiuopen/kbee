Bet Types Explained: Single, Multiple, and System Bets

Bet Types Explained Single Bet - The stake you enter applies to each individual selection. - If you choose multiple events and selections, your stake is placed separately for each one. Multiple Bet - You enter a single stake for all your selections combined. - The potential win is calculated by multiplying the odds of all your selections. - You can only win the bet if all your selections are won. System Bet - Offers more flexibility than Single or Multiple bets. - You can place your bet amount on all selections by entering the stake in 'Play All,' or choose different stakes for combinations such as Singles, Doubles, Trebles, etc. - To win Singles, at least one of your single events must win. - To win Doubles, any two combinations of your selections must win. - To win Trebles, any three combinations of your selections must win.
