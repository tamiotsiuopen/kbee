How AnyWin Bets Work: Rules, Usage, and Benefits

What is AnyWin? Overview AnyWin is a betting feature that allows you to win a fixed payout if any one of your selections wins, regardless of the outcome of your other selections. This feature is designed to provide quicker results and simplify multi-bet betting. Key Requirements and Restrictions - Minimum Selections: You must choose between 2 and 10 selections from different events. - Minimum Odds: Each selection must have odds greater than 1.99. - Bet Type: AnyWin is only available via the Multiple Bets tab as a single multi-bet. - Fixed Payout: The payout amount is locked in as soon as any selection wins. Additional winners do not increase the payout. - Void Selections: If any selections are voided and this affects the win condition, the payout may be recalculated. In rare cases, the payout may be lower than the original stake. - Fraud Prevention: SportyBet reserves the right to revoke winnings if misuse is suspected. Usage Guidelines How to Use AnyWin 1. Select two or more events and add them to your betslip. 2. Enable AnyWin by checking the appropriate box. 3. Confirm and place your bet. Market Types - AnyWin works for both Pre-Match and Live Betting. Cashout - Cashout is supported on AnyWin bets. Winning Condition - Your bet wins as soon as one of your selections is correct, regardless of the total odds. Benefits of AnyWin - Early Payouts: Receive your payout as soon as one selection wins. - Simplified Betting: Only one pick needs to win for a payout. - Exciting and Fast: Enjoy the thrill of multi-bets with quicker results.
