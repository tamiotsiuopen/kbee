How Does the 1UP Early Payout Feature Work?

1UP Overview Definition 1UP offers an early payout if your team leads by 1 goal at any point in the match, regardless of the final result. This applies whether your team wins, loses, draws, or if the match is abandoned. The feature is designed to provide fast payouts and allow you to place new bets without waiting for the match to finish. Availability - 1UP is available on select football leagues. - It is only applicable to pre-match bets in the dedicated 1X2 - 1UP market. - 1UP can also be selected through the Insure section of your Betslip. How 1UP Works in Multiple Bets - In a multiple bet, if your selected team leads by one goal, that selection is considered a win. - The outcomes of your other selections in the multiple bet remain subject to the results of those matches. Steps to Create a 1UP Bet 1. Choose the match or matches you want to bet on, focusing on the 1UP market. 2. Below the stake amount field, go to the Insure section. 3. Click the dropdown arrow next to 2UP. 4. Check the box for 1UP. 5. Review your selections to ensure 1UP is selected. 6. Place your bet. Important Notes - SportyBet reserves the right to void selections if any integrity issues or abusive behavior are detected. - 1UP is intended to make betting faster, more exciting, and more rewarding. If you have tried the above solutions and they did not work, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
