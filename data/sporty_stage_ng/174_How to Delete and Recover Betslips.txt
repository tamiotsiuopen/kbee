How to Delete and Recover Betslips

Betslip Management Deleting Betslips - Go to the bet history page. - Hold and press the ticket you want to delete. You can select multiple tickets and click Delete. - You can also swipe left to delete any betslip. Warning: Voided betslips cannot be deleted. Recovering a Deleted Betslip - When a betslip is deleted by mistake, an Undo option appears on the screen for a few seconds. - Use Undo to recover the deleted betslip. - Once Undo disappears, deleted tickets cannot be recovered. - Always double-check before deleting tickets from your Sports Bet History.
