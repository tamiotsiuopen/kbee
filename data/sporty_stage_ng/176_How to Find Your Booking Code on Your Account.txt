How to Find Your Booking Code on Your Account

How to Check Your Booking Code Overview The Booking Code is available on the Ticket Details page of your account. Follow these steps to locate your Booking Code. Step-by-Step Instructions 1. Log in to your account. 2. Click on your Account's Balance located in the top-right corner. 3. Select Sports Bet History. 4. Locate any running ticket for which you want to check the booking code. 5. Click on the desired ticket to go to the Ticket Details page and view the booking code.
