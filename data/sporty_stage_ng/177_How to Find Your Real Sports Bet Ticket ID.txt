How to Find Your Real Sports Bet Ticket ID

Locating Your Betslip Real Sports Bet History To find your Ticket ID for real sports bets: 1. Log in to your account. 2. Click on your Account's Balance in the top-right corner. 3. Select Sports Bet History and click on All to view a list of your placed bets. 4. Click on the specific bet you are looking for. 5. The Ticket ID, which is a 6-digit number, will be displayed at the top-left corner of the bet details. Note: Use this Ticket ID for any inquiries or support requests.
