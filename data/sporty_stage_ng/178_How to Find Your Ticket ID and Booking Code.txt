How to Find Your Ticket ID and Booking Code

How to Check Your Ticket ID Overview You can find your Ticket ID and Booking Code on the Ticket Details page of your account. Step-by-Step Instructions 1. Log in to your account. 2. Click on your Account's Balance in the top-right corner. 3. Select Sports Bet History. 4. In the Bet History area, locate the ticket for which you want to check the Ticket ID. 5. Click on the desired ticket to open the Ticket Details page. The Ticket ID will be displayed on the top left side of this page.
