How to Use a Gift or Free Bet on SportyBet

How to Use a Gift Overview Gifts can be used when placing bets and are available for selection on the betslip. This guide explains how to use available gifts on your account. Steps to Use a Gift 1. Log in to your SportyBet account. 2. For Real Sports or Instant Virtual: - Make your selections as if you are placing a bet. For Games: - Go to the games section at the bottom center of the home page. - Select the specific game you want to play (e.g., Red Black, Even Odd, Spin2Win, Spin da Bottle, Rush, Sporty Hero). 3. Locate the white gift box, usually at the bottom left of the screen, and click on it to apply the gift. 4. Place the bet. The gift will be used instead of funds from your account. Note The value of the Free Bet Gift used will be deducted from the Potential Winnings. If you have tried the above solutions and they did not work, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
