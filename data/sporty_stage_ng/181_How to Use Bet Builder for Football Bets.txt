How to Use Bet Builder for Football Bets

What is Bet Builder? Overview Bet Builder allows you to create a customized football bet by combining selections from the most popular markets within the same fixture. You can make up to 8 selections, and as you add each one, the odds are automatically calculated to form a single bet. Example of Bet Builder You can combine markets such as 1X2, Anytime Goalscorer, GG/NG, and number of corners to create a single football bet with combined odds. How to Use Bet Builder 1. Navigate to your chosen football fixture or event. 2. Select the Bet Builder tab from the header. 3. Review all available markets and make your selections. Odds will update automatically with each addition. 4. After adding all desired selections (up to 8), add the Bet Builder to your bet slip. 5. Enter your stake and place the bet. Important Notes - All selections in your Bet Builder must win for the overall bet to be successful. - If your Bet Builder includes a participant not in the starting lineup, or if any market within the Bet Builder is voided or pushed, the entire Bet Builder bet will be voided, regardless of the outcome of other selections.
