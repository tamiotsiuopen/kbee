How to View and Filter Your Bet History

Where Can I View My Current and Previous Bets? Overview The app's bet history feature groups your bets into Settled and Unsettled categories. You can filter these bets further by their outcome, such as Won, Lost, or Void, using the Bet Result dropdown menu. Viewing Bet History - All bets are now organized under Settled and Unsettled sections. - To view a specific type of bet (Won, Lost, Void), select either Settled or Unsettled, then use the Bet Result dropdown to filter by the desired status. - This organization allows you to access all your past bets based on whether they are settled and their outcome. How to Find Your Bet Ticket ID for Real Sports 1. Open the app and go to the ME section. 2. Navigate to Sports Bet History. 3. Tap on Settled and select either Settled or Unsettled from the dropdown, depending on the status of the bet you want to view. 4. Select the specific bet you wish to check. 5. The Ticket ID will be displayed at the top left corner of the screen. Filtering Bets - To quickly locate a particular betslip, use the Bet Result dropdown to filter bets by status: Void, Won, or Lost.
