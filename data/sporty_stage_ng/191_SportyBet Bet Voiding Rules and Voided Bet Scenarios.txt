SportyBet Bet Voiding Rules and Voided Bet Scenarios

SportyBet Bet Voiding Rules Event Status - If an event is postponed, abandoned, interrupted, or cancelled, it will be voided if it is not resumed within 48 hours of the kickoff time. Player Participation Rules - If a player does not participate at all, all bets on that player are voided. - If the player participates, regardless of time played, the bet remains valid. Exceptions for Specific Player Markets - For the following markets, bets are only valid if the player starts the match: - Player Goals (including Overtime) - Player Shots on Target - Player Total Shots - Player Assists - Player To Be Carded (including Overtime) - If the player does not start, these bets are void. Tennis-Specific Rules - In the event of a default, retirement, or walkover, all undecided bets are void. Market-Specific Voiding - For Over/Under market lines where the selection is a whole number (e.g., Over 1, Over 2, Over 3): - If the final score equals the line (e.g., Over 3 and the match ends 1:2 for a total of 3), the bet is void. Special Rules - Bore Draw Feature: If the full-time score is a draw, the bet will be voided. - Draw No Bet: If the full-time score is a draw, the selection will be voided. - Bet Builder: If any selection in a Bet Builder bet is voided, the entire Bet Builder bet is voided.
