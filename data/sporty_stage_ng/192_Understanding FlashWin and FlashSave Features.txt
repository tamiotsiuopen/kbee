Understanding FlashWin and FlashSave Features

FlashWin and FlashSave Overview Introduction to FlashWin - FlashWin is a feature that allows for early settlement of sports bets when winning conditions are detected before the match concludes. - The system analyzes games in real-time and may settle your bet early if you are on the path to victory, letting you celebrate sooner. How FlashWin Works 1. The advanced Smart System detects when your bet is likely to win before the match ends. 2. If a win is highly probable, your bet may be settled early. 3. In your bet history, a dynamic green flash icon will appear, replacing the usual green tick, to indicate an early FlashWin settlement. Important Notes about FlashWin - FlashWin does not guarantee a win on multiple bets if other selections are lost. - If FlashWin is triggered on a single bet, the entire bet is considered won. - FlashWin may not be available for all events and markets. Introduction to FlashSave - FlashSave appears on your selection if, after an early settlement via FlashWin, the final outcome does not match your original prediction. How FlashSave Works - Example: If you predict the Home team to win and FlashWin settles your bet at 3:0 in the 80th minute, but the final score becomes 3:3, your selection is still considered won. The FlashSave icon will be visible on your bet slip to indicate this situation.
