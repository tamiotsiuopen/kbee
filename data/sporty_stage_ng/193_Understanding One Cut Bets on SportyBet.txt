Understanding One Cut Bets on SportyBet

What is a One Cut Bet? Overview One Cut is a feature on SportyBet that allows you to receive a payout even if one of your selections loses in a multi-selection bet. The payout amount, called the One Cut Win amount, can be equal to or higher than your stake, depending on your settings. How to Create a One Cut Bet 1. Make multiple selections (one from each match, up to a maximum of 50 selections). 2. Below the stake entry option, go to the Insure section and select the checkbox for One Cut. 3. Use the slider to define how much you will win if one selection loses. You can set this amount to be lower, equal to, or higher than your stake before placing the bet. One Cut Slider - The slider allows you to control the One Cut Win amount. - Increasing the One Cut Win amount will decrease the potential payout if all selections win (To Win amount), and vice versa. Payout Scenarios - 0 games cut: You receive the To Win amount if all selections win. - 1 game cut: You receive the One Cut Win amount if one selection is lost. Important Points about One Cut Bets - The To Win amount is the payout if all your selections win after adding the One Cut feature. - The One Cut Win amount, as shown on your betslip when placing the bet, is what you receive if one selection is lost. - One Cut Bets cannot be cashed out. - Gifts cannot be applied to a One Cut betslip. - Void selections are not considered as lost and will be recalculated. Note: Void selections may lead to a recalculation of your bet amount and could result in a lower One Cut Win amount than your original stake. If you have tried the above solutions and still have issues, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
