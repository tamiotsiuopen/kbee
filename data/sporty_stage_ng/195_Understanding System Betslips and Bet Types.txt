Understanding System Betslips and Bet Types

System Bets Overview A system betslip is a type of ticket that includes markets, odds, and stakes from multiple events or games. System bets are unique because they allow you to place bets on all selections at once (using the Play All option) or on various combinations of your selections. Types of System Bets - Singles: To win a single bet, at least one of the selected events must win. - Doubles: To win a double, any two combinations of your selections must win. - Trebles: To win a treble, any three combinations of your selections must win. - Other combinations: System bets can also include higher-level combinations, depending on the number of selections in your betslip. How System Bets Work - You can choose to place your stake on all selections or on specific combinations (such as singles, doubles, trebles, etc.). - The payout depends on how many of your selections are correct and on which combinations you have chosen.
