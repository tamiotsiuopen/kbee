Understanding Void Bets

What is a Void Bet? Definition of a Void Bet - A Void Bet means the bet is either nil or invalid. - This can occur when an event is postponed, the event's results are unverifiable, or under other specific circumstances. How Void Bets Are Handled - When a match is set as void, it is considered to have an odd of 1.00. - Other selections on a winning ticket will be paid out regularly. Note If you have tried the above solutions and still have questions about why your bet was marked as void, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
