Cashout Rules for Bets Placed Using Free Bet Gifts

Cashout Using Free Bet Gifts - Bets placed with Free Bet Gifts are eligible for cashout. - If a bet is placed using both real money and a Free Bet Gift, the Free Bet Gift stake is forfeited upon cashout. Example: - If a customer bets 50 NGN from a Free Bet Gift and 50 NGN of real money, and the total cashout is 1000 NGN, the 50 NGN Free Bet Gift will be deducted. The customer receives 950 NGN.
