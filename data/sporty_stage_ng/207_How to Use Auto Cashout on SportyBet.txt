How to Use Auto Cashout on SportyBet

What is Auto Cashout Overview Auto Cashout is a feature by SportyBet that allows you to set a rule to automatically cash out a bet when a certain threshold is reached. The threshold can be set to an amount higher or lower than the current Cashout Offer. When the Cashout Offer reaches or exceeds your set threshold, the system will attempt to cash out the amount automatically, either fully or partially, depending on your rule. The funds will then be added to your SportyBet balance. Key Features - You can create an Auto Cashout rule after placing a bet. - Only one Auto Cashout rule can be set per bet at any given time. - The Auto Cashout rule can be removed at any time before it is triggered. - If you cash out manually, the Auto Cashout rule will be automatically removed. How to Create an Auto Cashout Rule 1. Log into your SportyBet account. 2. Click on Open Bets (You must place a bet to create an Auto Cashout rule). 3. Click on Cashout. 4. Click on Auto. 5. Enter the amount to set your threshold. This amount can be higher or lower than the current offer. You can use the slider to choose between fully cashing out or partially cashing out. 6. Click on Create Rule. The Auto Cashout rule is now created. How to Remove an Auto Cashout Rule - You can remove the Auto Cashout rule at any time before it is triggered. - The rule will also be removed automatically if you perform a manual cashout. Important Notes - Auto Cashout is not guaranteed to execute at or above the threshold you set. The system will make its best effort, but there may be cases where the market closes or a technical error occurs before the cashout can be completed. - SportyBet accepts no liability for any Auto Cashout rule that is not executed. If the above solutions have been tried and did not work, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
