How to Use the Cashout Feature

How to Cashout Overview Cashout allows you to settle your bets before the event is completed. Cashout Procedure 1. Log in to your account. 2. Go to your betslip. 3. Select the 'Cashout' option. 4. Only bets with the 'Cashout' icon can be cashed out immediately. Minimum Cashout Amount - The minimum cashout amount is NGN 0.1.
