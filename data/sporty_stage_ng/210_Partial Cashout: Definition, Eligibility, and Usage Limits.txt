Partial Cashout: Definition, Eligibility, and Usage Limits

What is a Partial Cashout? Definition A Partial Cashout allows you to withdraw a portion of your original stake from an unsettled bet, while leaving the remaining part of the stake active. Eligibility - Partial Cashout is available for unsettled bets that are either: - Single bets - Multiple bets with one combination Usage Limits - For Single Bets: Partial Cashout can be used up to 10 times per bet. - For Multiple Bets: Partial Cashout can be used up to 5 times per bet.
