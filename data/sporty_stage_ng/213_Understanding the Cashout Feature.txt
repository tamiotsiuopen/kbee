Understanding the Cashout Feature

What is Cashout? Overview - Cashout is a real-time feature that provides you with control over your bet. - You can use Cashout to guarantee a profit or to minimize your losses before the event is settled. How Cashout Works - The amount you receive from Cashout depends on the current likely outcome of your bet. - The more likely your bet is to win, the higher the Cashout amount offered will be. Note - For detailed Terms & Conditions of Cashout, please refer to the official documentation or website.
