How Odds Work and How to Calculate Winnings

Winning a Bet and Understanding Odds Understanding Odds - Odds indicate the probability of a specific outcome in an event. - Odds also determine the amount of money you can win if your bet is successful. Example of Calculating Winnings - If the odds are 4.0, your potential profit is four times your bet amount. - For instance, a NGN 10 bet at odds of 4.0 could result in NGN 40 if the bet wins.
