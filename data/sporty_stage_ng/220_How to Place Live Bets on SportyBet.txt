How to Place Live Bets on SportyBet

How to Play Live Betting Overview SportyBet offers a Live Betting feature that allows users in Nigeria to place bets on ongoing sports events. This feature covers a wide range of sports, including both traditional and eSports. Supported Sports for Live Betting - Football - Basketball - Tennis - Baseball - American Football - Table Tennis - Badminton - Counter-Strike - Dota2 - League of Legends (LoL) - Rugby - Cricket - Volleyball - Ice Hockey - Darts - Handball - Beach Volleyball - MMA - Boxing - Snooker - Futsal - eSoccer - eBasketball - eIce Hockey How to Place a Live Bet 1. Go to the Home page and click on Live. 2. Select the sport you want to bet on from the available list. 3. Browse the market list displayed under each selected match. 4. Select a live match to view real-time updates and match statistics. 5. Choose the events you wish to bet on and select the odds by clicking on them. 6. Your selections (up to a maximum of 50) will be automatically added to your betslip. 7. A message will appear to confirm whether your bet has been successfully placed. Additional Features - Click on Schedule to view upcoming matches that will be available for live betting or to place pre-match bets for matches scheduled for today and the next four days. - Real-time statistics and a match tracker are available for reference during live matches. Important Notes and Warnings - Live match updates and statistics are provided for your convenience, but they may sometimes differ from official results. - For the most reliable and up-to-date information, always refer to official sources. - All match data (results, stats, timing, player props, etc.) displayed on the Live Match Tracker may not match official sources and should not be used as the sole basis for placing or settling bets. - SportyBet does not accept liability for any loss caused by incorrect or outdated information displayed in the Live Match Tracker. - Bets are placed at your own risk and discretion.
