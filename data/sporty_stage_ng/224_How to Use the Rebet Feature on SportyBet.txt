How to Use the Rebet Feature on SportyBet

Rebet Feature Overview Definition The Rebet feature allows you to quickly reload open selections and place the same bet again. This function is only available for Real Sports. How to Use the Rebet Feature Using Rebet on the Ticket Details Page 1. Log in to your account. 2. Go to My Bets located at the bottom-right corner. 3. In the Sports Bet History area, find any running ticket you want to Rebet. 4. Click on the desired ticket to open the Ticket Details page. 5. Click on the green Rebet button. Using Rebet on the Submission Successful Page 1. Place a bet as usual. 2. After placing the bet, you will be redirected to the Submission Successful page. 3. Click on the Rebet button at the bottom of the page. Benefits of the Rebet Feature - Saves time by allowing quick repeat bets. - Ensures a seamless betting experience. Note If you have tried the above solutions and the Rebet feature is still not working as expected, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
