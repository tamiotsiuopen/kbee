Sporty SIM: Instant Football Bet Settlement Explained

Sporty SIM Overview Definition Sporty SIM is a feature that uses real matches and actual probabilities to instantly simulate the outcome of a football game. This allows your bet to be settled immediately, without waiting for the real match to finish. Availability - Sporty SIM is currently available only for football. - Only selected football markets are supported. How to Access Sporty SIM Bets 1. Log in to your account. 2. Tap on the Betslip at the bottom right of the home page. 3. In the pop-up that appears, switch from REAL to SIM using the toggle in the top-left corner. 4. To view your recent Sporty SIM bets, tap on My Games in the top-right corner. Note: SIM bets will not appear in your regular Bet History. To view them, go to the My Games section. Supported Markets - 1X2 - Over/Under - GG/NG - Double Chance - Handicap - Teams to Score - First Goal (pre-match only) - Last Goal (pre-match only) - Home Team Over/Under - Away Team Over/Under - 1st half 1X2 - 1st half Over/Under - 1st half Double Chance - 1st half First Goal (pre-match only) - 2nd half 1X2 - 2nd half Over/Under - 2nd half Double Chance - 2nd half First Goal (pre-match only) - 1X2 + Over/Under - 1X2 & GG/NG Important Notes - Cashout is not available for Sporty SIM bets. - If you have tried the above solutions and they did not work, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
