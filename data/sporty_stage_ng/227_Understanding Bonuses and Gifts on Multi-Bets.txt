Understanding Bonuses and Gifts on Multi-Bets

What is a Bonus? Definition of Bonus A bonus is an additional reward you can receive when you win a multi-bet with more than 2 selections. How Bonuses Work - Bonuses start from 3 percent of the Potential Winning for 2 selections. - The bonus percentage increases as you add more selections to your bet slip. - The maximum bonus can reach up to 1000 percent. Gifts vs. Bonuses - Gifts are different from bonuses. - Gifts cannot be withdrawn from your account.
