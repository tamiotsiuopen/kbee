Understanding Outright Betting

What is Outright Betting? Overview Outright betting involves placing bets on the long-term outcomes of a competition, tournament, or league. Examples include the English Premier League (EPL), the World Cup, or the Champions League. When Outright Bets Are Available - Outright bets are typically available before the competition begins. - These bets can also be placed during the season or tournament. Types of Outright Markets - Predicting the overall winner of a league, cup, or tournament - Predicting the top goal scorer - Predicting teams to be relegated or finish in the bottom three - Predicting the winner excluding certain teams (such as the "Big Six") - Other long-term outcomes Settlement of Outright Bets - Outright bets are based on season-long or tournament-long outcomes. - Bets usually remain unsettled until the event concludes or is close to completion. - Customers must wait until the final result is determined for the bet to be settled.
