How to Find Your Jackpot Bet History

Jackpot Bet History To find your Jackpot Bet history: 1. Log in to your SportyBet account. 2. Tap on the A-Z Menu icon at the bottom of the screen. 3. Select Jackpot at the top of the menu. 4. Click on the Bet History option in the top-right corner. This will provide you with access to your past Jackpot bets.
