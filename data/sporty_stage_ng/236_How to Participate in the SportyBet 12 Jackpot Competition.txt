How to Participate in the SportyBet 12 Jackpot Competition

How to Take Part in Jackpot Competition Overview This guide explains how to participate in the SportyBet 12 Jackpot competition, including making predictions, placing bets, checking results, and understanding special conditions. How to Enter the Jackpot Competition 1. Select "Jackpot" from the Navigation List on the main page. 2. On the Jackpot competition entry page, make your predictions for each of the listed matches by choosing Home win, Draw, or Away win. 3. The stake amount for each combination is NGN 100. 4. You can make more than one prediction for a match. Each additional combination increases the stake by NGN 100. 5. Review all your selections. 6. Click the "Place Bet" button to submit your entry. Important Notes - Once bets are submitted, they cannot be cancelled, amended, or refunded. - Bets can be placed until 15:00 Saturday, Nigerian time. - The countdown to the competition’s closure time is displayed on the competition entry page. Jackpot Rush Option - By choosing the "Jackpot Rush" option, a random selection of one prediction per match is automatically selected with a fixed stake of NGN 100. - You may add further predictions to the random selection. Each additional combination increases the entry cost by NGN 100. Checking Results - On Monday afternoon (Nigerian time), all match results and prize information are published in the "Previous Results" section on the "Jackpot" page. Special Conditions - If one Jackpot match is suspended, postponed, or cancelled and not resumed within 48 hours from the initial kick-off time, it counts as Void. Prize distribution in this case: - 30% of the Super Jackpot prize for 12 correct predictions - 40% of the 2nd prize for 11 correct predictions - 50% of the 3rd prize for 10 correct predictions - If two or more Jackpot matches are suspended, postponed, or cancelled (void), all bets for that Jackpot round are cancelled and stakes are refunded. Note If the above solutions have been tried and did not work, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
