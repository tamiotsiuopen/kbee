How to View Your Jackpot Bet History

Where to Find Your Jackpot Bet History Overview This section explains how to locate your Jackpot Bet history in your account. Steps to View Jackpot Bet History 1. Log in to your account. 2. Click on the AZ Menu icon at the bottom of the screen. 3. Click on Jackpot at the top. 4. Click on the bet history option in the top right corner.
