SportyBet Jackpot Competition: Overview and Prize Distribution

Jackpot Competition Overview Description - The SportyBet Jackpot Competition involves predicting the results of 12 matches selected by SportyBet each week. - Participants must be registered and have at least NGN 100.00 in their account to enter. Winning the Jackpot - If all 12 match results are predicted correctly, the Jackpot winner(s) share the Super Jackpot prize equally among all winning tickets. - Customers who correctly predict 11 or 10 match results also receive rewards. Multiple Winning Tickets - If a participant correctly predicts all 12 matches on more than one Jackpot ticket, each winning ticket is counted separately. - The Super Jackpot prize is always divided equally among all winning tickets, regardless of ownership. - Example: If you have 2 winning tickets and there are no other winners, you receive both shares. If there are other winners, the prize is split among all winning tickets, and you receive a share for each of your winning tickets.
