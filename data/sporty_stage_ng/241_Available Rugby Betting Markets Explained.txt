Available Rugby Betting Markets Explained

Rugby Markets Available Rugby Betting Markets 1X2 - Predict the outcome of the match. - Three possible outcomes: - 1: The home team wins - X: The teams draw - 2: The away team wins Head to Head (HH) - Predict the winner of the match, including possible overtimes. - Two possible outcomes: - 1 HH: The home team wins - 2 HH: The away team wins Heads-Up with Handicap (Spread) - A handicap is assigned to the favourite team. - If betting on the stronger team, it must perform better than the handicap for your bet to win. - The handicap (preceded by a minus sign) is subtracted from the team's final score. - Extra time is included in the calculation. Odd/Even - Predict whether the total number of points scored during the match will be odd or even. 1X2 1st Half - Predict the outcome of the first half of the match, considering only points scored in the first half. - Three possible outcomes: - 1: Home team wins the first half - X: First half ends in a draw - 2: Away team wins the first half Over/Under (Spread) - Predict whether the total number of points scored during regular time will be over or under the indicated spread.
