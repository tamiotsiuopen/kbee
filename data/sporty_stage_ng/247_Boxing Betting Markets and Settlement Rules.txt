General Rules and Voiding Bets - If there is a withdrawal or substitution of one of the boxers, bets will be void. - If a fight is declared a No Contest, all bets will be void and stakes returned. - If the scheduled number of rounds is changed before the fight, all Total Rounds, Round Betting, and Method of Victory bets will be void.
