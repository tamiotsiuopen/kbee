Counter-Strike Betting Market Types Explained

Counter-Strike Markets Market Types Winner - Predict which team will win the match. Map Handicap - Predict the winner of the match after applying the indicated map spread to the match result. Correct Score (in maps) - Predict the exact score of the match in terms of maps won by each team. Total Maps - Predict whether the total number of maps played in the match will be over or under the indicated spread. Map Winner (including overtime) - Predict which team will win the specified map, including overtime results. Map Round Handicap (including overtime) - Predict the winner of the map after applying the indicated round spread, including overtime results. Map Total Rounds (including overtime) - Predict whether the total number of rounds played in the map will be over or under the indicated spread, including overtime results. Map Winner - 1X2 - Predict which team will win the map, with three possible outcomes: Team 1 win, Team 2 win, or draw.
