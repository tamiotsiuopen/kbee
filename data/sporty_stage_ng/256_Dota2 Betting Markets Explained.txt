Dota2 Betting Markets Explained

Dota2 Markets Overview of Dota2 Betting Markets Winner - Predict which team will win the match. Map Handicap - Bet on the winner of the match after applying the indicated map spread to the match result. Total Maps - Bet on whether the total number of maps played in the match will be over or under the specified spread. Correct Score (in maps) - Predict the exact score of the match in terms of maps won by each team. Map Winner - Predict which team will win a specific map. Map Kill Handicap - Bet on the winner of the map after applying the indicated kill spread to the total kills during the map. Kill Draw No Bet - Bet on which team will have more kills in the map. If the number of kills is equal, the bet is void. Map 1st Aegis - Predict which team will destroy the first aegis in the map. Map 1st Tower - Predict which team will destroy the first tower in the map. Map 1st Barracks - Predict which team will destroy the first barracks in the map.
