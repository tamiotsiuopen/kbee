Explanation of Sports Betting Markets and Rules

All Sports Markets and Explanation General Rules for Sports Betting Markets Regular Time and Settlement - Most markets are settled based on regular time plus injury time unless otherwise specified. - Overtime and penalty shootouts do not affect the outcome unless stated for a specific market. - If a match is interrupted and resumed within 48 hours, bets are settled with the final result; otherwise, undecided bets are void. - Incorrect match data (e.g., time, score, team names) may result in bets being voided. - VAR-related changes may void bets on affected events. Football Betting Markets General Rules - Bets are settled on the result at the end of 90 minutes plus injury time, excluding extra time and penalties unless specified. - Only corners taken are counted; awarded but untaken corners are not. - Card values: yellow card = 1, red or yellow-red card = 2. Second yellow leading to red is not considered; a player cannot cause more than 3 cards or 35 booking points. - Player markets are valid if the player participates in the match, except for certain markets (Shots on Target, Total Shots, Assists, To Be Carded) which require the player to start. - Own goals are ignored for goalscorer-related markets. - Settlement is based on official TV and Press Association statistics unless clearly incorrect. Special Football Markets - Multiscorers: Predict the exact final score; 'Other Home/Away Win' means a win by a score not listed. - Player Assists: Awarded only if the final contribution directly leads to a goal, excluding own goals, penalties, direct free kicks, or corners. - Player Goals: Counted only if the player scores into the opposition's net (not own goals). - Match/Team Cards: Predict total cards for match or team; same card values apply as above.
