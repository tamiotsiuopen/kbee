Card & Booking Predictions: Markets for first player booked, player to be carded, and total bookings. - Match Outcome Markets: 1X2, Double Chance, Draw No Bet, Home/Away No Bet, and special combined outcome markets. - Handicap and Asian Handicap: Various handicap lines (0, 0.25, 0.5, 0.75, 1.0) with specific settlement rules for win, draw, and loss scenarios. - Over/Under, GG/NG, and related markets: Predict total goals, both teams to score, and stricter GG 2+ conditions. - Progression & Qualification: Predict team to qualify, winning method, and combined results in multi-leg ties. - Goal-Based Markets: Exact goals, goal range, odd/even, winning margin, teams to score. - Combined Outcome Markets: Combine 1X2 with GG/NG, Over/Under, Double Chance, and other conditions. - Time-Specific Goal Markets: Next/last goal, timing of goals, goals in specific intervals. - Overtime and Penalty Shootout Markets: Predict outcomes during extra time and shootouts, including winner, total goals, and specific events. - Booking and Corner Markets: Predict bookings and corners by team, half, or interval, with special rules for card values and settlement. - Race to Corners: Predict which team reaches a set number of corners first. - Time-Specific Match Result Markets: Predict match result within specific time intervals. - Combined Goal & Match Result Markets: Predict nth goal and match result, win to nil, and half-time/full-time combinations. - Outcome or Goals Combination Markets: Combine win/draw with over/under or GG/NG. - Clean Sheet Markets: Combine win/draw with clean sheet predictions. - 1-Minute Interval Markets: Predict events (goals, corners, bookings, offsides, penalties) in specific 1-minute intervals. -
