Other Sports Betting Markets Badminton - Match-Level: Winner, correct score, point handicap, total points, exact games, extra points. - Game-Level: Winner, point handicap, total points, odd/even, race to X points, Xth point. - General Notes: Games = sets, points = rallies, handicap and over/under use predefined lines, zero is even for odd/even. Boxing - Match Outcome: Winner, 1X2 (including draws), round-based markets (over/under rounds, winner & exact round), winning method, fight distance. - Special Rules: Technical decisions, no contest, fighter withdrawal, or change in scheduled rounds may void bets. Futsal - General: Bets apply to regular time unless specified. - Match Outcome: 1X2, next goal, double chance, draw no bet, handicap, over/under, odd/even, GG/NG. - First Half and Overtime: Same logic as full match, applied to half or overtime. - Penalty Shootout: Winner, nth goal. MMA - Match Outcome: Winner, 1X2 (draws void), round-based markets, winning method, fight distance. Darts - Match-Level: 1X2, winner, correct score (sets), set winner, total/odd-even sets, set/leg handicap, total/odd-even legs. - Set & Leg-Level: Win the rest, leg handicap, total legs, odd-even, correct score, specific leg outcomes. - 180s Markets: Most 180s, first 180, handicap, total 180s, player-specific 180s. Beach Volleyball and Volleyball - Match-Level: Winner, correct set score, total points, exact set. - Set-Level: Winner, total points, odd-even, Xth point, race to X points. - Scoring Format: Sets 1-4 to 25 points, set 5 to 15 points. Ice Hockey - Match-Level: 1X2, total goals, handicap, double chance, draw no bet, odd/even, highest score period. - Period-Level: 1X2, total goals, odd-even, Xth goal.
