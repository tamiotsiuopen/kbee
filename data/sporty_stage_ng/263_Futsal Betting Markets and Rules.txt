Futsal Betting Markets and Rules

alf Markets - 1st Half - 1X2: Predict which team will win the 1st half. - 1st Half - Which Team Will Win The Rest: Predict which team will win the rest of the 1st half, ignoring the current score. - 1st Half - Nth Goal: Predict which team will score the Nth goal in the 1st half. - 1st Half - Handicap: Predict the winner in the 1st half, considering the handicap in brackets. - 1st Half - Total: Predict whether the total number of goals scored in the 1st half is over or under a given line. Overtime Markets - Overtime - 1X2: Predict the winner in overtime. - Overtime - Which Team Will Win The Rest: Predict the result for the remainder of overtime. - Overtime - Nth Goal: Predict which team will score the Nth goal during overtime. - Overtime - Total: Predict whether the total number of goals scored during overtime is over or under a given line. Penalty Shootout Markets - Penalty Shootout - Winner: Predict the winner in the penalty shootout. - Penalty Shootout - Nth Goal: Predict which team will score the Nth goal in the penalty shootout. Note If the above solutions have been tried and did not work, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
