How Bore Draw Works for Football Bets

What is Bore Draw? Overview Bore Draw is a feature available for pre-match bets on the Half Time/Full Time and Correct Score markets across all supported football leagues. It is designed for matches that end in a 0-0 draw. If your qualifying bet is affected by this outcome, you will receive a full refund regardless of your bet’s result. This feature offers extra assurance for matches that end without any goals. Eligibility - All players placing qualifying bets on supported markets in supported leagues are eligible for the Bore Draw refund. How to Qualify for Bore Draw 1. Place your bet on the Half Time/Full Time or Correct Score market in a supported football league. 2. Ensure your bet is placed before the match begins. Exclusions - Bore Draw does not apply to bets that have been cashed out or settled early. Refund Process 1. After the game ends, the system automatically checks for qualifying Bore Draw bets. 2. If the final score is 0-0, the refund process is triggered. 3. Refunds are automatically credited to your account. Refund Options - Refunds are provided as cash or FBG. - No wagering requirements are attached to these refunds.
