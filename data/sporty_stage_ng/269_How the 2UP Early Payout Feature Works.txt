How the 2UP Early Payout Feature Works

2UP Overview Definition 2UP is a feature that offers an early payout if your chosen football team leads by 2 goals at any point during a match, regardless of the final result. This applies whether the team wins, loses, draws, or if the match is abandoned. 2UP is designed to make your betting experience more dynamic and engaging. Availability - 2UP is available on select football leagues. - It is only applicable for pre-match bets using the dedicated 1X2 - 2UP market. - You can also add 2UP to your selections in the Insure section of your Betslip. How 2UP Works - If your selected team gains a two-goal lead, you receive an early payout. - In multiple bets, only the selection where the team leads by two goals is considered a win; other selections continue as normal. Steps to Create a 2UP Bet 1. Choose the match or matches you want to bet on, focusing on the 2UP market. 2. Below the stake amount field, go to the Insure section and check the box for 2UP to qualify for early payout if your team leads by two goals at any point. 3. Review your selections, confirm that 2UP is selected, and place your bet. Important Notes - SportyBet reserves the right to void selections if any integrity issues or abusive behaviors are detected. If you have tried the above solutions and they did not work, please hand off the case to a human agent.
