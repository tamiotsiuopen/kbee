Ice Hockey Betting Market Types Explained

Ice Hockey Markets Overview This section explains the different types of betting markets available for ice hockey matches. Each market type is described below. 1X2 - Predict the result at full time. Xth Period 1X2 - Predict the 1X2 result of the specified period. Total Goals - Predict whether the total number of goals scored at full time is over or under a given line. Xth Period Total Goals - Predict whether the total number of goals scored in the specified period is over or under a given line. Handicap - Predict the final result of the match while considering the handicap shown in brackets. - Example: (0:1.5) means the away team has a 1.5 goal advantage; (1.5:0) means the home team has a 1.5 goal advantage. Double Chance - A double chance bet allows you to cover two of the three possible outcomes in the match with one bet. - Possible options: - 1 or X: Competitor 1 wins or draw - 1 or 2: Competitor 1 wins or competitor 2 wins - 2 or draw: Competitor 2 wins or draw Draw No Bet - Predict the match result at full time. - If the teams draw, the selection will be voided. - Possible options: - Competitor 1 wins - Competitor 2 wins Odd/Even - Predict whether the total number of goals in the match will be odd (1, 3, 5, 7, 9, etc.) or even (0, 2, 4, 6, 8, etc.). Highest Score Period - Predict which period of the match will provide the most goals. - Possible options: - 1st - 2nd - 3rd - Equal Xth Goal - Predict which team will score the specified goal number. Xth Period Xth Goal - Predict which team will score the specified goal number during the specified period. Xth Period Odd/Even - Predict whether the total number of goals in the specified period will be odd or even.
