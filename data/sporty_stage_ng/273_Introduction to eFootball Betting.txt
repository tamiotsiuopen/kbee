Introduction to eFootball Betting

eFootball Definition - eFootball is a competitive football video game played between two real players (e.g., FIFA or PES). Game Duration - Each match lasts 10 to 12 minutes in real time. Betting Features - Markets similar to regular sports: Match Result (1X2), Total Goals, Both Teams to Score, Correct Score, etc. How to Identify eFootball - Player names are shown beside team names (e.g., Chelsea (Annie)).
