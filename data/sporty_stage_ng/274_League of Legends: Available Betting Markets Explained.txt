League of Legends: Available Betting Markets Explained

League of Legend Markets Available Market Types Winner - Predict which team will win the match. Map Handicap - Predict the winner of the match after applying the indicated map spread to the match result. Total Maps - Predict whether the total number of maps played in the match will be over or under the indicated spread. Correct Score (in maps) - Predict the exact score of the match in terms of maps won by each team. Map Winner - Predict which team will win a specific map. Map 1st Tower - Predict which team will destroy the first tower in the map. Map 1st Dragon - Predict which team will kill the first dragon in the map. Map 1st Baron - Predict which team will kill the first baron in the map. Map 1st Blood - Predict which team will achieve the first blood in the map. Map 1st Inhibitor - Predict which team will win the first inhibitor in the map.
