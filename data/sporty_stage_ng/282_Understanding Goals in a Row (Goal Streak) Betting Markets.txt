Understanding Goals in a Row (Goal Streak) Betting Markets

Goals in a Row Markets Overview The 'Goals in a Row' markets are prediction markets where you bet on whether a team will score a specified number of consecutive goals without the opposing team scoring in between. These are also known as goal streaks. 2 Goals in a Row (2+ Goal Streak) - Predict that a team will score two or more consecutive goals without the opponent scoring between them. - Available options: - Home Team to score 2+ goals in a row - Away Team to score 2+ goals in a row - Any Team to score 2+ goals in a row 3 Goals in a Row (3+ Goal Streak) - Predict that a team will score three or more consecutive goals without the opponent scoring between them. - Available options: - Home Team to score 3+ goals in a row - Away Team to score 3+ goals in a row - Any Team to score 3+ goals in a row
