What Happens When a Match Is Postponed, Abandoned, Cancelled, or Interrupted

Match Postponement, Abandonment, Cancellation, or Interruption - If a match is abandoned, cancelled, or interrupted, there is a 48-hour waiting period to see if it will be resumed or rescheduled. - If not resumed within 48 hours, the match is settled as void (odd of 1.00). - Other selections on a winning ticket are paid out regularly.
