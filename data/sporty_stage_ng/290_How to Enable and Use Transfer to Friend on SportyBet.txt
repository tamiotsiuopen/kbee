How to Enable and Use Transfer to Friend on SportyBet

Enabling Transfer to Friend Overview The Transfer to Friend feature allows you to transfer funds from your SportyBet balance or top up a friend's SportyBet account. This feature is subject to security requirements to protect your account and funds. Requirements to Enable Transfer to Friend - Your account must be previously verified with BVN (Bank Verification Number). BVN verification is no longer available for new users, but previously verified accounts can use this feature. - You must have your Withdrawal PIN set. - Your email address must be verified. How to Enable Transfer to Friend 1. Log in to your SportyBet account. 2. Click on your Account's Balance at the top-right corner. 3. Click on Withdraw. 4. Click on Transfer to Friend. 5. Ensure your Withdrawal PIN is set and your email is verified. If not, you can set them from this screen by selecting the respective option. 6. Click on Enable once the Withdrawal PIN is set and Email is verified. 7. Enter the 6-digit code sent to your registered phone number to verify it. You can request the 6-digit code up to 5 times in 24 hours. Make sure Do Not Disturb is disabled on your phone to receive the code. Note: If you have tried the above steps and still have not received the code, the case should be handed off to a human agent. 8. Upon successful verification, you will receive a Congratulations message confirming that Transfer to Friend has been subscribed for. The feature will be activated after a 24-hour cooldown period. Click OK to finish. 9. You will receive an SMS and an email alert on your registered mobile number and verified email address after successfully applying for the Transfer to Friend option. Transaction Limits Sender Limits - Minimum value per transaction: NGN 100.00 - Maximum value per transaction: NGN 5000.00 - Maximum transaction value per day: NGN 5000.00 Receiver Limits - Maximum value that can be received per day: NGN 5000.00 - A maximum of 5 friends can be added to a SportyBet account.
