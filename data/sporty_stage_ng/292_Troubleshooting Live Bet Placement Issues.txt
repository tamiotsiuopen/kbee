Troubleshooting Live Bet Placement Issues

Unable to Place a Live Bet Possible Reasons for Live Bet Issues - The odds of your selections may have changed. - One or more of your selections may no longer be offered. To continue placing bets, remove these selections from your Betslip. - You may not have a stable internet connection. Please check your connection. Note: If you have tried the above solutions and are still unable to place a bet, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
