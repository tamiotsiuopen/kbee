What to Do If Your Bet Is Settled Incorrectly

An Incorrectly Settled Bet Overview If you believe your bet has been settled incorrectly, review the bet details and settlement information carefully to ensure there is a genuine error. Next Steps - Double-check the event results and your bet slip for accuracy. - Compare the settlement with the official event outcome. Note If you have tried the above steps and the issue is not resolved, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
