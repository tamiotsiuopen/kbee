What to Do If Your Bet Remains Unsettled

An Unsettled Bet Overview Some sports bets may remain unsettled when SportyBet is waiting for official results to be confirmed. Settlement Timeframes - Certain events, such as Italian - Serie D football matches, may take up to 5 days for bet settlement.  - If your sports bet remains unsettled after 12 hours from the match completion time, ensure you have waited the appropriate period for official results. Note If you have tried the above solutions and your bet is still unsettled, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
