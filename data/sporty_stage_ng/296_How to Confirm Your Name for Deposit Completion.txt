How to Confirm Your Name for Deposit Completion

No, I want to confirm my name Overview It is mandatory to confirm your account details in order to complete your deposit. Account Confirmation Process 1. After making a deposit, you will be redirected to the account confirmation screen. 2. If you did not confirm your account during your first deposit, you will receive a pop-up prompting you to confirm your details the next time you visit SportyBet. 3. Review your account details on the confirmation screen. 4. If the details are correct, select Confirm to proceed. 5. Once confirmed, your deposit transaction will be successfully submitted and credited to your SportyBet wallet. If Your Name Is Incorrect - If you notice that your name is incorrect, select Not my Name, Name Update. - Click on Verify to upload the necessary documents for correction. Note If you have tried the above solutions and your issue is not resolved, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
