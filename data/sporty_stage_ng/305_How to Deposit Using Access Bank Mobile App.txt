How to Deposit Using Access Bank Mobile App

Depositing Using Access Bank Mobile App 1. Log in to your Access Bank mobile app. 2. Click on Sporty Wallet Funding. 3. Select LOTTERY AND BETTING as the category. 4. Type SPORTYBET in the search box and select it as the biller. 5. Select SportyBet Wallet TopUp as the product. 6. Enter the deposit amount. 7. Enter your SportyBet wallet number or ID. 8. Click PAY to proceed, review the details, and confirm. 9. Click Pay to finalize the process.
