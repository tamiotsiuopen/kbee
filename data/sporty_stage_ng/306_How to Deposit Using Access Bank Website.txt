How to Deposit Using Access Bank Website

Depositing Using Access Bank Website 1. Log in to your Access Bank internet banking web portal. 2. Click on Bill Payments. 3. Click on Sporty Wallet Funding. 4. Select the source account if you have multiple accounts. 5. Type SPORTYBET in the search box and select it as the biller. 6. Select SportyBet Wallet TopUp as the product. 7. Enter the deposit amount. 8. Enter your SportyBet wallet number or ID. 9. Click Proceed to review the details and confirm. 10. Click Continue to finalize the payment.
