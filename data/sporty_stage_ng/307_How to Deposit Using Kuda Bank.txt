How to Deposit Using Kuda Bank

Deposit via Kuda Bank How to Deposit Using Kuda App or Website 1. Log in to your Kuda Bank app or website. 2. Click on Payment. 3. Select Pay a Bill. 4. Choose Betting. 5. Select SportyBet. 6. Enter the amount you wish to deposit. 7. Enter the phone number linked to your SportyBet wallet. 8. Complete the payment process. A successful notification will be displayed, and your SportyBet account will be credited instantly.
