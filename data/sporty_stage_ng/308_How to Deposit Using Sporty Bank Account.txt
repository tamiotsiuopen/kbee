How to Deposit Using Sporty Bank Account

Deposit via Sporty Bank What is a Sporty Bank Account? A Sporty Bank account is a virtual bank account number linked to your Sporty wallet through a partnership with a Nigerian bank. Sporty itself is not a bank. Any bank transfers to this account, whether from you or another person, will be automatically credited to your Sporty balance. Requirements Before Activation - You must verify your BVN (Bank Verification Number). - You must have completed at least one successful withdrawal to a bank account that provides your account name. How to Generate and Use a Sporty Bank Account 1. Log in to your SportyBet account and go to My Account. 2. For WAP and app users: Click your balance on the top right corner or select the Me tab at the bottom right of your screen. For web users: Hover your pointer over My Account to view the drop-down menu. 3. Click the Deposit button. 4. Scroll through the deposit options and select Sporty Bank. 5. If this is your first time setting up Sporty Bank, you will be prompted to verify your BVN. Enter your BVN when requested. This step is only required during the initial setup. 6. Wait for the system to generate your Sporty Bank Account Number. 7. If you have already activated a Sporty Bank account, you can start your transfer using the account number shown on this page. 8. Transfer your desired deposit amount to the generated Sporty Bank Account Number. 9. Upon successful payment, your betting wallet will be credited instantly. Important Notes and Warnings - Only transfers processed via NIP (Nigerian Interbank Payment) are accepted to this account. - Do not attempt transfers via POS, ATMs, or over-the-counter deposits to this account number. Escalation Note If you have tried the above solutions and still experience issues, please escalate the case to a human agent.
