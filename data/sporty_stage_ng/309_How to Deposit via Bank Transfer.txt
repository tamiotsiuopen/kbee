How to Deposit via Bank Transfer

Deposit via Bank Transfer Overview This guide provides step-by-step instructions on how to deposit funds into your SportyBet account using a bank transfer.  1. Log in to your SportyBet account and go to My Account. - For WAP and app: Click your balance on the top right corner or click the Me tab on the bottom right of your screen. - For the web: Hover your pointer over My Account to view the drop-down menu. 2. Click the Deposit button. 3. Scroll through the deposit options and select Bank Transfer. 4. Enter the amount you would like to deposit. 5. Follow the on-screen payment steps. For reference, the steps are: - Step 1: Enter your preferred amount (ensure this is the same amount you will transfer) and click or tap Top Up Now. - Step 2: You will be provided with a temporary bank account. This account expires in 30 minutes. - Step 3: Transfer the money to the provided account using your online banking or USSD. After making the transfer, click the Verify Transfer button from the live web page. - Step 4: Check your transaction history in SportyBet. Processing Time - Bank transfers are usually credited within 10 minutes. Note - If the deposit is not credited within 60 minutes, and you have followed all the steps above, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
