How to Deposit via Coral Pay Using the BillerOne App

Deposit Via Coral Pay Overview Deposits using Coral Pay are completed directly from the BillerOne app. Procedure to Deposit via Coral Pay 1. Log into the BillerOne App. 2. From the home page, select Games. 3. Select SportyBet, then select SportyBet Wallet Topup. 4. Enter the amount you wish to fund. 5. Enter your SportyBet ID (Phone Number). 6. Select your payment option and complete the payment. 7. Upon successful payment, you will receive in-app and email notifications. Note If you have tried the above steps and your issue is not resolved, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
