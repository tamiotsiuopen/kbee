How to Deposit via Direct Bank (UBA, GTBank, Zenith, Kuda, Opay, Access)

Deposit Via Direct Bank Overview This guide explains how to deposit funds into your SportyBet account using Direct Bank options. Multiple banks are supported, each with specific instructions for different deposit methods. General Steps to Start a Direct Bank Deposit 1. Log in to your SportyBet account and go to 'My Account'. 2. For WAP and app users: Click your balance on the top right corner or select the 'Me' tab at the bottom right of your screen. 3. For web users: Hover your pointer over 'My Account' to view the drop-down menu. 4. Click the 'Deposit' button. 5. Scroll through the deposit options and select 'Direct Bank'. 6. Select your preferred bank and follow the instructions provided. Supported Banks for Direct Bank Deposits - United Bank of Africa (UBA) - GTBank - Zenith Bank - Kuda Bank - Opay - Access Bank Deposit Procedures by Bank United Bank of Africa (UBA) Option 1: Deposit with USSD 1. Dial *919*26*3#. 2. Input your UBA Magic Banking PIN. 3. Enter your SportyBet registered phone number. 4. Enter the deposit amount. 5. Confirm the amount to be paid. 6. An SMS will be sent for confirmation. Note: After completing these steps, your SportyBet account will be credited. Option 2: Deposit with LEO Messenger 1. Go to https://m.me/ubachatbanking or use the LEO Messenger button. 2. Type 'Pay Bills'. 3. Select 'Betting/Lottery'. 4. Select 'SportyBet'. 5. Enter your SportyBet registered phone number. 6. Enter the deposit amount. 7. Confirm payment. 8. A confirmation message will be sent. Note: After completing these steps, your SportyBet account will be credited. Option 3: Deposit at UBA Branches - Visit any UBA branch to fund your SportyBet wallet. Note: Deposit via UBA website and mobile app will be available soon.
