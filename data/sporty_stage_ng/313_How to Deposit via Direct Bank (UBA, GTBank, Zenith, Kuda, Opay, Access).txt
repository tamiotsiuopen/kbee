From the home page, click on 'Betting' and select 'SportyBet'. 3. Enter your SportyBet phone number and deposit amount. 4. Confirm your details and select your payment method. 5. Upon successful payment, you will receive a notification and your wallet will be credited instantly. Access Bank Option 1: Deposit via Access Bank Website 1. Log in to your Access iBanking web account. 2. Click on 'Bill Payments'. 3. Click 'Sporty Wallet Funding'. 4. Select source account (if you have multiple accounts). 5. Type 'SPORTYBET' in the search box and select as biller. 6. Select 'SportyBet Wallet TopUp' as product. 7. Enter the deposit amount. 8. Enter the wallet number/ID. 9. Click 'Proceed' to review and confirm details. 10. Click 'Continue' to finalize payment. Option 2: Deposit via Access Bank Mobile App 1. Log in to your Access Bank app. 2. Click 'Sporty Wallet Funding'. 3. Select 'Lottery and Betting' as category. 4. Type 'SPORTYBET' in the search box and select as biller. 5. Select 'SportyBet Wallet TopUp' as product. 6. Enter the deposit amount. 7. Enter the wallet number/ID. 8. Click 'Pay' to review and confirm details. 9. Click 'Pay' to finalize the process. Note If you have tried all the above solutions and your deposit is still unsuccessful, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
