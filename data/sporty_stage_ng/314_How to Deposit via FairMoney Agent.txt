How to Deposit via FairMoney Agent

Deposit via Fairmoney Overview This guide explains how to deposit funds into your SportyBet account using a FairMoney agent. Procedure 1. Locate your closest or preferred FairMoney agent. 2. Provide your SportyBet phone number and the amount you wish to top up to the agent. 3. The FairMoney agent will process the transaction. 4. Once SportyBet receives the amount transferred via FairMoney, your SportyBet account will be credited. Note You should retain the payment receipt for your records.
