How to Deposit via GT Bank (Website, Mobile App, and USSD)

Deposit via GT Bank Overview This guide explains how to deposit funds into your betting wallet using GT Bank through three different methods: the GT Bank website, the GTBank mobile app, and USSD. Depositing Using GT Bank Website 1. Log in to your GT Bank account. 2. On the Internet banking homepage, select 'Payments & Collections' from the drop-down menu. 3. Click 'Sports and Gaming'. 4. Scroll and select 'SPORTYBET'. 5. Click on 'Make New Payments'. 6. Enter your SportyBet login phone number and the amount you wish to deposit. 7. Confirm all your details and review bank charges. 8. Answer any security questions and enter your token code or USSD as required. 9. Upon successful payment, your betting wallet will be credited instantly. Depositing Using GTBank Mobile App 1. Log in to your GTBank mobile app. 2. Click on 'Other Payments' and select 'More Payments'. 3. Click on 'Sports and Gaming' and select 'SPORTYBET'. 4. Enter your SportyBet login phone number and the amount you wish to deposit. 5. Select the GT Bank account you wish to make the deposit from. 6. Enter your mobile banking PIN and confirm the transaction. 7. Upon successful payment, your betting wallet will be credited instantly. Depositing Using USSD 1. Dial *737*50*Amount*558# on your registered mobile phone. 2. Follow the on-screen prompts to complete your deposit. Note If you have tried all the above solutions and your deposit is still not successful, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
