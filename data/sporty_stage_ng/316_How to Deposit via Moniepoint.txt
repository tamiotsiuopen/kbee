How to Deposit via Moniepoint

Deposit via Moniepoint Overview This section explains how to deposit funds into your SportyBet account using Moniepoint. Steps to Deposit 1. Log in to your Moniepoint account. 2. Click on PayBills under the Betting category. 3. Select SportyBet as the biller. 4. Enter the phone number registered with your SportyBet account. 5. Enter the amount you wish to top up. 6. Enter your PIN to authorize the transaction. 7. You will receive a notification once your payment has been processed successfully. 8. Your SportyBet account will be credited with the deposited amount. Note If you have tried the above steps and your issue is not resolved, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
