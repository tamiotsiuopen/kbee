How to Deposit via Opay

Deposit via Opay Overview This guide explains how to deposit funds into your SportyBet account using Opay. There are two main methods: Opay Checkout and Pay from Opay App. Opay Checkout 1. Log in to your SportyBet account. - From the homepage, click on your Account Balance in the top-right corner or select the Me tab at the bottom right. - Click on Deposit. 2. Enter Deposit Amount. - Enter the amount you wish to deposit into your account. - Click the Top Up Now button. 3. Complete the Transaction. - Enter your PIN (depending on the verification method used) to complete the transaction. 4. Confirmation. - You will receive a confirmation that your SportyBet account has been successfully funded. Pay from Opay App 1. Log into your OPay App. 2. From the home page, click on Betting and select SportyBet from the list. 3. Enter your SportyBet Phone Number (for example, 080xxxxxxxx) and the amount you wish to deposit. 4. Confirm all your details are correct and select your preferred payment method. 5. Upon successful payment, you will receive a notification and your SportyBet wallet will be credited instantly.
