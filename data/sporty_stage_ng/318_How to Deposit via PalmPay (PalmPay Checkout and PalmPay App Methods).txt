How to Deposit via PalmPay (PalmPay Checkout and PalmPay App Methods)

Deposit via Palmpay Overview This guide explains how to deposit funds into your SportyBet account using PalmPay. Two methods are available: PalmPay Checkout through the SportyBet platform and direct payment from the PalmPay app. PalmPay Checkout Method 1. Log in to your SportyBet account. 2. From the homepage, click on your Account Balance in the top-right corner or select the Me tab at the bottom right. 3. Click on Deposit. 4. Enter the amount you wish to deposit into your account. 5. Click the Top Up Now button. 6. Enter your PIN (depending on the verification method used) to complete the transaction. 7. You will receive a confirmation that your SportyBet account has been successfully funded. Pay from PalmPay App Method 1. Log in to your PalmPay App. 2. From the homepage, click on Betting and select SportyBet from the billers list. 3. Enter your SportyBet phone number. 4. Click the amount you wish to deposit. 5. Input your transaction PIN and complete your payment. 6. Once the transaction is successful, your SportyBet account will be funded instantly. Note If you have tried the above solutions and your deposit did not process successfully, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
