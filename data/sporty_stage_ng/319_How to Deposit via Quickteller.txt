How to Deposit via Quickteller

Deposit via Quickteller Overview This guide explains how to deposit funds into your SportyBet account using Quickteller. How to Deposit Using Quickteller Step 1: Log in to Your SportyBet Account - For WAP and App: Click your balance at the top right corner, or select the 'Me' tab at the bottom right of your screen. - For Web: Hover your pointer over 'My Account' to view the drop-down menu. Step 2: Initiate Deposit - Click the 'Deposit' button. - Scroll through the deposit options and select 'Quickteller.' Step 3: Complete Deposit on Quickteller - Visit https://www.quickteller.com/sportybet - Enter your SportyBet Login Mobile Phone Number, the amount you wish to deposit, and your email address. - Verify the payment details and click on 'PAY.' Step 4: Confirmation - A payment receipt will be generated. - You will receive an email notification at the provided email address. - Your money has now been successfully sent to your SportyBet account.
