How to Deposit via TENN

Deposit via TENN Overview This section provides instructions on how to deposit funds into your SportyBet account using the TENN payment method. Deposit Procedure 1. Log in to your SportyBet account: - From the homepage, click on your Account Balance in the top-right corner or select the Me tab at the bottom right. - Click on Deposit. 2. Enter your TENN account login details: - Depending on the verification method used, log in to your TENN account. - Complete the transaction as prompted. 3. Confirmation: - You will receive a confirmation that your SportyBet account has been successfully funded.
