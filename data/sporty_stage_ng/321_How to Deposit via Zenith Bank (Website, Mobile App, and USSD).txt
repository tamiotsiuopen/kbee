How to Deposit via Zenith Bank (Website, Mobile App, and USSD)

Deposit via Zenith Overview This guide explains how to deposit funds into your betting wallet using Zenith Bank through three different methods: the Zenith Bank website, the Zenith Bank mobile app, and USSD. Deposit via Zenith Bank Website 1. Log in to your Zenith Bank account on the website. 2. On the Internet banking homepage, click on 'Payments' from the drop-down menu. 3. Select 'Gaming and Lottery'. 4. Complete the required information on the page. 5. Click on 'Confirm your payment'. 6. Upon successful payment, your betting wallet will be credited instantly. Deposit via Zenith Bank Mobile App 1. Log in to your Zenith Bank mobile banking app. 2. Click on the dash at the top left of the screen. 3. Select 'Bills, payment and collections'. 4. Choose 'gaming/lottery' as the category. 5. Select 'SportyBet' as the biller. 6. Enter the amount you wish to deposit. 7. Enter your SportyBet registered phone number in the reference field. 8. Click 'Continue'. 9. A confirmation popup will appear for you to enter your security pin, token, or both if required. 10. If the payment is successful, a confirmation popup will display. 11. Upon successful payment, your betting wallet will be credited instantly. Deposit via USSD 1. Dial *966*6*7767# on your mobile phone. 2. Enter your 10 or 11 digit registered mobile number. 3. Enter the amount you wish to deposit. 4. Enter your pin. 5. You will receive a notification when the deposit is successful. Note If you have tried all of the above solutions and your deposit is still not successful, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
