How to Set Up and Use a Sporty Bank Account for Deposits

Sporty Bank Account Overview Definition A Sporty Bank Account is a virtual bank account number linked to your Sporty wallet. It is created in partnership with a Nigerian bank. Sporty is not a bank itself. Any bank transfer made to this account, by you or others, will be automatically credited to your Sporty balance. Requirements to Generate a Sporty Bank Account - You must verify your BVN (Bank Verification Number). - You must have completed at least one successful withdrawal to a bank account that confirms your account name. How to Set Up a Sporty Bank Account Step-by-Step Instructions 1. Log in to your SportyBet account. 2. Navigate to "My Account": - On wap/app: Tap your balance (top right) or the "Me" tab (bottom right). - On web: Hover over "My Account" to open the drop-down menu. 3. Click the "Deposit" button. 4. Scroll and select "Sporty Bank" from the deposit options. 5. Enter your BVN when prompted. 6. Wait for the system to generate your Sporty Bank Account Number. This step is only required for the first-time setup. If already activated, use the account number shown on the page. 7. Transfer your desired amount to the generated account number. 8. Upon successful payment, your Sporty wallet will be credited instantly. Important Notes - Only NIP (Nigerian Interbank Settlement System Instant Payment) transfers are accepted. - Do not use POS terminals, ATM transfers, or over-the-counter deposits.
