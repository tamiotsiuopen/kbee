How to Remove or Change Your Debit or Credit Card for Deposits

Removing or Changing Your Debit/Credit Card Overview This guide explains how to remove or change the debit or credit cards used for deposits in the SportyBet App. Steps to Remove or Change Your Card 1. Open the SportyBet App. 2. Go to the Accounts section. 3. Select Deposits. 4. Choose Card. 5. Click on Switch Card to edit or remove the existing card.
