How to Resolve a Deposit Not Reflected in Your Account

My Deposit Has Not Reflected Overview This article provides steps to follow if your deposit has not appeared in your account. Steps to Resolve Missing Deposit 1. Wait for up to 10 minutes after making your deposit. 2. If the deposit still does not reflect in your account after 10 minutes: - Go to the Transactions section. - Click on the Fix Status button. Note If you have tried the above solutions and your deposit still has not reflected, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
