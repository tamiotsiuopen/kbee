How to Resolve Pending Deposits

My Deposit is Pending Overview Deposits may remain pending for several reasons. The process differs slightly for new and existing users. For New Users - It is mandatory to confirm your account details after making a deposit. - After depositing, you will be redirected to the account confirmation screen. - Check your details on the screen. - If the details are correct, select Confirm to proceed. - After confirmation, your deposit transaction will be submitted and credited to your SportyBet wallet. - If the name shown is incorrect: - Select Not my Name, Name Update. - Click Verify to upload the necessary documents for correction. For Existing Users - If your deposit status remains pending after confirming your name: - Check your bank account to verify if you have been debited. - If you have been debited, please wait. Deposits are usually processed immediately, but in some cases, it may take up to 60 minutes for the final status due to network or third-party provider delays. Note If your deposit does not reflect in your SportyBet wallet within 60 minutes after being debited and following the above steps, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
