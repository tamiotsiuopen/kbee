What to Do If Your Deposit Fails

My Deposit Has Failed Overview If your deposit shows as failed and the amount has been deducted from your bank account, the amount will be automatically reversed to the original deposit source. What to Do Next 1. Wait for the automatic reversal of funds to your original deposit instrument. 2. If you need an update on the status of your fund reversal, contact your bank first. Possible Reasons for Deposit Failure - Transaction rejected by your bank - Low balance in your bank account - The deposit transaction was not completed - The amount entered on SportyBet and the amount transferred from your bank do not match - Network issue at the payment gateway Note If you have tried the above solutions and your issue is not resolved, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
