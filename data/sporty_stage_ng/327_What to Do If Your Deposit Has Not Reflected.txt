What to Do If Your Deposit Has Not Reflected

My Deposit Has Not Reflected Overview This guide provides steps to follow if your deposit has not yet appeared in your account. Steps to Take if Deposit Has Not Reflected 1. Wait up to 10 minutes for the deposit to process. 2. If the deposit still does not reflect after 10 minutes: - Go to the Transactions section. - Click on the Fix Status button. Note If the above solutions have been tried and did not work, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
