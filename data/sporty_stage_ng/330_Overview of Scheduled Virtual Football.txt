Overview of Scheduled Virtual Football

Overview of Scheduled Virtual - Football Description - Scheduled Virtual - Football is a virtual football game where players can place real money bets on the outcomes of virtual football matches within a simulated football league. League Structure - The virtual football games are organized into different leagues. - Each league consists of 20 teams. - Matches are generated every 3 minutes. - Each match lasts 3 minutes. - The maximum number of goals per match is 6. Gameplay Experience - All players see the same matches and results at the same time, regardless of their location. - Match simulations are created using independent random number generators and algorithms. - Player performance is based on real professional football players, considering factors such as number of goals, fitness, and consecutive match statistics. Viewing Matches - You can watch a video of each of the 10 matches in play by clicking the LIVE button. - Match details such as goals scored, goal times, and team names are displayed below the video for each match. - You can switch to view any of the 9 other matches available in the same week. League Standings and Historical Data - Live league table standings are displayed at all times. - You can view historical records for a match, including: - Last 5 results of a match attended - Head-to-head results - Average goals in the last five matches - League tables and results history are available for reference.
