Virtual Football: Betting Rules and Available Markets

Virtual Football - Betting and Markets Betting Rules - Betting is allowed up to 10 seconds before kick-off. - Betting options for future match days of the current event remain open. - Virtual Football bets cannot be combined with real sports bets in the same betslip. Available Betting Markets 1. Final Result - Predict who will win the match: Home team (1), Draw (X), or Away team (2). 2. Double Chance - Bet on two out of three possible outcomes. The bet wins if either of your two predictions is correct. - 1/X: Home team wins or Draw - X/2: Draw or Away team wins - 1/2: Home team wins or Away team wins 3. Halftime/Fulltime - Predict the result at halftime and at fulltime. Both predictions must be correct to win. - Possible selections: 1/1, 1/X, 1/2, X/1, X/X, X/2, 2/1, 2/X, 2/2 4. Goal Goal/No Goal - Predict whether both teams will score at least one goal (Goal Goal) or at least one team will not score (No Goal). 5. Over/Under - Bet on whether the total number of goals scored during the match will be over or under a specified number (e.g., Over 1.5 means 2 or more goals must be scored to win). 6. 1X2 + Over/Under - Combine a match result prediction (Home win, Draw, Away win) with an Over/Under prediction. Both must be correct to win (e.g., Home + Over 1.5: Home team wins and more than 1.5 goals are scored). 7. Total Goals - Predict the total number of goals that will be scored in a match. 8. Correct Score - Predict the exact final score of the match (Home-Away, e.g., 1-0 means Home scores 1, Away scores 0). 9. Multigoal - Predict the range in which the total number of goals scored in a match will fall (e.g., 0-2 means between 0 and 2 goals). 10. Asian Handicap - Bet on the team that will win when a handicap is applied (e.g., -1: the selected team must win by 2 or more goals to win the bet; if they win by 1, the bet is void; if they draw or lose, the bet is lost).
