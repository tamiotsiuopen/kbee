Virtual Bikes: Betting Options and Market Types

Virtual Bikes - Betting and Markets Betting Overview Betting is permitted up to 10 seconds before the race starts. Betting options for future races on the current race day remain open until their respective cut-off times. Available Betting Options 1. Win - Bet on a selected bike to win the race. Example: Bike No.1 finishes 1st. 2. Place (1-2) - Bet on a selected bike to finish 1st or 2nd in the race. Example: Bike No.1 finishes 1st or 2nd. 3. Show (1-3) - Bet on a selected bike to finish 1st, 2nd, or 3rd in the race. Example: Bike No.1 finishes 1st, 2nd, or 3rd. 4. Even/Odd - Bet on whether a bike with an even number (2 or 4) or odd number (1 or 3) will finish 1st in the race. 5. Over/Under - Bet on whether the winner's bike number is over or under 3.5. - Over: Bike No.3, No.4, or No.5 finishes 1st. - Under: Bike No.1, No.2, or No.3 finishes 1st. 6. Exacta - Bet on two bikes to finish 1st and 2nd in the exact order predicted. Both predictions must be correct to win. Example: Bike No.1 finishes 1st, Bike No.2 finishes 2nd. 7. Quinella - Bet on two bikes to finish 1st and 2nd in any order. Both predictions must be correct to win. Example: Bike No.1 and Bike No.2 finish 1st and 2nd in any order. 8. Trifecta - Bet on three bikes to finish 1st, 2nd, and 3rd in the exact order predicted. All three predictions must be correct to win. Example: Bike No.1 finishes 1st, Bike No.2 finishes 2nd, Bike No.3 finishes 3rd.
