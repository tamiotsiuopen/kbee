Virtual Bikes: How Race Events Work

Virtual Bikes - Race Events Overview Race events in Virtual Bikes are shown continuously. A new race event begins as soon as the current one finishes. Each race event lasts approximately 3 minutes and has 6 participants. Watching and Interacting with Races - You can watch the video of any of the 10 matches in play at any time by clicking the LIVE button. - Details such as positions, players, and odds for win, place, and show markets are displayed below the video. Accessing Market Information and Statistics - To view all available markets, select GO TO ALL MARKETS and click on the relevant option. - Historical statistics of participants are available in this section. Results History - The RESULTS HISTORY section provides past race outcomes for your reference.
