Betting Options and Market Rules for Virtual Greyhounds (Dogs)

Virtual Greyhounds (Dogs) - Betting and Markets Overview This section explains the available betting options and market rules for Virtual Greyhounds (Dogs). Betting Rules - Betting is allowed up to 10 seconds before the race starts. - Betting options for future races of the current race event remain open. Available Betting Options 1. Winner - Bet on the selected dog to win the race. - Example: Dog No.1 finishes 1st. 2. Place (1-2) - Bet on the selected dog to finish 1st or 2nd position in a race. - Example: Dog No.1 finishes 1st or 2nd. 3. Show (1-3) - Bet on the selected dog to finish 1st, 2nd, or 3rd position in the race. - Example: Dog No.1 finishes 1st, 2nd, or 3rd. 4. Even/Odd - Bet on whether an even-numbered (2/4/6) or odd-numbered (1/3/5) dog will finish 1st. 5. Over/Under - Bet on whether the winning dog number is over or under 3.5. - Over: Dog No.4, No.5, or No.6 finishes 1st. - Under: Dog No.1, No.2, or No.3 finishes 1st. 6. Exacta - Bet on the 2 dogs finishing in 1st and 2nd positions, in the exact order predicted. - Both predictions must be correct for the bet to win. - Example: Dog No.1 finishes 1st, Dog No.2 finishes 2nd. 7. Quinella - Bet on the 2 dogs you expect to finish 1st and 2nd in any order. - Both predictions must be correct for the bet to win. - Example: Dog No.1 and No.2 finish 1st and 2nd in any order. 8. Trifecta - Bet on the 3 dogs you expect to finish 1st, 2nd, and 3rd in the correct order. - All three predictions must be correct for the bet to win. - Example: Dog No.1 finishes 1st, Dog No.2 finishes 2nd, Dog No.3 finishes 3rd.
