How to Watch and Follow Virtual Greyhounds (Dogs) Race Events

Virtual Greyhounds (Dogs) - Race Events Overview - Race events are shown continuously in Virtual Greyhounds (Dogs). - A new race starts as soon as the current one finishes. - Each race lasts approximately 2 minutes. - Each race has a fixed number of 6 participants. Watching and Following Races - You can watch the video of any of the 10 matches in play by clicking the LIVE button. - While watching, details such as positions, players, and odds for win/place/show markets are displayed below the video. Accessing Statistics and Results - To view historical statistics of participants, go to ALL MARKETS and click on the relevant option. - RESULTS HISTORY is available for your reference to check past outcomes. Note - If you have tried the above solutions and still need assistance, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
