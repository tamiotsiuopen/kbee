How to Play and Bet on Virtual Horses

How to Play Virtual Horses Overview This guide explains how to participate in Virtual Horses events, place bets, and view your betting history. Steps to Play Virtual Horses 1. Select an available event. You may place bets on any match event after the current one. 2. Choose your bet option for any available match. Click on the odds for the bet you wish to place. 3. The betslip will open with your selection. After making all your selections, enter your stake in the betslip and click on Place Bet. Confirm your bet to place it. 4. If your bet wins, your winnings will be automatically credited to your account. 5. To view your Virtual Horses bets, go to the Virtuals page and click on MY BETS next to BETSLIP. Restrictions - Virtual Horse Racing bets cannot be combined in the same betslip with bets placed on real sport events.
