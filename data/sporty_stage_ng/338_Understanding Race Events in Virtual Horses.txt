Understanding Race Events in Virtual Horses

Virtual Horses - Race Events Overview of Race Events - Race events are shown continuously. A new race event begins as soon as the current one finishes. - Each race event includes 6 participants. - Each race lasts approximately 120 seconds. Watching Live Races - You can watch the video of any of the 10 matches in play at any time by clicking the LIVE button. - While watching a match, details such as positions, players, and odds for win, place, and show markets are displayed below the video. Accessing Market Information and Statistics - To view historical statistics of participants, go to ALL MARKETS and click on the relevant option. - RESULTS HISTORY is also available for reference to review past race outcomes.
