Virtual Horses: Betting Options and Market Types

Virtual Horses - Betting and Markets Betting Overview - Betting is allowed up to 10 seconds before the race start. - Betting options for future races of the current race days remain open. - You can select any bet option for all available matches. - To place a bet, click on the odds relating to the bet you wish to place. Available Betting Options 1. Win - Bet on the selected horse to win the race (for example, Horse No.1 finishes 1st). 2. Place (1-2) - Bet on the selected horse to finish in 1st or 2nd position in a race (for example, Horse No.1 finishes 1st or 2nd). 3. Show (1-3) - Bet on the selected horse to finish 1st, 2nd, or 3rd position in the race (for example, Horse No.1 finishes 1st, 2nd, or 3rd). 4. Even/Odd - Bet on whether the winning horse's number is even (2/4/6) or odd (1/3/5). 5. Over/Under - Bet if the winning horse is over or under No. 3.5. - Selection Over: Horse No.4, No.5, or No.6 finishes 1st. - Selection Under: Horse No.1, No.2, or No.3 finishes 1st. 6. Exacta - Bet on the 2 horses finishing in 1st and 2nd positions, in the exact order predicted. Both predictions must be correct for the bet to win (for example, Horse No.1 finishes 1st, Horse No.2 finishes 2nd). 7. Quinella - Bet on the 2 horses you expect to finish 1st and 2nd in any order. Both predictions must be correct for the bet to win (for example, Horses No.1 and No.2 finish 1st and 2nd in any order). 8. Trifecta - Bet on the 3 horses you expect to finish 1st, 2nd, and 3rd in the correct order. All three predictions must be correct for the bet to win (for example, Horse No.1 finishes 1st, Horse No.2 finishes 2nd, Horse No.3 finishes 3rd).
