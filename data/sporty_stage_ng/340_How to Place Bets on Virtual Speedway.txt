How to Place Bets on Virtual Speedway

How to Play - Virtual Speedway Overview Virtual Speedway allows you to place bets on scheduled virtual speedway racing events. How to Place a Bet 1. Select an available event. You may bet on any match event after the current one. 2. Choose your preferred bet option for any available match. Click on the odds for the bet you wish to place. 3. The betslip will open with your selection. After making all your selections, enter your stake in the betslip and click on Place Bet. 4. After confirming, your bet will be placed. Winnings - If your bet wins, your winnings will automatically be credited to your account. Viewing Your Bets - To see your Virtual Speedway bets, go to the Virtuals page and click on MY BETS next to BETSLIP. Important Note - Virtual Speedway Racing bets cannot be combined in the same betslip with bets placed on real sport events.
