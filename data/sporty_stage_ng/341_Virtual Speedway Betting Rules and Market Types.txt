Virtual Speedway Betting Rules and Market Types

Virtual Speedway - Betting and Markets Betting Rules - Betting is allowed up to 10 seconds before the race start. - Betting options for future races of the current race days remain open. Available Betting Options 1. Win - Bet on the selected Motorbike to win the race. - Example: Motorbike No.1 finishes 1st. 2. Place (1-2) - Bet on the selected Motorbike to finish 1st or 2nd position in a race. - Example: Motorbike No.1 finishes 1st or 2nd. 3. Even/Odd - Bet on whether an Even (2/4) or Odd (1/3) numbered Motorbike will finish 1st in a race. 4. Over/Under - Bet on whether the winner's Motorbike number is over or under 2.5. - Over: Motorbike No.3 or No.4 finishes 1st. - Under: Motorbike No.1 or No.2 finishes 1st. 5. Exacta - Bet on two Motorbikes to finish 1st and 2nd in the exact order predicted. - Both predictions must be correct for the bet to win. - Example: Selection 1, 2 means Motorbike No.1 finishes 1st and Motorbike No.2 finishes 2nd. 6. Quinella - Bet on two Motorbikes to finish 1st and 2nd in any order. - Both predictions must be correct for the bet to win. - Example: Selection 1 & 2 means Motorbike No.1 and No.2 finish 1st and 2nd in any order.
