Virtual Speedway: Race Events and Viewing Guide

Virtual Speedway - Race Events Overview - Race events are held continuously in Virtual Speedway at MotoArena Torun. - A new race event begins immediately after the previous one ends. - Each race event lasts approximately 2 minutes. - Each race features a fixed number of 4 participants. Watching Races - You can watch the video of any of the 10 matches currently in play by clicking the LIVE button. - While watching a match, details such as positions, players, and odds for win, place, and show markets are displayed below the video. Accessing Additional Information - To view historical statistics of participants, use the GO TO ALL MARKETS section and select the relevant option. - RESULTS HISTORY is available for your reference to review past outcomes.
