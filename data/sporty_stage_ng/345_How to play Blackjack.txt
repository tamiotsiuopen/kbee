How to play Blackjack

Understanding the Blackjack Game Blackjack is one of the most popular and strategic card games found in online casinos. The goal of Blackjack is simple: get a hand total closer to 21 than the dealer without going over 21. Each round starts with both the player and dealer receiving cards. Numbered cards are worth their face value. Face cards (J, Q, K) are worth 10. Aces can be worth 1 or 11, depending on what benefits the player’s hand. Players make decisions such as Hit, Stand, Double Down, or Split, depending on their cards.
