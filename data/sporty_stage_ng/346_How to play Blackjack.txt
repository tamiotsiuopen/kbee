Common Cases Where Players Lose Bets in Blackjack 1. Busting (Going Over 21) Description: Players often lose by asking for too many cards. If your total exceeds 21, your hand automatically loses—even if the dealer also busts later. Example: You have 15 and choose to hit. You receive a 9, making your total 24. You immediately lose. 2. Dealer Beating the Player with a Higher Total Description: If the dealer’s final card total is closer to 21 than yours, you lose—even if you didn’t bust.
