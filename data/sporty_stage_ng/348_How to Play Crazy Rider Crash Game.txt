How to Play Crazy Rider Crash Game

Crazy Rider About the Game Crazy Rider is a crash game where a biker launches off a cliff and rides through the sky. The multiplier increases the longer the biker rides. The objective is to cash out before the crash event, which occurs when the biker lets go of the bike and opens his parachute. Players must decide when to cash out: waiting longer increases potential winnings but also the risk of losing the bet if the biker crashes first. How to Access Crazy Rider on SportyBet 1. Log in to your SportyBet account. 2. Go to the Games or Casino section. 3. Find Crazy Rider under Crash Games. 4. Click Crazy Rider to start playing. Placing Bets on Crazy Rider - Choose your stake: Select the amount you want to wager for the round. - Start the ride: Confirm your bet to begin the biker's journey. - Cash out: Click 'Cash Out' at any time before the crash to secure your winnings. Watching the Ride - The biker rides into the sky as the multiplier increases. - If you cash out before the crash, your winnings are secured. - If you wait too long and the biker crashes, you lose your bet. How Payouts Work - Winnings are calculated as: Stake multiplied by the multiplier at the moment of cash out. - Example: If you bet ₦500 and cash out at 3.0x, you win ₦1,500. - If you do not cash out before the crash, you lose your stake.
