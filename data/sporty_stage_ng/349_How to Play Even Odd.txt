How to Play Even Odd

About the Game Even-Odd is a fast and exciting dice-based game that puts your prediction skills to the test. The goal is simple: predict whether the total of three dice will be EVEN or ODD. With quick rounds and instant outcomes, the game offers nonstop action and the chance to double your stake on every win. ﻿How the Game Works The game is played using three dice. Once you place your bet, the dice are drawn one after another. The numbers shown on the dice are added together to determine the final total. If the total is EVEN and you predicted EVEN, you win. If the total is ODD and you predicted ODD, you win. Every winning round pays DOUBLE (2X) your stake. ⚠️ Important: If all three dice show the same number (a Triple), the bet is automatically lost, regardless of your selection. Placing Your Bet Players can easily set their stake by: Adjusting the slider between the minimum and maximum bet limits, or Selecting an amount using the betting chips. For faster gameplay, enabling One-Tap Bet removes bet confirmation prompts, allowing you to place bets instantly. Free Bet Gifts Even-Odd also supports Free Bet Gifts, giving players additional chances to play and win: Available Free Bet Gifts can be viewed by clicking the Gift icon on the game screen. If no Free Bet Gifts are available, the icon will not appear. You may choose to use either the full gift amount or a partial amount when placing a bet. Free Bet Gifts cannot be combined with real money bets. Only one option can be used per bet.
