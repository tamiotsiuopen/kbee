How to Play Jollof War

About the Game – Jollof Wars is a fast-paced and entertaining prediction game inspired by two of the most celebrated Jollof Rice styles in Africa. The game invites players to make a simple yet exciting decision: which Jollof will win the round — Nigeria or Ghana? With quick gameplay and instant outcomes, every round delivers suspense and fun. Once a bet is placed, the game comes alive as a spoon at the center of the screen spins and randomly turns toward one of the Jollof dishes, determining the winner of the round. Make the right prediction and enjoy DOUBLE (2X) your stake as winnings. How to Play Playing Jollof Wars is straightforward and intuitive: Choose your preferred Jollof Rice option — Nigeria or Ghana. Set your stake by adjusting the slider between the minimum and maximum limits, or by selecting from the available chips. Place your bet and watch the spoon decide the outcome. If the spoon points to your selected dish, you win 2X your stake. If it turns to the other dish, or stops in the middle — meaning both dishes are equally delicious — the bet is lost. Seamless and Fast Gameplay For a smoother experience, players can enable One-Tap Bet, allowing bets to be placed instantly without confirmation pop-ups. This feature is ideal for fast-paced play. You can also review your activity at any time through the Bet History option found in the hamburger menu. Easy Navigation To exit the game and return to the Game Lobby, simply tap the “<” button located at the top-left corner of the screen.
