How to Play One Punch

About the Game 1Punch is a fast-paced crash game inspired by the classic punching-ball arcade machine. A boxer steps up, ready to deliver a powerful punch — and it’s up to you to decide how far that punch will go. The aim is simple: set your target, place your bet, and win if the punch reaches your chosen coefficient. How to Play 1Punch To start, select a Target Coefficient that you believe the punch will reach. Next, choose your Bet Amount. You can then place your bet in one of two ways: Manual Bet: Press the Punch button to place a single bet. Auto Bet: Press the Auto button to place bets automatically. When Auto Bet is active, the same target coefficient and bet amount will be used for every round until you turn it off. Once the bet is placed, the coefficient begins to rise from 1.01x. If the final house coefficient is equal to or higher than your selected target, you win. If the coefficient stops below your target, the bet is lost. Winnings Calculation Your winnings are calculated using a simple formula: Bet Amount × Target Coefficient The higher the target you choose, the higher the potential payout — but with increased risk. House Coefficient & Fair Play In every round, the house coefficient is generated randomly by an algorithm. This ensures that each punch outcome is unpredictable and fair. The coefficient continues to increase until it reaches its final value for that round. Winning Chance The Winning Chance shown in the game is an estimated value. It is calculated dynamically based on cumulative probability and the game’s Return to Player (RTP), helping players make informed decisions. One-Tap Bet For faster gameplay, you can enable One-Tap Bet. This removes bet confirmation prompts, allowing you to place bets instantly with a single tap.
