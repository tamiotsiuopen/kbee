How to Play Pearl Drop Slot Game

Peral Drop About the Game - Pearl Drop is a slot game with 5 fixed paylines. - To win, line up matching symbols from left to right on adjacent reels. - Each spin offers the chance to win payouts multiplied by your bet per line. How to Access Pearl Drop on SportyBet 1. Log in to your SportyBet account. 2. Go to the Slots section and search for Pearl Drop. 3. Open the game, set your bet, and spin the reels. Placing Bets on Pearl Drop - Set your bet per line. The total stake is calculated as: bet per line multiplied by 5 paylines. - Click Spin to start the game. - Wins are displayed immediately after each spin. Winning in Pearl Drop - Symbols pay from left to right on adjacent reels, starting from the leftmost reel. - There are 5 fixed paylines. - If you win on multiple paylines in a single spin, all wins are added to your total. - All wins are multiplied by the bet per line.
