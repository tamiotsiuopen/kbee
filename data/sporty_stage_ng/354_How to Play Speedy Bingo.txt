How to Play Speedy Bingo

Speedy Bingo Overview Speedy Bingo is a fast-paced version of traditional bingo designed for players who enjoy quick gameplay and instant results. The game features short rounds, allowing for multiple games to be played in a short period. Game Setup - Each round begins with 2 bingo cards by default (can be set to 1 card). - Each card contains 15 numbers arranged in a 3x9 grid, with numbers ranging from 1 to 90. Gameplay Game Start - 60 balls are drawn at the start of each round. - Matching numbers on your cards are marked in orange. Winning Conditions - Win on a single card by completing any of the following: - 1 Line: Any horizontal line - 2 Lines: Any two horizontal lines - Full House: All three horizontal lines Scoring and Prizes - Each card is scored separately. - Each prize (1 Line, 2 Lines, Full House) can be won once per card. - Prizes are cumulative; for example, you can win 1 Line, 2 Lines, and Full House on the same card. - Prize calculation: Prize equals Bet multiplied by the prize multiplier. Extra Balls Feature - After the initial 60 balls, you have the option to purchase up to 5 extra balls to increase your chances of winning. Betting and Controls - Your bet is split across all active cards. - The game supports Auto Play and Turbo Mode for faster gameplay. Free Bet Gift - Tap the Gift icon to claim Free Bets if available. - You can use the full or partial value of the Free Bet gift. - Real money and Free Bets cannot be mixed in a single game. - Extra Balls are not available when playing with Free Bets.
