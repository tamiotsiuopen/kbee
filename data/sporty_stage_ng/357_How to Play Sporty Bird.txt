How to Play Sporty Bird

About Sporty Bird Game Sporty Bird is an exciting multilevel crash game where timing, strategy, and quick decisions lead to big rewards. In every round, a bird takes flight and a win multiplier starts climbing. Your goal is simple: cash out before the bird flies away. The longer you stay in the air, the higher the multiplier — but wait too long and you lose your bet. As you continue playing and completing rounds, you’ll unlock new birds, levels, and exclusive rewards, making every session more thrilling than the last. Getting Started Playing Sporty Bird is quick and easy: Place your bet before the round begins. Watch the bird fly as the coefficient increases from 1x upward. Cash out at the right moment to secure your winnings. If you don’t cash out before the bird flies away, the bet is lost. Your winnings are calculated as: Bet Amount × Cashout Coefficient (X) You can place up to two bets at the same time, giving you more flexibility and strategy in every round. Bet & Cash Out Features Manual Cash Out: Press the Cash Out button anytime before the bird flies away to lock in your winnings. Auto Bet: Automatically places your selected bet amount in every new round until you turn it off. Auto Cash Out: Automatically cashes out when your chosen multiplier is reached — perfect for consistent play. Levels, Progress & Rewards Sporty Bird features multiple levels, each with its own bird and progression goals. The number of rounds required to unlock the next level is displayed on the game screen. Completing a level may reward you with Free Bet Gifts. Details of available rewards per level can be viewed in the Levels and Rewards pop-up. The more you play, the more you unlock! Bonus Rounds Bonus Rounds add an extra layer of excitement: A Bonus Meter can appear at any time during gameplay. Keep placing bets and completing rounds to fill the meter.
