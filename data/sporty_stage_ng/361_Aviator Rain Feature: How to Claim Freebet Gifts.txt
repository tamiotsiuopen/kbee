Aviator Rain Feature: How to Claim Freebet Gifts

Aviator Rain Feature Overview Aviator Rain is a feature in the Aviator game that allows users to claim freebet gifts directly from the game's chat section. These gifts are distributed randomly throughout the day and are subject to specific eligibility criteria. Eligibility and Claiming Freebet Gifts - Users must meet specific stake criteria to be eligible for Aviator Rain freebet gifts. - The stake criteria are detailed on the Aviator Rain promotion page. - Once the staking conditions are met within the designated time frame, users can claim the Rain freebet gifts. - Aviator Rain events occur randomly multiple times each day. - The number of rain gifts distributed is limited. Using Freebet Gifts - After claiming, free bet gifts can only be used on Aviator. - Free bets can be accessed from the Hamburger menu within Aviator. - The validity period for each gift is displayed in the Hamburger menu next to the respective gift.
