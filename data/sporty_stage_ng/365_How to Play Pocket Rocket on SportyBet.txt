Common Cases Where Players Lose Bets in Pocket Rocket Timing Failures (Not Cashing Out on Time) - If you wait too long to cash out and the plane crashes before you do, you lose your entire bet. - Example: The plane reaches a 1.5x multiplier, but you wait for 2x. If the plane crashes at 1.7x, you lose your bet. Same Cash-out Odds or Coefficient - The coefficient is the multiplier at which the plane crashes (for example, 1.5x, 2x, 10x). - If you set your cash-out odds exactly at the point where the plane flies away, your bet is settled as lost. Your cash-out odds must be less than the odds at which the plane flies away. - Example: If you set your cash-out at 1.5x and the plane crashes at 1.5x, you lose.
