Sporty Roulette: Game Overview

Overview of Roulette Game Description - Roulette is a game involving a spinning wheel and a small white marble. - The marble is spun around the outside of the bowl until it comes to rest in one of the wheel's divisions. - The wheel divisions are numbered from 1 to 12 in a non-sequential pattern and alternate between red and black colors. - There is also a green division marked with the number 0. Note - For detailed instructions on how to play roulette, refer to the in-game guide.
