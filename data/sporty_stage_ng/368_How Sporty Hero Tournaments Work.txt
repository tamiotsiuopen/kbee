How Sporty Hero Tournaments Work

Sporty Hero Tournaments Overview A Sporty Hero Tournament is a structured competition in the Sporty Hero game where players compete against each other within a set timeframe and according to predefined rules. The objective is to score higher than other participants by collecting the most points or winnings before the tournament ends. How to Participate in a Sporty Hero Tournament 1. Join the tournament by clicking the join button in Sporty Hero. 2. Ensure you meet the required stake criteria to be eligible. 3. The minimum cashout threshold is active during the tournament. 4. The more you stake, the more points you earn. A higher cashout coefficient will also result in higher points. Leaderboard and Rankings - Your rank, points, and potential prize amount are displayed in the tournament's Leaderboard section within Sporty Hero. Terms and Conditions - Full terms and conditions are available in the Prize & Rules section of the tournament. Prizes and Rewards - All tournament winners receive Free Bet Gifts on the following day. - Free Bet Gifts appear in the "Gifts" section of the user's account. - Free Bet rewards can be used on Sporty Hero and have an expiry date, which is specified in the gift details.
