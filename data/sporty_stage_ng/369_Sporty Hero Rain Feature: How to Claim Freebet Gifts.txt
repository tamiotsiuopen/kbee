Sporty Hero Rain Feature: How to Claim Freebet Gifts

Sporty Hero Rain Feature Overview Rain is a feature in Sporty Hero that allows users to claim freebet gifts directly from the game's chat section. These gifts are distributed randomly throughout the day. Eligibility Criteria - Users must meet specific stake criteria to be eligible for Rain freebet gifts. - The stake criteria and other requirements are detailed on the promotion page if the Sporty Hero Rain promotion is active. - Only a limited number of Rain gifts are distributed each day. How to Claim Rain Freebet Gifts 1. Meet the staking conditions within the designated time frame as specified on the promotion page. 2. Once eligible, claim the Rain freebet gift from the chat section. 3. After claiming, the freebet gift will appear in the 'Gifts' section of your account. Usage and Expiry - Rain freebet rewards can be used on Sporty Hero. - Each freebet gift comes with an expiry date, which is mentioned in the gift details. Note If the above solutions have been tried and did not work, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
