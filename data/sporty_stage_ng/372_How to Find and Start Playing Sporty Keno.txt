How to Find and Start Playing Sporty Keno

Where to Find Sporty Keno Overview This guide explains how to locate and start playing Sporty Keno, as well as the available options within the game interface. Steps to Find and Start Sporty Keno 1. Log in to your account. 2. Choose the Games section. 3. Locate Sporty Keno and select Play. Game Interface Options - Start: Begin the game. - How to Play: View the game's rules and payout table. - Top Recent Wins: Check the scoreboard of the game. - Exit: Leave the Sporty Keno game. Additional Information - Your Sporty Account balance is displayed in the top right corner of the screen. - You can mute or unmute the game sound from the same screen.
