How to Play Sporty Keno: Rules and Winning Criteria

Overview of Sporty Keno Game Description Sporty Keno is a game offered by SportyBet that is designed to be fun, exciting, refreshing, and rewarding. Game Rules - Sporty Keno is a numbers-based game. - The game uses numbers from 1 to 80. - In each round, a user can select between 1 and 8 numbers. - After the user makes their selection and places a bet, the House draws 20 numbers. Winning Criteria - If the user's selected numbers (called Hits) match one or more of the numbers drawn by the House, the user wins a prize. - The prize is calculated as a multiplier of the Bet Amount, which is referred to as Odds.
