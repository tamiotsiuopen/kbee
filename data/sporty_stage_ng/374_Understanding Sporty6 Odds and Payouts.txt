Understanding Sporty6 Odds and Payouts

Sporty6 Odds Explanation - In Sporty6, you select six distinct numbers from 1 to 48. The system draws 35 winning numbers. - You win if all six numbers are present in the 35 drawn. - Odds are not fixed; they depend on how quickly your numbers appear in the sequence. - To receive 10,000 odds, all six numbers must be among the first six drawn. - If your sixth number appears later (e.g., 15th, 25th, or 34th), odds will be lower (e.g., odds of 2 if matched by the 34th number). - If not all six numbers are matched, it is a lost bet. - Refer to the in-game How to Play section and payout chart for full details.
