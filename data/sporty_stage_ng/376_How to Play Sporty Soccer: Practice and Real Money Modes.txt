How to Play Sporty Soccer: Practice and Real Money Modes

How to play Sporty Soccer Getting Started - To begin playing Sporty Soccer, you must first pass the training course by hitting the target 3 times in the tutorial. - You can practice for free on the Practice page or play for real money on the Play page. Practice Mode - Flick the ball to hit the target as many times as possible. - Your highest score will be recorded. - If you are among the top 10 players, you can appear on the global leaderboard. - Your current global rank is displayed at the bottom of the leaderboard. Real Money Mode - Choose a stake to place your bet and press the PLAY button to start the game. - Flick the ball to hit the target. Gameplay Outcomes 1. If you hit the target: - Rounds 1-9: You win the bet and can either Challenge (continue to the next round) or Cashout within 30 seconds. - Clicking Challenge adds your new winnings to your previous Total Stake and restakes it for the next round. - If you do not choose within 30 seconds, the system will automatically Cashout for you. - Round 10: If you hit the target, an automatic Cashout occurs and you win the maximum payout. 2. If you miss the target: - You lose the Total Stake and return to the Betting Menu to place another bet. Additional Rules - On average, a Random Event will occur on 10% of your shots, causing you to lose the round regardless of your aim. - You can hit the target up to 10 times in a single session.
