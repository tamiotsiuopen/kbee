Sporty Soccer: Game Overview and Terminology

Overview of Sporty Soccer Introduction Sporty Soccer is an easy and fun game that offers players the opportunity to win money. Real Money Mode Definitions 1. Real Money Mode - The mode of the game where actual money is staked. 2. Bet Menu / Betting Menu - The screen where you can choose your stake and start the first round. 3. Shot - Any attempt to hit the target. 4. Round - Any one of the 10 attempts to hit the target. Each round consists of one shot. 5. Game Session - The container for all rounds. Each game session includes 10 rounds. 6. Initial Stake - The initial amount of money bet or staked. 7. Total Stake - The current stake, which includes winnings from previous rounds that have been re-staked. For example, if a user stakes 1, makes their first shot, and continues to the next round, their total stake is now 1.07. 8. Pot Winning - The amount a user will win if they successfully hit the target in the current round. 9. UFO - The UFO that takes the ball away, causing the shot to miss, the round to be lost, and the game session to end. 10. Lightning Strike - The lightning strike that hits the ball, causing the shot to miss, the round to be lost, and the game session to end. 11. Random Event - Either the UFO or Lightning Strike. 12. Challenge - To add your winnings to your previous total stake and proceed to the next round. 13. Cashout - Taking your initial stake and winnings, ending the game without challenging the next round.
