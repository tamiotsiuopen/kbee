Betting Markets and Their Definitions in Instant Virtuals

Instant Virtuals - Betting and Markets Overview Sporty Instant Virtuals offers a variety of betting markets for virtual sports matches. Each market type allows users to place different kinds of bets on match outcomes and events. Market Types and Definitions 1X2 - Bet on the overall outcome of the match. - Three possible outcomes: - 1: Home team wins - X: Draw - 2: Away team wins Over/Under - Bet on whether the total number of goals scored during the match is over or under a specified number (for example, 1.5). Double Chance - Bet on two possible outcomes combined: - 1X: Home team wins or draws - X2: Away team wins or draws - 12: Home team wins or away team wins GG/NG (Both Teams to Score) - GG: Both teams score at least one goal during the match - NG: One or both teams do not score any goal during the match Handicap - The final result is calculated by adding a handicap to one team: - (0:1): Away team starts with one goal advantage - (1:0): Home team starts with one goal advantage Both Team to Score - Bet on which team(s) will score during match time. There are four possible outcomes.
