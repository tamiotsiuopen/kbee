How to Play Instant Basketball and Available Markets

Instant Basketball and Betting Markets Playing Instant Basketball - Log in to your SportyBet account. - Click the A-Z menu at the bottom of the page, then select Virtuals at the top. - Choose Instant Basketball. - Select from available betting markets: Winner, Over/Under, Home Team Total, Away Team Total, Handicap, Winner and Total, 1X2. - Enter your bet amount and confirm to place your bet.
