How to Play Instant Dog Racing

Overview of Instant Dog Racing Game Description - Instant Dog Racing is an instant virtuals game where players place real-money bets on computer-generated greyhound races. - Race outcomes are randomly generated, but the simulation maintains the strengths and speeds of virtual dogs to closely match real-world counterparts, making the odds feel authentic. - Each round features six competing dogs and a 25-second live race video for a real-time experience. - Players can choose to watch the live race with dynamic camera angles and immersive commentary or click the Skip to Result button for immediate results. - All race results are determined using independent random number generators to ensure fairness and unpredictability. Available Betting Markets - Main: Predict which dog will finish 1st (Win), top 2 (Place), or top 3 (Show). - Odd/Even: Predict whether the winning dog's number will be odd or even. - Exacta: Predict the 1st and 2nd place dogs in the exact order. - Quinella: Predict the 1st and 2nd place dogs in any order. - Trifecta: Predict the 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place dogs in the exact order. - Over/Under 3.5: Predict whether the winning dog's number will be over or under 3.5. How to Play 1. Log in to your SportyBet account, go to Virtuals, and select Instant Dog Racing. 2. Make your bet selection and enter your stake amount. 3. Confirm your selection. 4. Optionally, click Skip to Result to see the results instantly. How to Check Your Instant Dog Racing Bet History 1. Log in to your SportyBet account, go to Virtuals, and click on Instant Dog Racing. 2. Click the icon at the top right-hand corner of the page to view your Instant Dog Racing Bet History. Platform Availability - Instant Dog Racing is currently accessible only via the mobile website. - The game will be launching soon on both Android and iOS apps.
