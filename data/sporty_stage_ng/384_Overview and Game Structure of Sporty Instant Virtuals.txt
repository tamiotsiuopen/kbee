Overview and Game Structure of Sporty Instant Virtuals

Overview of Instant Virtuals Description - Sporty Instant Virtuals is an instant virtuals game where you can place real-money bets on computer-generated sports matches. - The matches are randomly generated, but the simulation keeps the strengths of virtual teams close to real-world teams, resulting in odds similar to those of real sports matches. Game Structure - Each round consists of 10 matches. - Each round features a live video that lasts 25 seconds, with all matches running simultaneously. - As this is an instant virtuals game, you can click the skip button to bypass the live video and receive your result immediately. - The live video shown for each round is randomly selected from the matches you have bet on. - If you bet on multiple matches, the match being shown in the live video is indicated by a green football icon next to it. Technical Details - All match simulations are created using independent random number generators.
