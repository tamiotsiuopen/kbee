How to Play 6+ Poker (Short-Deck Hold'em)

How to Play 6+ Poker Overview 6+ Poker, also known as Short-Deck Hold'em, is a variant of poker similar to Texas Hold'em. The game is played between two sides: the Player and the Dealer. Each side aims to form the best five-card poker hand using any combination of seven cards (five community cards and two pocket cards). The game uses a 36-card deck (cards 6 through Ace), which changes the probabilities and hand rankings. Notable differences include: - Flush ranks higher than a full house. - Three of a kind beats a straight. - Aces are the highest card and can be used for both low and high straights (lowest: A-6-7-8-9, highest: 10-J-Q-K-A).
