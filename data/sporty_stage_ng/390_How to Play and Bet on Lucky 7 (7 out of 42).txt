How to Play and Bet on Lucky 7 (7 out of 42)

 Total sum of numbers is 903 (yellow balls sum to 451, black balls to 452). - Drum: Part of the machine where balls are placed after presentation, then shuffled for selection. - Tube: Part where balls are mixed and drawn. - Odds: Multiplier set by the organizer, used to calculate winnings. Rules - 7 winning balls are drawn from 42. - A draw is valid only if at least 7 winning balls are selected. Otherwise, the draw is void and all stakes are returned. - If more than 7 balls are drawn, only the first 7 are counted. Gameplay - Only one betting round per draw. - Betting round lasts about 4 minutes and starts immediately after the previous draw ends. - Broadcast starts after the betting round ends. Betting Limits - Minimum and maximum betting limits are set by the gambling company. Special Cases Cancelled Draws - Draws may be cancelled due to technical issues (internet connection, studio problems, presenter mistakes). - Presenter mistakes that can result in cancelled draws include: - Balls not prepared before the draw starts. - Balls not visible on screen due to presenter actions. - If video and audio streams do not match or are missing: - If sound is lost or the presenter announces incorrect numbers, the video broadcast determines the official results. - If a player cannot see the live broadcast for technical reasons on their end but the broadcast is available in the archive, the draw is considered valid. Game Organization Procedure - All balls must be clearly visible in the game machine before each draw. - Draws run 24/7 every 4 minutes, with maintenance breaks as needed. - The organizer may change the time and duration of broadcasts. - Longer breaks (1-5 cancelled draws in a row) may occur due to technical failures; all bets for these draws are void and stakes are returned. - Draw results and broadcast archives are available on the organizer's website.
