How to Play Lucky 5 (5 out of 36)

How to Play Lucky 5 (or 5 out of 36) Overview Lucky 5, also known as 5 out of 36, is a lottery-based game where players can place bets on a variety of outcomes. Winning bets can return profits up to a thousand times the stake. The game involves randomly drawing 5 winning balls out of 36 using a mechanical game machine. Draws occur every 4 minutes, daily. Game Process - Five winning balls are randomly drawn into the cylinders of the game machine. - The order of the winning balls is determined by the cylinders from left to right; the leftmost cylinder is considered first. - There is only one betting round per draw, and players can place bets on all available outcomes for the upcoming draw. - The betting round lasts about 3 minutes and takes place between game draws. How to Place a Bet 1. Log in to your account. 2. Make a deposit if needed. 3. Select a betting category and choose the outcome of the draw. 4. Press Confirm to continue. 5. Enter your stake in the 'Amount' field of the bet slip. 6. Press the Place bet button. 7. A message saying 'Bet accepted' will appear after your bet is placed. Subscription Betting - When placing a bet, select the number of draws you wish to participate in. - Enter your stake in the 'Amount' field. - Press the Place bet button. Combination Bets - A combination bet consists of two different bets. To win, both bets must be successful. - Winnings are calculated by multiplying the stake by the odds. - It is not possible to combine (parlay) card games bets. Steps to place a combination bet: 1. Select a betting category and choose the outcome of the draw. 2. Add another bet to your bet slip. 3. Enter your stake in the 'Amount' field. 4. Press the Place bet button. Checking Draw Results and Bet History - Each draw has a unique number. Results can be found on the 'Results' page. - Betting history can be checked on the 'Bet history' page.
