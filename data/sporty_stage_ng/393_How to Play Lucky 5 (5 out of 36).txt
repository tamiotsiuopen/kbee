Sums by color: white 45, green 126, red 207, blue 288. - Drum: Part of the machine where balls are mixed and drawn from. - Cylinders: Five identical parts connected to the drum where winning balls are drawn. - Mixing and Selection process: Starts when all balls fall into the drum and ends when all 5 winning balls are selected into the cylinders. - Odds: Numerical value set by the organizer, multiplied by the stake to determine winnings. Rules - Five winning balls are randomly drawn into the cylinders. - The draw is considered complete when the Mixing and Selection process is finished. - If not complete, the draw is void and stakes are returned, or the process continues until 5 balls are selected. - The order of winning balls is determined by the cylinders from left to right. The filling order of cylinders does not affect the result. Gameplay - Only one betting round per draw. - Bets can be placed on all available outcomes for the upcoming draw. - The betting round lasts about four minutes, starting immediately after the previous draw ends. Betting Limits - Maximum and minimum betting limits are set by the gambling company. Special Cases Cancelled Draws - Draws may be cancelled due to technical failures (e.g., internet issues, studio problems, presenter mistakes). - Presenter mistakes that can lead to cancelled draws include: - Balls not prepared before the draw starts. - Balls not visible on screen due to dealer actions. Continuing the Mixing and Selection Process - If fewer than 5 balls are selected when the process ends, it may be continued. - If selected balls fall back into the drum during the process, it may be continued.
