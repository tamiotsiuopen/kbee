Game Organization Procedure - Balls must be clearly visible in the machine before each draw. - Game draws run 24/7 every 4 minutes, with maintenance breaks as needed. - The organizer may change the time and duration of broadcasts. - Longer breaks (1-5 cancelled draws in a row) are allowed due to technical failures; all bets on these draws are void and stakes are returned. - Results and broadcast archives are available on the organizer's website. Equipment Used - Game machine as described above. - Set of 36 balls as described above. Additional Information - The English version of the rules is the legal basis for the relationship between player and operator. In case of discrepancies with other languages, the English version prevails. - Any claims related to draw results or technical issues must be made to the operator within 30 days of the draw in question. Note If you have tried all the above solutions and still experience issues, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
