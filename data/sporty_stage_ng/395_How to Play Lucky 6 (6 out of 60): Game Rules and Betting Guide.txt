How to Play Lucky 6 (6 out of 60): Game Rules and Betting Guide

How to Play Lucky 6 (or 6 out of 60) Overview Lucky 6, also known as 6 out of 60, is a dynamic lottery-based game where players can bet on a wide range of outcomes. Six winning balls are randomly drawn from a set of 60 using a game machine. The order of the winning balls is determined by the position of the machine's cylinders, from left to right. Betting rounds occur between draws, lasting about 3 minutes, and draws take place every 4 minutes daily. Game Process - Six winning balls are drawn from 60 balls into the cylinders of the game machine. - The order of winning balls is determined by the position of the cylinders, left to right. - There is only one betting round per draw. - Players can bet on all available outcomes for the upcoming draw. - Each betting round lasts about 3 minutes. - Draws occur every 4 minutes, 24/7, with maintenance breaks as needed. How to Place a Bet 1. Log in to your account. 2. Make a deposit if needed. 3. Select a betting category and choose the outcome of the draw. 4. Press Confirm to continue. 5. Enter your stake in the bet slip field labeled Amount. 6. Press the Place bet button. 7. A message 'Bet accepted' will appear after your bet is placed. Subscription Betting - When placing a bet, select the number of draws you wish to participate in. - Enter your stake in the Amount field. - Press the Place bet button. Combination Bets - A combination bet consists of two different bets. To win, both must be successful. - Winnings are calculated by multiplying the stake by the odds. - Card games bets cannot be combined (parlayed) with Lucky 6 bets. Steps to place a combination bet: 1. Select a betting category and choose the outcome of the draw. 2. Add one more bet to your bet slip. 3. Enter your stake in the Amount field. 4. Press the Place bet button. Checking Draw Results and Bet History - Each draw has a unique number. - Results can be found on the Results page. - Betting history can be checked on the Bet history page.
