How to Play Speedy 7 Live Game

How to Play Speedy 7 Overview Speedy 7 is a live betting game where players predict whether the next card will be black or red over a sequence of seven betting rounds. Players may cash out their winnings after any successful prediction or continue playing to attempt a bigger win. Bonus prizes are awarded for making four or seven correct successive picks. Game Rules Definitions - Betting round: The period when players can place bets. - Odds: The numerical value based on the probability of an outcome, used to calculate winnings. - Suit: One of the four card types (Hearts, Diamonds, Spades, Clubs). - Colour: Red (Hearts, Diamonds) or Black (Spades, Clubs). - Deck: 52 cards, 13 from each suit. - Shoe: Device holding the deck before the game starts. - Draw: The sequence from the first betting round to the seventh card being revealed. - Betting outcome: The result of a bet, expressed in odds, which may change during the draw. - Betting options: All possible betting outcomes available to the player. - Lucky streak: The number of consecutive bets won in one draw without missing any betting rounds. General Rules - Players predict the next face-up card; correct consecutive predictions start a streak. - Only one streak per player at a time. - Players can join in any betting round of a draw. - Players can cash out during any betting round. - After cashing out, players may start a new streak, even in the same draw. - The lucky streak ends after the seventh card is dealt, or if the player does not make a choice or cashes out. Dealing Process - Cards are dealt one by one after each betting round until seven cards are on the table. - The betting round is active before the first card and after each dealt card, except after the seventh card. - The draw ends when the seventh card is dealt, and winnings are determined and paid to players who have not yet cashed out.
