Card Shuffling - Two decks are used: one for dealing and one for shuffling. - At the end of each draw, the used deck is collected and shuffled for the next draw. - The previously shuffled deck is used for the next round of dealing. Bonuses - 4-Card Streak Bonus: Awarded to players who correctly guess four cards in a row in the same draw without cashing out. The bonus is paid on the initial stake, regardless of the game's subsequent outcome. - 7-Card Streak Bonus: Awarded to players who start from the first betting round and correctly guess all seven cards without cashing out. The bonus is paid as additional winnings, calculated by fixed odds multiplied by the initial bet. The fixed odd value is determined by the gambling company. Special Cases - Cancelled draws may occur if: - The scanner cannot read the card or readings do not match the card on the table. - Cards are misplaced or mixed. - Technical issues occur in the studio (e.g., internet problems, technical failure, dealer error). - Dealer mistakes include: sequence errors, marked/damaged cards, cards falling off the table, cards turned face-up due to shuffling, incorrect scanning, or two cards drawn in one round. - If a draw is cancelled, the player receives a refund of their stake or accumulated winnings.
