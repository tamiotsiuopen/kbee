How to Play Wheel of Fortune Live Game

How to Play Wheel Of Fortune Overview Wheel of Fortune is a fast and simple live game with clear betting options. The game is presented live, with a presenter spinning a wheel, and players bet on the outcome of each spin. Game Process - The presenter introduces the Wheel of Fortune and spins it counter-clockwise, then clockwise with a light hand stroke. - Only one spin is made during a draw unless a repeat is required. - The spin and draw are valid if the wheel makes at least three full spins clockwise. A green light and spin counter confirm this. - The spin starts when the wheel begins spinning clockwise and the pointer leaves its previous sector. - The result is determined by the sector number or symbol where the pointer lands after the wheel stops. - There is one betting round per draw, and players can place bets on all available outcomes. - Each betting round lasts about two minutes, and draws occur every two minutes daily. How to Place a Bet 1. Log in to your account. 2. Make a deposit if needed. 3. Select a betting category and choose the outcome of the draw. 4. Press Confirm to continue. 5. Enter your stake in the Amount field on the bet slip. 6. Press the Place bet button. 7. A message 'Bet accepted' will appear after your bet is placed. Subscription Betting - When placing a bet, select the number of draws you wish to participate in. - Enter your stake in the Amount field. - Press the Place bet button. Combination Bets - A combination bet consists of two different bets; both must win for the combination to win. - Winnings are calculated by multiplying the stake by the odds. - Card games bets cannot be combined (parlayed). - To place a combination bet: 1. Select a betting category and choose the outcome of the draw. 2. Add one more bet to your bet slip. 3. Enter your stake in the Amount field. 4. Press the Place bet button.
