Free Bet Gift Overview and Rules

Free Bet Gift Overview Definition A Free Bet gift is a type of promotional gift offered by SportyBet. It can be used to place bets without any spending conditions. How Free Bet Gifts Work - When using a Free Bet gift, the amount of the gift is deducted from any potential winnings if the total return is greater than the stake. - Example: If you use a NGN 50.00 Free Bet gift on odds of 4.0, your potential win will be NGN 150.00 instead of NGN 200.00. This is calculated as (Potential Win - Free Bet gift). General Rules for Using Free Bet Gifts - Free Bet gifts can only be used on Single and Multiple Betslips. - They can be used partially with cash. - If a bet that used only a Free Bet gift as the stake is voided, the Free Bet gift will be refunded. - If a bet that used both a Free Bet gift and cash as the stake is voided, both the Free Bet gift and the cash will be refunded. - Only one Free Bet gift can be used per ticket/bet. - Gifts or Free Games in your account cannot be withdrawn to your balance and can only be used to place bets. Important Note SportyBet reserves the right to cancel, revoke, expire, and/or remove any Gift at any time.
