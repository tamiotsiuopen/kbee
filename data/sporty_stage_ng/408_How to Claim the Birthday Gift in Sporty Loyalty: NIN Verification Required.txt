How to Claim the Birthday Gift in Sporty Loyalty: NIN Verification Required

Birthday Gift Verification Overview The Birthday Gift is a special reward available to users on the Sporty Loyalty platform. To claim this gift, users must complete an additional identity verification step. Location of the Birthday Gift - The Birthday Gift can be found in the Ready to Claim tab on the Sporty Loyalty platform. Identity Verification Requirement - NIN (National Identification Number) verification is mandatory to claim the Birthday Gift. - BVN (Bank Verification Number) verification alone is not sufficient. - Even if the user is already BVN verified, NIN verification is still required. Claiming the Birthday Gift: Step-by-Step 1. Log into the Sporty Loyalty platform. 2. Navigate to the Ready to Claim tab. 3. Locate and select the Birthday Gift. 4. When prompted, complete the NIN verification process. Important Notes - Without completing NIN verification, the Birthday Gift cannot be claimed. There are no exceptions to this rule. - Once NIN verification is successfully completed, the Birthday Gift will be unlocked and ready to use. Summary - Gift Name: Birthday Gift - Location: Ready to Claim tab in Sporty Loyalty - Requirement: NIN verification (mandatory, even if BVN verified) - Without NIN: Gift cannot be claimed - With NIN: Gift is successfully unlocked
