How to Participate in Sporty Loyalty Missions

Sporty Loyalty Mission Overview Sporty Loyalty Mission allows users to participate in curated challenges for the chance to earn rewards. Upon successful completion of a mission, users receive gifts. Mission Availability - The Mission tab is visible only if you are selected by the system for a mission. - Selection for missions is random. Availability is not guaranteed, and users should not expect missions to always be available. - Once selected, you must accept the mission to begin. Mission Rules - The rules and conditions may differ for each mission. - Review the rules of each mission carefully before participating. Viewing Active Missions To view your active missions: 1. From the SportyBet homepage, tap on ME in the bottom right corner. 2. Select Sporty Loyalty. 3. Tap on Missions. - The conditions for each mission can be read in detail in the Mission section. How to Participate in a Sporty Loyalty Mission - On the Sporty Loyalty page, you will see any missions available for your account. - Each mission displays a title and a progress bar. - Complete the requirements shown to fill the progress bar and receive your rewards. Note: If the above solutions have been tried and did not work, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
