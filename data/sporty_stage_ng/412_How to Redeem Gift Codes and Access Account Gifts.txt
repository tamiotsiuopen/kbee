How to Redeem Gift Codes and Access Account Gifts

Gift Codes and Redeeming Gifts Overview - Gifts available to your account can be accessed directly in the gifts section by clicking on your balance. - You do not need a gift code to access gifts that are already available to your account. Redeeming a Gift Code - If you receive a specific code from SportyBet, you can redeem it by entering the code and clicking on 'Redeem your gift code'. Important Notes - Gift Codes are not related to promotions. Note: If you have tried the above solutions and they did not work, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
