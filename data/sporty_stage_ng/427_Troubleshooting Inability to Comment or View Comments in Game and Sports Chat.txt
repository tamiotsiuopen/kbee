Troubleshooting Inability to Comment or View Comments in Game and Sports Chat

I Can't Comment on Chat or See My Comments in the Chat Section of Games or Real Sports Overview This guide explains why you may not be able to comment or see your comments in the chat sections of games or real sports, and outlines steps to participate in interactive chat features. How to Access Chat Features Pre-match Comments - Select the event you are interested in. - Scroll to the comments section. Live Matches - Go to Live Betting. - Choose an ongoing event. - Open the chat box to join the conversation. Game Chats - Pick any game of your choice. - Access the chat box located at the top right corner of the screen. Possible Reasons for Inability to Comment or See Comments - If you cannot chat or comment, or your comments are not visible, you may have been temporarily blocked from using the chat service. Chat Service Rules To ensure a positive experience for all users, follow these rules: 1. Share tips, support others, and engage positively. 2. Keep messages and links relevant; avoid spamming. 3. Treat others with respect; do not use hate speech or insults. 4. Do not share misleading information or sensitive information such as OTPs, financial details, or phone numbers. What to Do if Blocked - Wait for 5 minutes and then try sending a message that meets the above requirements. Note If you have tried the above solutions and are still unable to comment or see your comments, the case should be handed off to a human agent. Additional Guidance - Always be respectful of other players. - Help maintain a positive and safe chat environment for everyone.
