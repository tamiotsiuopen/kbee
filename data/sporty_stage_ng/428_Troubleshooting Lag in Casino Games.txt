Troubleshooting Lag in Casino Games

Why are my casino games lagging? Common Causes and Solutions 1. Stable Internet Connection - Ensure you are connected to a stable internet connection. - If your connection is weak, try moving to an area with a better network signal. 2. Update the App - If you are using the app, make sure it is updated to the latest version. 3. Clear Cache and Cookies - If you are using the SportyBet website or app on a mobile phone, clear the cache and cookies. - Ensure your browser is up to date if you are playing on the web. Required Information for Further Assistance If the above solutions have been tried and did not work, the case should be handed off to a human agent. Please gather the following information: - Device model - Platform (web, app, or wap) - Android/iOS version - SportyBet app version installed on the device - Game name (if the issue occurs while playing a specific game)
