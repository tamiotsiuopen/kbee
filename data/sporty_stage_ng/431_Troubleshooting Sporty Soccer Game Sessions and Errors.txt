Troubleshooting Sporty Soccer Game Sessions and Errors

Important Information - In Real Money Mode, if you do not shoot the ball for any reason, your round will remain active for 14 days. - Each time you open Real Money Mode, you will return to your active game session to shoot for the current round. - After 14 days, if you still have a pending round, the system will automatically forfeit your stake and end the game session. - If you reach the shooting screen in Real Money Mode, you must shoot to complete the round within 14 days. Warning: If you see a popup dialogue showing 'Illegal Session', it means you may have an unfinished game session open on another device. You can finish the session on the original device. Note: If you have tried the above solutions and are still unable to complete your round or resolve your issue, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
