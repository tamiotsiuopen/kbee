Troubleshooting Unsettled Casino or Virtual Bets

My Casino/Virtual Bet is Still Unsettled Overview - Casino and Virtual bets are usually settled instantly. Troubleshooting Unsettled Bets - If your bet remains unsettled for more than 2 hours, proceed with the following steps. How to Find the Casino/Virtual Ticket ID 1. If you are using SportyBet on your phone browser or the app, tap on your account Balance in the top-right area. 2. Select Transactions. 3. Select the type of Transactions you wish to see more details for; in this case, choose Bets. 4. Click on the specific Bet to see the details. In the details, you will find the 6-digit Ticket ID. Note If you have tried the above solutions and your bet is still unsettled, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
