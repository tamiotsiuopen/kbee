Unable to Comment or View Comments in Game or Sports Chat

I Can't Comment on Chat or See My Comments in the Chat Section of Games or Real Sports Overview This guide explains what to do if you are unable to comment or see your comments in the chat sections of games or real sports events. How to Participate in Chat and Comments Pre-match Comments - Select the Pre-match event you are interested in. - Click on comments and join the conversation. Live Matches - Go to Live Betting. - Choose an ongoing event. - Open the chat box to join the conversation. Game Chats - Pick any game of your choice. - Access the chat box located at the top right corner of the screen. Possible Reasons for Being Unable to Comment or See Comments - If you cannot chat or comment, or cannot see your own comments, you may have been temporarily blocked from using the chat service. Chat Service Rules To maintain a positive chat environment, please follow these rules: 1. Share tips, support others, and engage positively. 2. Keep messages and links relevant; avoid spamming. 3. Treat others with respect; no hate speech or insults. 4. Do not share misleading information or sensitive information such as OTPs, financial details, or phone numbers. What to Do Next - Wait at least 5 minutes and try sending a message that follows the rules listed above. - Always be respectful of other players to help keep the chat service positive and safe. Note: If you have tried the above solutions and still cannot comment or see your comments, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
