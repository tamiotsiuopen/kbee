What to Do If Your Casino or Virtual Bet Is Incorrectly Settled

My Casino/Virtual Bet is Incorrectly Settled Overview This article provides guidance on what to do if you believe your Casino or Virtual bet has been incorrectly settled. Finding the Casino/Virtual Game Ticket ID Follow these steps to locate your 6-digit Ticket ID for a Casino or Virtual game: 1. If you are using SportyBet on your phone browser or the app, tap on your account Balance in the top-right area. 2. Select Transactions. 3. Select the type of Transactions you wish to see more details for, in this case, Bets. 4. Click on the bet to see the details. In the details, you will be able to find the 6-digit Ticket ID. Note If you have tried the above solutions and your issue is not resolved, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
