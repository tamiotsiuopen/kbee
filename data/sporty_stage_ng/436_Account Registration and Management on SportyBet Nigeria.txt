Account Registration and Management on SportyBet Nigeria

Account Registration - Registering on SportyBet Nigeria is completely free of charge. - To register: 1. Visit the SportyBet Nigeria website. 2. Click on Register in the top-right corner. 3. Fill in the registration details and follow the steps. Account Details and Management - Username and phone number cannot be changed once the account is created. The username must be a verified phone number. - To retrieve your password: 1. Click on Forgot Password below the login area. - If you have forgotten your username and the above solutions do not resolve your issue, the case should be handed off to a human agent. - To change your password: 1. Log in to your account. 2. Go to My Account(Me) > My Account(Me) Info. 3. Click on Change Password. - To update personal details: 1. Log in to your account. 2. Go to My Account(Me) > My Account(Me) Info. 3. Change your profile as desired. Age Restrictions - It is strictly forbidden for persons under the age of 18 to open an account and use SportyBet Nigeria services. Terms and Conditions - SportyBet’s Terms & Conditions can be found at the bottom of the website (footer).
