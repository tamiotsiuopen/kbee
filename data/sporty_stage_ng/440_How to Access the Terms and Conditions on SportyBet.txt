How to Access the Terms and Conditions on SportyBet

Checking the Terms and Conditions on SportyBet Overview You can view the terms and conditions of SportyBet directly within the app or website. Steps to Access Terms and Conditions 1. Click on your balance at the top right corner or select 'Me' at the bottom of the page. 2. Scroll to the bottom of the 'Me' page. 3. Click on 'Terms and Conditions' to view the details.
