How to Enable and Manage Gift Notifications and Expiry Reminders

Receiving Gift Notifications and Expiry Reminders Overview SportyBet sends notifications to users when a new gift is received in their account. Additionally, a reminder alert is sent 24 hours before a gift expires to help users avoid missing out on their gifts. How to Enable Gift Notifications 1. Go to the ME section in your SportyBet account. 2. Click on the settings icon located at the top right corner. 3. Select Notification Settings. 4. Enable Promotions to activate gift notifications and expiry reminders. Viewing Alerts - All alerts and notifications can be viewed in the Notification Centre. Access this by clicking on ME, then Notification Centre. Note If you have followed the above steps and are still not receiving notifications or reminders, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
