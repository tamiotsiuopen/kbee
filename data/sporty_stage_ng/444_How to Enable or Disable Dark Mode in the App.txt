How to Enable or Disable Dark Mode in the App

Enabling and Applying Dark Mode in the App Overview This guide explains how to enable or disable Dark Mode in the app for Android, iOS, and WAP platforms. It also describes how to match the app's appearance with your device's system theme. Android How to Enable Dark Mode 1. Tap Me. 2. Tap the Settings icon in the top right corner. 3. Under the Dark Mode section, select ON to activate Dark Mode. How to Disable Dark Mode 1. Follow the same steps as above. 2. Under the Dark Mode section, select OFF to return to Light Mode. iOS and WAP How to Enable Dark Mode 1. Tap Me. 2. Tap the Settings icon in the top right corner. 3. Click on Dark Mode in the top right corner of the screen. 4. Under the Dark Mode section, select ON to activate Dark Mode. How to Disable Dark Mode 1. Follow the same steps as above. 2. Under the Dark Mode section, select OFF to return to Light Mode. Follow System Settings - Choosing Follow System Settings will automatically match your app’s appearance with your device's system theme.
