How to Find and Use the Search Bar on SportyBet

Where to Find the Search Bar? Availability of Search Feature - The search bar is available on the Home page. - It can be accessed through the mobile website, Android app, and iOS app. How to Use the Search Bar - To search for specific markets, select a particular match. - Tap the search icon located next to the favourites section for quick access. Warning - The search feature is not available on the desktop website.
