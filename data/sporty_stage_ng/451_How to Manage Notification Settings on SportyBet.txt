How to Manage Notification Settings on SportyBet

SportyBet Notification Settings Guide Overview SportyBet provides a customizable notification system that allows users to manage alerts related to their account, payments, bets, and matches. Accessing Notification Settings 1. Go to the ME section (bottom right corner of the app). 2. Select Settings (top right corner). 3. Choose Notification Settings. Notification Options and Their Functions 1. All Notifications - Purpose: Acts as a master switch to enable or disable all notifications at once. - Function: Toggle this off to stop receiving any notifications from SportyBet. 2. Account - Purpose: Manage login-related alerts. - Function: Disable pop-up notifications when logging in from another device. 3. Payment - Purpose: Control payment-related alerts. - Function: Enable or disable notifications for deposits, withdrawals, and other payment activities. 4. Social - Purpose: Manage social interaction alerts. - Function: Toggle notifications related to social features within the app. 5. Real Bet Settlement - Purpose: Get notified when your bets are settled. - Function: Enable or disable alerts for bet outcomes, such as when a bet is won. 6. Match Alerts - Purpose: Receive updates on specific matches. - Function: Tap Match Alerts to access individual match settings. Toggle 'Receive Match Alerts' to enable or disable alerts for all matches. You can also manage alerts for specific matches individually. How to Receive Goal Alerts for a Match 1. Open the match page in the SportyBet app. 2. Click the bell icon located at the top right corner to enable goal alerts for that match. Note If you have tried the above solutions and they did not work, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
