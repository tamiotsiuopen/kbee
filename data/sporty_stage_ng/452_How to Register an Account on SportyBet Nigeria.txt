How to Register an Account on SportyBet Nigeria

How to Register on SportyBet Overview This guide explains how to create a SportyBet Nigeria account using the website or mobile app. Registration Steps 1. Visit www.sportybet.com/ng or download the SportyBet app from the Google Play Store or Apple App Store. 2. Click the Join Now button at the top-right corner of the home page. 3. Enter your mobile number and click the Next button. 4. On the next screen, enter your password and select Create Account to proceed. 5. To verify your phone number, select your preferred method from the options shown on the screen to receive your One Time Password (OTP). Note: To use the Telegram option, you must have an active Telegram account linked to your phone number. 6. Enter the 6-digit OTP you received via your selected delivery method. After Successful Registration - You have now created your SportyBet account. - Complete your first deposit to start betting. - Watch out for exciting promotions. Note If you face any challenges with receiving the OTP, please follow the troubleshooting steps provided in the relevant help section. If you have tried all solutions and still cannot register, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
