How to Update the SportyBet App

How to Update SportyBet App Overview This section provides step-by-step instructions to update the SportyBet App to the latest version. Steps to Update the SportyBet App 1. Log in to your SportyBet App. 2. Tap on 'Me' from the menu at the bottom of the screen. 3. Select the 'Update App' option. 4. Tap on 'UPDATE NOW' to download and install the latest version of the SportyBet App, which includes improvements and new features.
