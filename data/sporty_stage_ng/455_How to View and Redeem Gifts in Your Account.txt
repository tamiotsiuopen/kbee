How to View and Redeem Gifts in Your Account

Viewing Available Gifts Overview This section explains how to check which gifts are available to you in your account. Steps to View Gifts 1. Log in to your account. 2. Click on your Account's Balance located at the top-right corner of the page. 3. Select the 'Gifts' option. 4. You will see a list of all your available gifts, including both 'Valid' and 'Used/Expired' gifts. Redeeming a Gift Code - You can redeem a gift code that may have been shared with you by the SportyBet Team. Note If you have followed the steps above and still need help, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
