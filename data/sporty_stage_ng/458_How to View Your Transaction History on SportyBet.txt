How to View Your Transaction History on SportyBet

Account Statements and Transaction History Overview SportyBet does not provide account statements or any physical statements. How to View Your Transaction History 1. Log in to your SportyBet account. 2. Click on your Account's Balance in the top-right corner. 3. Click on 'Transactions.' 4. Select the desired transaction category from the drop-down column. Available categories include: - Deposits - Withdrawals - Bets - Winnings - Refund Important Note You can only view your transactions for the past 6 months.
