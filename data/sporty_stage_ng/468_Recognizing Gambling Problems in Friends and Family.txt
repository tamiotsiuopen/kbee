Recognizing Gambling Problems in Friends and Family

Friends and Family Overview SportyBet recognizes that gambling problems can negatively affect not only the player but also their friends and family. Signs of Gambling Issues Be aware of the following signs if you are concerned that a friend or family member may have gambling issues: - Uncontrolled spending - Lying about gaming behavior - Constantly borrowing money or stealing - Loss of interest in hobbies or abnormal situations - Neglecting work or studies Recommended Actions If you observe these signs or other concerning behaviors: - Have the courage to reach out to your friends and family for support. - Sharing your concerns can help you receive appropriate guidance and emotional support.
