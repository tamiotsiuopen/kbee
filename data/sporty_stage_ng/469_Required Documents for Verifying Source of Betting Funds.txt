Required Documents for Verifying Source of Betting Funds

Verifying the Source of Your Betting Funds Overview To verify the source of the funds you use for betting, you may be required to provide additional information and documentation. This process ensures that you have sufficient funds to support your gambling activities. Required Information and Documentation - You may be asked to provide details about your occupation and salary. - Supporting documentation may be required, as determined by the SportyBet team. - Employment details may be requested, but your employer will not be contacted as part of these checks. Enhanced Verification Process - You will be informed about the specific information or documentation required during the Enhanced Verification process. Note If you have tried the above solutions and still have issues, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
