Responsible Gaming Self-Assessment

Responsible Gaming Overview SportyBet encourages responsible gaming and believes that most players enjoy betting safely. However, a small number of individuals may experience harmful effects from gambling. Self-Assessment Questions Answering the following questions can help you determine if you may have a gambling problem: 1. When you lose money, do you feel lost or depressed? 2. When you lose money, do you want to gamble again to win money back as soon as possible? 3. Do you run out of all the money in your betting account? 4. Have you borrowed money from others and spent it on betting? 5. Have you lost interest in your family, friends, or hobbies because of betting? 6. Do you ever feel suicidal because of betting? Note If you have tried the above self-assessment and believe you may have a gambling problem, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
