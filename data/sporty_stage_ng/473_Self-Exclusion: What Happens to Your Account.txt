Self-Exclusion: What Happens to Your Account

My Account is on Self-Exclusion Overview Self-Exclusion is a feature that restricts your ability to place bets on SportyBet for a chosen period. This measure is designed to support responsible gaming and cannot be lifted before the selected period ends. Account Access During Self-Exclusion - You cannot place any bets while Self-Exclusion is active. - You can still log in to your account to withdraw any available funds. - Only funds that have been previously staked and are available as withdrawable balance can be withdrawn. - Funds that have not been previously staked are not withdrawable during this period. Duration and Reinstatement - The Self-Exclusion period will remain in place for the entire duration you selected. - Early removal of Self-Exclusion is not allowed. - Once the Self-Exclusion period ends, you may return and resume betting activities. Important Rules and Warnings - Creating multiple accounts to bypass Self-Exclusion is strictly prohibited. - Attempting to circumvent Self-Exclusion may result in permanent suspension and forfeiture of funds. - These policies are enforced to maintain a safe and fair gaming environment. Note If you have tried all the above solutions and still need assistance, please escalate the case to a human agent.
