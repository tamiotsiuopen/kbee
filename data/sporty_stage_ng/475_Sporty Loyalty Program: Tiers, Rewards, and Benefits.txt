Sporty Loyalty Program: Tiers, Rewards, and Benefits

Sporty Loyalty Program - Informative Summary Overview The Sporty Loyalty program is a tier-based rewards system that provides exclusive benefits to users based on their wagering activity. As users place more bets, they progress through tiers and unlock greater rewards such as free bet gifts, increased rakeback percentages, special avatars, and VIP services. Special Avatars - Special Avatars are exclusive rewards available from the Silver tier and above. - As users advance through tiers, new avatars are automatically unlocked and added to their profile. How to View Your Avatar Gallery 1. Log in to your SportyBet account. 2. Tap on "Me." 3. Select your username to open your profile. 4. Click on your Avatar image to access the gallery. Note: Special Avatars are personalized images and cannot be used for placing bets. How the Loyalty Program Works - Users are automatically assigned a tier based on their accumulated wagering (both lifetime and monthly). - Tier upgrades occur once per month when users meet the required criteria. - The Sporty Loyalty Tier Upgrade gift is credited only once per user for each specific tier. If a user downgrades and later upgrades back to the same tier, they will not receive the upgrade reward again for that tier. - Users can track their current tier, benefits, and progress toward the next level. - Rewards include free bet gifts, rakeback on bets, upgrade gifts, and VIP perks.
