SportySocial: Overview and Profile Creation

SportySocial Overview Definition SportySocial is a personalized social profile page on our platform designed for sharing your Booking Codes and connecting with others through code-based activity. It allows you to follow other users, gain followers, and view popular or followed codes in one centralized space. Sharing Your SportySocial Profile - Make your betting activity social by sharing your SportySocial page. - You can share your profile via a link or directly to social media platforms. Steps to Share Your Profile 1. Tap Share. 2. Choose to share your SportySocial profile via a link or directly to social media. Creating Your SportySocial Profile There are two ways to create your SportySocial profile: Method 1: Via Share 1. Go to Bet History or Open Bets. 2. Tap on an unsettled bet. 3. Open the ticket and tap Share. 4. Choose 'Create My SportySocial.' Method 2: Via My Profile 1. Navigate to Me. 2. Tap Profile. 3. Go to My SportySocial. 4. Tap 'Create.' Username Selection - Your chosen username becomes your permanent SportySocial URL. - Once set, your username cannot be changed. Following and Followers - Anyone with your profile link can view your page and follow you. - You can follow other users to stay updated with their shared Booking Codes. Code Hub: Explore and Engage - The Code Hub is your central location for discovering betting codes. - To access: - Tap Betslip. - Open the Code Hub tab. - Browse through: - Popular codes - Following: codes shared by users you follow - Load Code: enter and explore any Booking Code
