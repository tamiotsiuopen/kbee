Tips for Responsible Gaming

Stay in Control, It's only a Game Overview Gaming should always be an enjoyable activity. The following tips can help you stay in control and ensure that gaming does not become a problem.  1. Only place bets with amounts you can afford to lose. 2. Treat gambling as entertainment, not as a way to make money. 3. Bet sensibly and never chase your losses. 4. Keep track of the time and amount you spend on gambling. 5. Balance gambling with other activities. Consider whether you are still having fun, especially if gaming is your only entertainment. 6. Take regular breaks from gambling. You can use the self-exclusion option if needed. 7. Do not gamble when under abnormal conditions such as after consuming alcohol or when you are upset. Note If you have tried the above solutions and still have concerns about your gaming behavior, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
