How to Withdraw and Withdrawal Information on SportyBet Nigeria

Withdrawals - To withdraw money: 1. Go to My Account(Me). 2. Click on Withdraw. 3. Enter the desired amount to withdraw. 4. Click on Withdraw to confirm. - Your winnings will be immediately sent to your bank account after confirmation. - All withdrawals are free of charges. - Withdrawals are normally processed automatically but may fail due to the Daily Limit Transfer amount.
