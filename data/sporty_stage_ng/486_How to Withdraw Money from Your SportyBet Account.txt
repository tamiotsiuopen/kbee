How to Withdraw Money from Your SportyBet Account

How to Withdraw Money Overview This guide explains how to withdraw your winnings from your SportyBet account. Withdrawal Procedure 1. Log in to your SportyBet account. 2. From the homepage, click on your Account's Balance in the top-right corner or click the 'Me' tab at the bottom right. 3. On the next page, click on 'Withdraw'. 4. Select the bank you wish to withdraw to and enter your account number. 5. Enter the amount you want to withdraw. 6. Click on the 'Withdraw' button. 7. If the bank account number is being used for the first time, you will be required to confirm your identity. 8. Enter the verification code sent to the mobile phone number registered to your account. 9. Ensure that all information provided is correct. 10. If all details are correct, click the 'Withdraw' button again to confirm. 11. Your withdrawal will be processed. 12. If you wish to withdraw to a different bank account, click on the 'Switch Bank Account' button. Account Safety - Every time a new bank account is added, SportyBet will verify your identity via SMS to ensure account safety. Important Notes - For Opay and PalmPay: Withdrawals can only be processed to a wallet account that matches both the name and phone number registered on your SportyBet account. - For Other Banks: Withdrawals can only be processed to a bank account with the same name as the one registered on your SportyBet account. If you have tried all the above solutions and still cannot withdraw, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
