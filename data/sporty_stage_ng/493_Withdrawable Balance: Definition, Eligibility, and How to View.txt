Withdrawable Balance: Definition, Eligibility, and How to View

What is Withdrawable Balance? Definition Withdrawable Balance is the amount available to withdraw from your SportyBet account. Withdrawal Eligibility Criteria - To proceed with your withdrawal, your all-time withdrawal amount must be below 2 times your all-time Total Stake or Total Winnings amount. How to View Withdrawable Balance - You can view your Withdrawable Balance on your Withdrawal page.
