What to Do If Your Withdrawal Is Successful but Not Credited to Your Bank Account

My Withdrawal is Successful but I Am Not Yet Credited Overview If your withdrawal has been marked as successful but the funds have not been credited to your bank account, you should share the Session ID for the corresponding withdrawal transaction with your bank. The Session ID is generated for transfers once the transfer is successfully initiated and serves as proof of transfer. The bank should be able to trace the transfer using the Session ID and apply the amount to your bank balance. Important: Treat your Session ID confidentially and never reveal it to anyone other than your bank. How to Find the Session ID for Your Withdrawal Transaction 1. Log in to your account. 2. Click on your Account Balance at the top-right corner. 3. Click on Transactions. 4. Select Withdrawals from the drop-down column. 5. Select the withdrawal transaction for which you need the Session ID. 6. Find the Session ID under the withdrawal transaction details. What to Do After Sharing the Session ID with Your Bank - If you have provided the Session ID to your bank and still have not received the funds in your bank account, the case should be handed off to a human agent. Special Note for GTB Bank Users - If your withdrawal is successful but not credited, reach out to your bank with the Session ID and allow up to 72 hours for it to reflect in your account. - If the withdrawal is still not reflected after 72 hours, the case should be handed off to a human agent. Required Documentation for Escalation - Provide your withdrawal bank account statement in PDF format, including transaction details from 10 days prior to the withdrawal date up to the current date. Warning: Only share your Session ID with your bank for security reasons.
