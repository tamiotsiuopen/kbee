What to Do If Your Withdrawal Is Taking Too Long

Withdrawal is Taking Too Long Overview Most withdrawals are processed and reflected in your bank account within 10 minutes. However, for some banks, the processing time can take up to a maximum of 24 hours. Troubleshooting Steps - If your withdrawal has been in pending status for less than 24 hours, please wait as it may still be processing. - If your withdrawal has been in pending status for more than 24 hours, and the above solutions have been tried and did not work, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
