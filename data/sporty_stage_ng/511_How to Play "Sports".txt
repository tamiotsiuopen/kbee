How to Play "Sports"

SportyBet provides the best sports betting experience in Nigeria. Click the "Sports" tab. Filter events by time, sports, and/or league. View the "popular" events area on the Home page for recommended events such as "Today's Football", "English Premier League", and many other recommended events. View important pre-match games in the "Highlights" area. Browse ongoing live betting games or scheduled virtuals to place a bet. We not only provide sports betting for Football, but we also provide all kinds of bets for other sports. Choose the events you wish to play and select the odds by clicking on them. The chosen selections (no more than 50) will be automatically added to the betslip. The amount of the Total Stake must always be between NGN 10 and NGN 4,00,00,000. The value of every single stake should be no less than NGN 10. Click on "Place Bet" and "Confirm" the bet to place it. A message will be displayed to inform whether a bet has been successfully placed or not.
