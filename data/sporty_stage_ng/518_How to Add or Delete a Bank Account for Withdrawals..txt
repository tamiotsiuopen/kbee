How to Add or Delete a Bank Account for Withdrawals.

Bank Account Management for Withdrawals Adding a Bank Account via Website 1. Go to "My Account" and click "Withdraw". 2. Select “Withdraw to a new bank account.” 3. Select your bank, enter your account number and the withdrawal amount. Click "Withdraw". If this is your first time using this account, enter the verification code sent to your registered mobile number. 4. Ensure all details are correct then click "Withdraw". 5. Your withdrawal will be processed. Deleting a Bank Account via Website 1. Go to "My Account" and click "Withdraw". 2. Hover over the bank account you wish to delete. 3. Click "Delete" when the option appears. Adding a Bank Account via Mobile Website or SportyBet Application 1. Click on the option "ME" and click "Withdraw". 2. Click "Manage Accounts", then click on "Add a New Account". 3. Select your bank, enter your account number and the withdrawal amount. Click "Withdraw". If this is your first time using this account, enter the verification code sent to your registered mobile number. 4. Ensure all details are correct then click "Withdraw". 5. Your withdrawal will be processed. Deleting a Bank Account via Mobile Website or SportyBet Application 1. Click on the option "ME" and click "Withdraw". 2. Click "Manage Accounts", click on the three dots next to the bank account you wish to delete. 3. Click "Delete". NOTE: For the first 24 hours after adding a new bank account, withdrawals are limited to NGN 50,000 or less. After 24 hours, you can withdraw as per your withdrawal limit. WARNING: All withdrawals should be processed to the payment account from which deposits were made. Withdrawals can only be made to accounts in your name.
