Manage Withdrawal Accounts.

The Manage Accounts feature lets users view, reorder, add, or remove bank accounts used for withdrawals. NOTE: You won’t be able to access the "Manage Accounts" option until you add at least one withdrawal bank account to your SportyBet account. 1. Reorder & Customize Accounts Users can rearrange their bank accounts using drag-and-drop. Find the drag handle (three horizontal lines) on the right side of an account Long-press and drag the account to your preferred position Set Default by Dragging Drag an account to the top of the list A confirmation message appears: “[Bank Name] is now your default account”. Tap OK to confirm. 2. Manage Individual Accounts Tap the three-dot menu (…) next to an account Choose one of the following options: Set as Default – Pins the account to the top of the list and the main withdrawal screen. Delete – Removes the bank account from your profile. 3. Add a New Bank Account At the bottom of the Manage Accounts page, tap Add a New Account Select your bank from the Select a Bank dropdown Enter the Account Number Enter the withdrawal amount and tap Withdraw Review details on the Confirm to Withdraw pop-up and tap Confirm If this is your first time using this account, enter the verification code sent to your registered mobile number. NOTE: For the first 24 hours after adding a new bank account, withdrawals are limited to NGN 50,000 or less. After 24 hours, you can withdraw as per your withdrawable balance. WARNING: Withdrawals can only be made to a bank account registered under the same name as your SportyBet account.
