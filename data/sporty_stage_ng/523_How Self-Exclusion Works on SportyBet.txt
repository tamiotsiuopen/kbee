How Self-Exclusion Works on SportyBet

Self Exclusion Overview SportyBet's Self-Exclusion option allows players to close their accounts for a specified period of time. During this period, players cannot bet or play games, but they can still log in to withdraw funds, subject to certain conditions. Account Access During Self Exclusion - Players cannot place bets or play games. - Players can log in to their accounts to withdraw funds. - Accounts cannot be re-enabled until the self-exclusion period ends. Withdrawals During Self Exclusion - Players can withdraw any remaining balance in their SportyBet wallet. - As per SportyBet's risk management procedures, balances that have not been previously staked are not withdrawable. Open Bets During Self Exclusion - Open bets will settle as they normally do. - Users who have opted for self-exclusion will not be able to view open bets during the exclusion period. Available Gifts during Self Exclusion - All gifts come with an expiry date. If your account is on self-exclusion and the expiry date is reached during that period, the gift may expire while you are on self-exclusion and cannot be used later after self-exclusion period ends. Note If the above solutions have been tried and did not work, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
