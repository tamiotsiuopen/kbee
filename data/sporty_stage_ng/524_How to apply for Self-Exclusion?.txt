How to apply for Self-Exclusion?

To apply for Self-Exclusion, please follow the steps below Step 1: Log in to your account. Step 2: Scroll to the bottom of the page and click on About Us. Step 3: Click on Responsible Gaming Step 4: Click on Self-Exclusion Step 5: Click on Set up Self-Exclusion Step 6: Select the Self-Exclusion period from the drop-down menu. Users may choose to self-exclude their accounts for a minimum of 24 hours, 48 hours, 7 days, 30 days, or up to a maximum of 90 days. Please note that accounts cannot be re-enabled until the time period is concluded. Step 7: Check the end time as you selected in Step 6. Click on Self-Exclude. Please note: The chosen self-exclusion duration will be displayed each time the user logs into their SportyBet account, serving as a reminder of the active restriction. You will now be immediately logged out of your account and successfully self-excluded Please note that accounts under self-exclusion cannot be re-enabled until the time period is concluded. Also, users who have opted for self-exclusion are prohibited from creating new SportyBet accounts during the self-exclusion period. Please also note that if the available gifts reaches for an expiry date during the self-exclusion period, the gift will expire and cannot be used later after self-exclusion period ends.
