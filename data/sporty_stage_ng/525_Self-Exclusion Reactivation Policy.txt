Self-Exclusion Reactivation Policy

Unable to Reactivate Account Self-Exclusion Reactivation If you are trying to reactivate your account after self-exclusion, self-exclusion cannot be lifted until the selected exclusion period has ended. Please also note that all gifts come with an expiry date. If your account is on self-exclusion and the expiry date is reached during that period, the gift may expire while you are on self-exclusion and cannot be used later after self-exclusion period ends.
