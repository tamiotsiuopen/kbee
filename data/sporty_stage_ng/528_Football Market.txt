Football Market

1X2 – Red (R): Predicts the winner at regular time; settlement is critical. Double Chance – Red (R): Combines two possible outcomes; fundamental for settlement. Draw No Bet – Red (R): Predicts winner with draw voided; ignoring draw rules can void bets. Home No Bet – Red (R): Predicts winner with home win voided; settlement depends on result. Away No Bet – Red (R): Predicts winner with away win voided; settlement depends on result. Handicap – Red (R): Full-time score plus handicap affects settlement; critical for bettors. Asian Handicap – Red (R): Settlement may split stake or be half won/lost; precise rules critical. Over/Under – Red (R): Predicts total goals; quarter/half/whole lines affect winning. Home Team Over/Under – Red (R): Team-specific total goals over/under; critical for settlement. Away Team Over/Under – Red (R): Team-specific total goals over/under; critical for settlement. GG/NG – Red (R): Predicts whether both teams will score; fundamental for settlement. To Qualify – Red (R): Determines which team advances; settlement depends on tournament rules. Exact Goals – Red (R): Predicts precise number of goals; miscalculation can void bet. Will There Be Overtime – Red (R): Predicts if match goes beyond regular time; critical for settlement. Goal Range – Red (R): Predicts range of goals; precise outcome affects bet. Odd/Even – Red (R): Total goals determine odd/even result; zero goals count as even. Winning Margin – Red (R): Predicts exact goal margin; miscalculation can void bet. Teams to Score – Red (R): Predicts which team scores; critical for combined markets. 1X2 & GG/NG – Red (R): Combines match winner and both teams scoring; critical settlement rules. Over/Under & GG/NG – Red (R): Combines goals over/under and both teams scoring; critical. 1X2 & Over/Under – Red (R): Combines match winner and goal total; fundamental market. 1st/2nd Half GG/NG – Red (R): Predicts both teams scoring in half; settlement based on half-time result.
