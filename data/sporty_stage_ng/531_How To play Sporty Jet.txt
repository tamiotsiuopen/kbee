How To play Sporty Jet

About Sporty Jet Sporty Jet is a crash game where a jet takes flight at the beginning of each round. A coefficient (multiplier) increases continuously as the jet flies. Players must cash out before the jet flies away to win. If you do not cash out in time, your bet is lost. How to Play Sporty Jet Step 1: Place a Bet Choose your bet amount before the round starts. Press the BET button to join the round. Step 2: Watch the Coefficient Increase Once the round begins, the jet starts flying. The coefficient starts at 1.00x and increases continuously (e.g., 2.10x, 10.50x, 22.65x). The longer the jet flies, the higher the potential payout. Step 3: Cash Out to Win Press CASH OUT at any time before the jet flies away. Your winnings are locked at the coefficient shown when you cashed out. If the jet flies away before you cash out, your bet is lost. Automatic Cashout Option - You can set a target multiplier for auto-cashout in the game settings. The game will automatically cash out for you when your target multiplier is reached. Consider using the auto-cashout feature to avoid missing your preferred multiplier. Example: If the Sporty Jet crashes at 1.5x and you set auto-cashout at 1.5x, you will lose the bet. Set your cash-out at 1.49x or lower to avoid this. Coefficients Explained Coefficients represent the multiplier applied to your bet. All coefficients start at 1x and increase over time. Higher coefficients offer higher rewards but carry higher risk. Winnings Formula Your Winnings = Your Bet × Cashout Coefficient Example: Bet Amount: 200 Cashout Coefficient: 22.65x Winnings: 200 × 22.65 = 4530 Betting & Cashout Rules How to Place a Bet Select the bet amount. Press BET before the round starts. How to Cash Out Press CASH OUT while the jet is still flying. Your winnings are instantly credited. Losing a Bet If you do not cash out before the jet flies away, your bet is fully lost.
