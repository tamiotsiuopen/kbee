How To play Sporty Jet

. Click the “Claim” button to receive the Free Bet. Free Bets must be used in Sporty Jet before they expire. Expired Free Bets cannot be used. One-Tap Bet Feature When One-Tap Bet is enabled: Bet confirmations are skipped. Bets are placed instantly with a single tap. This feature helps place bets faster but should be used carefully. Free Bet Gifts Accessing Free Bet Gifts Click the Gift icon on the game screen to view available Free Bet Gifts. If no Free Bet Gifts are available, the Gift icon will not appear. Using Free Bet Gifts You may use: The full gift amount, or A partial gift amount for your bet. Free Bet Gifts can only be used in Sporty Jet. Important Rules You cannot combine Free Bet Gifts with real money in a single bet. Only one option can be used at a time: Either Real Money, or Free Bet Gift
