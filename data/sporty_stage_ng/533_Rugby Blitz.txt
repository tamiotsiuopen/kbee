What Is ?  is a crash game, meaning the multiplier increases in real time from 1.00x upward, and players must decide when to cash out. The longer you stay in the round, the higher the multiplier climbs — but if the game “crashes” before you cash out, you lose that bet. Think of it like a rugby run: You keep running for more points, but one tackle ends everything. How to Play  (Step‑by‑Step) 1. Place Your Bet Before the round starts, choose how much you want to stake. You can: Enter the amount manually Use quick‑bet buttons Set auto‑bet options Once the round begins, your bet is locked in. 2. Watch the Multiplier Rise The game starts at 1.00x and begins climbing: 1.10x 1.50x 2.00x 5.00x 10.00x …and sometimes much higher But at any moment, the game can crash. 3. Cash Out Before the Crash Your goal is to hit Cash Out before the multiplier stops. If you cash out at: 2.00x → you win double your bet 5.00x → you win 5 times your bet 10.00x → you win 10 times your bet If the crash happens first, your bet is lost. Why It’s Called  The theme is inspired by rugby: The multiplier represents your player running down the field The crash represents getting tackled Cashing out is like passing the ball before getting hit It’s simple, fast, and adrenaline‑filled — just like a real rugby blitz play. Winnings Calculation Your winnings are calculated using a simple formula: Bet Amount × Target Coefficient The higher the target you choose, the higher the potential payout — but with increased risk. House Coefficient & Fair Play In every round, the house coefficient is generated randomly by an algorithm. This ensures that each  outcome is unpredictable and fair. The coefficient continues to increase until it reaches its final value for that round. Winning Chance The Winning Chance shown in the game is an estimated value.
