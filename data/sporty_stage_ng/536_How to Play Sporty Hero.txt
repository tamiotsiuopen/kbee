Common Reasons for Losing Bets Timing Failures (Not Cashing Out on Time) - If you wait too long to cash out and the Sporty Hero crashes before you do, you lose your bet. - Example: If you aim for a 2x multiplier but the Sporty Hero crashes at 1.7x, you lose your bet. Same Cash-out Odds or Coefficient - The multiplier at which the Sporty Hero crashes is called the coefficient (e.g., 1.5x, 2x, 10x). - If you set your cash-out odds exactly equal to the crash multiplier, your bet is considered lost according to game rules. Your cash-out odds should always be less than the crash multiplier. - Example: If the Sporty Hero crashes at 1.5x and you set auto-cashout at 1.5x, you will lose the bet. Set your cash-out at 1.49x or lower to avoid this.
