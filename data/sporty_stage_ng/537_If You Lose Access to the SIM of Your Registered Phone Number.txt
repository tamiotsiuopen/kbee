If You Lose Access to the SIM of Your Registered Phone Number

If you have lost your SIM card, please contact your mobile network provider and request a new SIM with the same number as it cannot be changed to a different number. If you are unable to retrieve your SIM then please create a new SportyBet account with another number and contact us for the deactivation of your existing account with any two of the details of your Last deposit amount and date, Last withdrawal amount and date, Last stake amount and date, First and last four digits of the card used for deposits, Current SportyBet wallet balance
