Can I 'DELETE' my SportyBet account?

Please note that a number once registered to open a SportyBet account cannot be deleted. However, you may deactivate your SportyBet account: 1. Deactivation While Logged In To deactivate your SportyBet account while logged in: - Go to My Account - Navigate to Safety & Security - Click on Deactivate - Enter your registered mobile number - Select 'Deactivate' - Choose 'Other' as the reason - Complete the account ownership verification ✅ Result: Your account will be temporarily deactivated 2. Deactivation While Logged Out To deactivate your SportyBet account while logged out: - Navigate to the login page and click on Deactivate or Reactivate Your Account - Enter your registered mobile number - Select 'Deactivate' - Choose 'Other' as the reason - Complete the account ownership verification ✅ Result: Your account will be temporarily deactivated 3. if you still want to permanently restrict your account, you are requested to confirm the below details for verification purposes - 1) Last Deposit Amount and Date 2) Last Withdrawal Amount and Date 3) Last Stake Amount and Date 4) The first and last 4 digits of the card used for deposit 5) Current SportyBet wallet balance
