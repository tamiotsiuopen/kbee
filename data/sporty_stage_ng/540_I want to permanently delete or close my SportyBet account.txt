I want to permanently delete / close my SportyBet account

Please note that a number once registered to open a SportyBet account cannot be deleted. However, you may deactivate your SportyBet account using the self deactivation function under My Account > Safety & Security OR You can opt for the self exclusion under Responsible Gaming section. If you still want us to deactivate your account from our end, you are requested to confirm the below details for verification purposes - 1) Last Deposit Amount and Date 2) Last Withdrawal Amount and Date 3) Last Stake Amount and Date 4) The first and last 4 digits of the card used for deposit 5) Current SportyBet wallet balance
