How to Resolve Withdrawal Failure Due to Name Mismatch

Withdrawal Failed Due to Name Not Matching Overview If you are unable to withdraw due to a name mismatch, you will receive an error message. This occurs because the name on your withdrawal bank account does not match the name on your SportyBet account. How to Update Your Name for Withdrawal 1. Attempt the withdrawal again. When the error message appears, click on Customer service displayed below the error message. 2. You will be redirected to a verification page. Click on Verify. 3. Enter your NIN details in the Enter NIN field to update your name correctly. - If you do not have an NIN or encounter an error entering your NIN, click on the link I don't have an NIN and complete the requested details. 4. Fill in the online form and upload the required information: - Full Name (First, Middle, Last) - ID Information - First Deposit Information - Ensure all documents are below 5MB in size. 5. Click Submit. The Submit button will only be active after all three details are updated. 6. To further verify your account, select your preferred OTP delivery method from the options on the screen. For the Telegram option, you must have an active Telegram account linked to your phone number. 7. Enter the 6-digit OTP code received via your chosen method. 8. Wait up to 48 hours for document verification. What to Do If Verification Is Rejected - If your submission is rejected, check the reason by clicking on the Rejected button. - Click on Verify again and upload the correct details as requested. - Once the details are verified, your name will be updated. If You Do Not Receive Feedback Note: If you have tried the above solutions and have not received any feedback after 48 hours, the case should be handed off to a human agent. Important Note If a withdrawal has been processed successfully from your SportyBet account in the past, the name on the account cannot be changed.
