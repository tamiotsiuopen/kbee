What is Boost Gift?

1. What is the Boost Gift? ✅ Green Answer: The Boost Gift lets you temporarily increase your Loyalty Wager or Rakeback percentage for a set number of days. Once activated, you can track your Boost Status and remaining time on the Loyalty page. Benefit: Helps you earn rewards faster while you play. 2. How do Wager Boost Gifts work? ✅ Green Answer: Increases your Loyalty Monthly or Lifetime Wager. Speeds up your loyalty tier progression. Important: Does not affect weekly rewards—they stay the same. Tip: Use this to climb loyalty tiers faster without changing weekly earnings. 3. How do Rakeback Boost Gifts work? 🟢 Green/Amber Answer: Temporarily increases the percentage of weekly rakeback you earn. Lets you earn more rewards for the same betting activity during the boost. Caution: This only lasts for the boost period; once it ends, your rakeback returns to normal. 4. Can I use both Wager and Rakeback Boost Gifts together? ✅ Green Answer: Yes, the two boosts are independent. You can activate both at the same time. Each boost will work according to its own rules. Track each boost’s duration separately on the Loyalty page. 5. How long does a Boost Gift last? 🟠 Amber Answer: Duration depends on the specific Boost Gift. After activation, check your Loyalty page to see the remaining boost time. Tip: Always monitor your remaining boost to maximize benefits. 6. Does the Boost Gift affect my weekly rewards? 🟢 Green/Amber Answer: Wager Boost Gift: No change to weekly rewards. Rakeback Boost Gift: Increases your weekly rakeback percentage for the boost period. Caution: Only Rakeback Boost affects weekly earnings. 7. Where can I see my Boost status? ✅ Green Answer: After activating a Boost Gift, check the Loyalty page. Your Boost Status and Remaining Boost Time are displayed. Helps you track both duration and benefits.
