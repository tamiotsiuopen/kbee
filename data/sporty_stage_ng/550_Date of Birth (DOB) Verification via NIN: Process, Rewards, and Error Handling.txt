SportyBet Date of Birth (DOB) Verification via NIN – Error Handling & Next Steps Overview This guide explains how users can verify their Date of Birth (DOB) on SportyBet using their National Identification Number (NIN), the rewards available for successful verification, and how to handle common errors and issues. Verification Process Users can verify their DOB and NIN via: - Profile Page - Sporty Loyalty Page Rewards Upon Successful Verification - Immediate gift is credited to the Gift section if DOB is verified via NIN. - Birthday gift is automatically credited on the user's birth date (only if DOB is verified via NIN). - Users with a Sporty Loyalty tier of Copper or higher receive a special loyalty gift. Important Notes - The Birthday Gift Program is active only for users who verify DOB via NIN. - Gifts appear in the Gift section of the user’s account. Error Handling Temporary Verification Service Issues - Error Message: The Verification Service is currently unavailable. Please try again later. - Action: Wait 24 hours from your last attempt before retrying. Too Many Attempts Error - If you see a message about too many attempts, you have exceeded the allowed verification tries. - To resolve, prepare the following documents: - A clear copy of your NIN card showing: - Date of Birth (DOB) - NIN number - Full Name - Screenshot of the error message received - Another government-issued ID showing: - DOB - Full Name - Your exact registered Date of Birth - Note: If the above solutions have been tried and did not work, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
