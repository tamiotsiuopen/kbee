Introducing Daily Streaks on SportyBet!

🏆  Want to make your bets even more exciting? Now you can win more rewards just by betting every day! With Daily Streaks, the more consecutive days you bet, the higher your Loyalty Reward Multiplier becomes. That means your weekly rewards get boosted! 🔥 How It Works Place at least one bet every day. Build your streak – the longer your streak, the bigger your bonus! Don’t miss a day! If you skip a day, your streak resets. But don’t worry — you can fix missed days using Streak Repair Tools. 🛠️ Streak Repair Tools Missed a day? Use your Streak Repair Tool to fix it and keep your streak alive! You’ll earn repair tools as you progress or through Sporty loyalty missions. 📅 Track Your Progress Check your Streak Calendar in the app to see: Which days you’ve bet Your current multiplier How close you are to the next level 📱 Stay on Track We’ll remind you 3 hours before the day ends if you haven’t placed a bet yet — so you never miss your streak! 💥 Show Off Your Streak Built an amazing streak? Share it with your friends on social media straight from the app using the Show Off button! 📍 Where to Find Daily Streaks on SportyBet To find the Daily Streak page on SportyBet, follow these simple steps: Open the SportyBet app or WAP. On the homepage, click ME at the bottom-right of the page. You’ll now see the Daily Streak section. Click on it to check what streak you’re currently on. Ready to Start? Just place a bet today and begin your streak. Keep the flame alive and earn more every week! 🔥
