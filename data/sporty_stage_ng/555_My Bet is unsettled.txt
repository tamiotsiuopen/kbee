My Bet is unsettled

Bets may remain unsettled in cases where SportyBet is waiting for the official results of the match. For example - If your selection belongs to an Italian-Serie D match (Football), it may take up to 5 working days for the settlement of your bet. If your bet still remains unsettled after 12 hours of the match completion time, please contact customer care by clicking on the Need more Help button below, as they will be able to investigate the issue and provide you with an update on the status of your bet.
