What to Do If Your Withdrawal is Marked Successful but Not Credited

My Withdrawal is Successful but Not Credited Overview This guide provides steps to follow if your withdrawal is marked as successful but the funds have not yet been credited to your bank account. Steps to Resolve Missing Withdrawal Credit 1. Obtain the Session ID for the withdrawal transaction. This Session ID serves as proof of transfer and can be used by your bank to trace and apply the funds to your account. Keep the Session ID confidential and only share it with your bank. How to Find the Session ID 1. Log in to your account. 2. Click on your Account Balance located at the top-right corner. 3. Click on 'Transactions.' 4. Select 'Withdrawals' from the drop-down column. 5. Choose the relevant withdrawal transaction. 6. Locate the Session ID under the withdrawal transaction details. Next Steps - Share the Session ID with your bank and request them to trace the transfer and credit the amount to your account. Special Note for GTB Bank Users - If your withdrawal is successful but not credited, provide the Session ID to your bank and allow up to 72 hours for the funds to reflect in your account. - If the funds are still not credited after 72 hours, proceed to the next section. Escalation If you have contacted your bank with the Session ID and still have not received the funds in your bank account, the case should be handed off to a human agent. Prepare your withdrawal bank account statement in PDF format, including transaction details from 10 days prior to the withdrawal date up to the current date. Warning Do not share your Session ID with anyone except your bank. Treat this information as confidential.
