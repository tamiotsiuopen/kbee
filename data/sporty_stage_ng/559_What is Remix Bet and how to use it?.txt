What is Remix Bet and how to use it?

Remix Bet: Explore New Betting Possibilities 🔄 Remix Bet is a feature that allows you to generate new bet combinations from a ticket that has already been settled (won or lost). Remix Bet analyzes your previous selections and suggests similar bets automatically. This means you can explore new possibilities without manually rebuilding your ticket. Who Can Use Remix Bet Remix Bet is available only for settled bets. If your ticket qualifies: If Rebet is possible, the Rebet button will appear. If Rebet is not available, the Remix Bet button will show instead. How to Access Remix Bet You can access this feature in two ways: From your Bet History, for tickets placed within the last six months. From the Ticket Details page. Once you open a settled ticket, simply tap the Remix Bet button to explore new combinations. What You Can Do with Remix Bet The feature automatically generates 4 new bet combinations based on your previous ticket. You can: Browse the suggested options. Add any combination you like to your betslip. Exit anytime—your remix selections are not saved unless you place the bet. Tips for Using Remix Bet A one-time hint will guide you when you first access the feature. A short tutorial explains how Remix Bet works. The system shows 4 suggested combinations at a time. Tap “Try New Picks” to see the next set. Suggestions are initially ranked by popularity and will later become personalized to your betting style. Error Handling If the system cannot generate suggestions, you’ll see: “Sorry, we couldn’t generate picks right now. Please try again later.” Adding bets to your betslip follows standard error-handling procedures. Important Notes Remix Bet is only available on the latest app versions. Older versions will not support this feature. How Remix Bet Works Using Remix Bet is simple and hassle-free: Open a settled ticket from your betting history. Tap the Remix Bet option.
