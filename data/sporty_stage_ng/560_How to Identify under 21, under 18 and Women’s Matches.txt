How to Identify under 21, under 18 and Women’s Matches

 SportyBet always displays under-21, under-18, and women’s football matches, clearly labeled at the end of the team's name to help users quickly identify the type of game and match. Look for the labels at the end of team names: “U21” – Indicates the team is under 21, e.g., Real Madrid U21. “U18” – Indicates the team is under 18, e.g., Barcelona U18. “W” – Indicates a women’s team, e.g., Arsenal W. These labels make it easy to distinguish between under 21, under 18 women, and other matches, ensuring users know exactly what to expect in terms of players and competition level. Checking the team name for these tags is the quickest way to identify the match type, whether you’re following emerging talent or women’s football.
