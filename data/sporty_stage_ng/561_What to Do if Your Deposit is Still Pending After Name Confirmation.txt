What to Do if Your Deposit is Still Pending After Name Confirmation

Yes, but the deposit is still pending Overview This section provides guidance if your deposit remains pending even after confirming your name with SportyBet. Steps to Follow 1. Check your bank account to confirm whether you have been debited for the deposit amount. 2. If you have been debited, please wait. Deposits are usually processed immediately, but in some cases, it may take up to 60 minutes for the transaction to be completed due to network or third-party provider delays. Note If your deposit does not reflect in your SportyBet wallet within 60 minutes after being debited, and you have tried the above solutions, the case should be handed off to a human agent.
